[印度奥委会称尊重人民感情 解除李宁赞助合约](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210610-1154244)
------

<div>
<p>印度奥委会以国内民众感受为由，据报决定取消由中国运动服制造商李宁作为官方装备赞助商，印度选手将在东京奥运会上穿着无品牌的服装。</p><p>自中印两国去年在喜马拉雅山脉边境爆发冲突，导致20名印度士兵丧生后，中国企业在印度遭遇抵制声浪。</p><p>据路透社报道，印度奥委会当时决定重新检视与李宁的合作关系，双方合作关系将在东京奥运会后到期。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>印度奥委会六天前发表奥运队服，但前天表示，基于尊重“全国人民的情感”，奥委会已经中止了与李宁的合作。</p><p>中国外交部发言人汪文彬昨天称，希望印度能客观公正地看待两国之间的正常合作，“避免政治化”。</p><p>印度奥委会说，不希望队服的制造商问题成为一种干扰，目前正赶紧寻找新的赞助商。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
