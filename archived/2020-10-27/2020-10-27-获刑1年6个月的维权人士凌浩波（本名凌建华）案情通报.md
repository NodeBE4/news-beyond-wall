font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">凌浩波：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本名凌建华（推特帐号<span lang="EN-US">@52ling</span>）<span lang="EN-US">1986</span>年生于湖北省随州市，近年在广州工作生活。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日凌浩波在广东华登（河源市）玩具制品有限公司被警方带走，原因和去向不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此前，凌浩波因为关注邓传彬被刑事拘留一案并在推特发布邓案的相关消息，于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日被广州白云区公安分局传唤，随后凌浩波回到老家湖北随州。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日凌浩波返回广州后被白云区嘉禾房东告知：已应警察要求注销其门卡，强迫其搬迁；凌浩波<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日到河源工厂上班，<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被警方带走后失联。后凌浩波于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日被刑事拘留，于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日由广东河源市检察院批准逮捕，罪名为“寻衅滋事”。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，凌浩波已被秘密审判，刑期未知，被从河源市看守所转入河源监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
