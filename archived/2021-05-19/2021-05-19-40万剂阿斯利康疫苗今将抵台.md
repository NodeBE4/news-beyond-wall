[40万剂阿斯利康疫苗今将抵台](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210519-1147919)
------

<div>
<p>40万剂阿斯利康疫苗今天（19日）将抵达台湾，运输飞机由长荣航空执飞。</p><p>据联合报报道，记者了解到，运送阿斯利康疫苗的飞机已从荷兰阿姆斯特丹起飞，预计傍晚6时抵达桃园国际机场。班次为BR78、航班编号B-17803，机型为波音787-10。</p><p>随着疫情升温，台湾民众施打疫苗的需求随之增加。台湾行政院发言人罗秉成透露，台湾疫情指挥中心指挥官、卫福部长陈时中今上午10点半向行政院长苏贞昌报告，在疫苗采购的部分，约有40万剂的阿斯利康疫苗已在今日凌晨3点从阿姆斯特丹起飞，预计下午4时会抵达台湾，对台湾来讲是及时雨。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
