[美国曾阻优衣库衬衫进口 担心涉新疆劳工](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210519-1147923)
------

<div>
<p>美国海关今年1月阻止了一批迅销旗下优衣库衬衫的进口，理由是这批产品违反了一项相关指令，该指令禁止进口涉嫌由中国新疆生产建设兵团强迫劳工生产的产品。</p><p>据彭博社报道，美国海关与边境保护局在一份日期为5月10日的文件中公布了这起发生在洛杉矶港的阻止优衣库棉质男士衬衫进口的事。文件中指出，优衣库恳请放行这批衬衫，但遭到了海关的拒绝。优衣库是由日本顶级富豪柳井正创立的亚洲最大服装零售商迅销旗下的主要品牌。</p><p>美国海关文件中说，优衣库曾进行抗辩，并提供了这批衬衫所用棉花并非来自新疆生产建设兵团的证据。但是优衣库未能提供充足信息证明这批产品与新疆强迫劳工没有关联。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
