[华为人事调整 余承东任智能汽车解决方案BU CEO](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210519-1147864)
------

<div>
<p>中国电信巨头华为昨天进行新一轮的人事调整。身兼多职的余承东被免去华为云CEO职位，张平安被任命为华为云CEO；此外，余承东新出任华为智能汽车解决方案BU CEO，王军继续担任智能汽车解决方案BU总裁。</p><p>根据澎湃新闻报道，这一轮人事调整后，余承东淡出华为云业务。华为云业务董事长由华为轮值董事长徐直军担任。</p><p>上月9日，徐直军被任命华为云董事长，余承东被任命为华为云CEO。当时外界调侃这一任命为华为“梦之队”，外界也有猜测华为云业务接下来可能会独立上市。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>一位华为内部人士透露，余承东之前在华为云业务的使命是厘清华为云的架构，现在云业务主要交给张平安负责了，“云业务短期内应该不会调整了。”</p><p>不过，余承东淡出华为云业务依然担任华为消费者业务CEO职位，并且新增了华为智能汽车解决方案BU CEO一职。从这一调整看，华为把智能汽车业务交给余承东负责，由余承东和王军两人搭档。这可能将推进华为消费者业务和智能汽车业务进一步整合和协同。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
