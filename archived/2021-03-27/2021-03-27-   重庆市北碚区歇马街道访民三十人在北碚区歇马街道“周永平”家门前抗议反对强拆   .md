[重庆市北碚区歇马街道访民三十人在北碚区歇马街道“周永平”家门前抗议反对强拆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/blog-post_55.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日重庆市北碚区歇马街道访民何朝正、刘高胜、李忠秀、周永平、张兴芳、叶昌蓉等三十人在北碚区歇马街道周永平家门前抗议反对强拆。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">他们都因上访被强加罪名关黑监狱、判刑、拘留等。他们要求重庆市北碚区歇马街道落实国家信访政策，依法解决访民合理合法的诉求，保障被征地农民原有生活水平不降低，长远生计有保障。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日地方当局暴力强拆刘高胜的父母（刘代刚、史君）<span lang="EN-US">297</span>平米房屋，拆房时把史君打伤住院<span lang="EN-US">22</span>天，至今未赔偿。近十年多次上访北京，多次被打击报复关押判刑、刘高胜被判刑三年二个月（<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日刑满释放）拆迁诉求至今不依法赔偿解决。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因遭强征土地 老父亲被打后死亡、李忠秀表示：“<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日政府采取强征，来了一千多名警察和保安，因为补偿过低，农民在现场抗议，被抓了五十多人，戴上手铐带走的。被打伤的群众有三十人，包括我父母，他们都是七八十岁的人了，也被打伤住进医院。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
