Editorial judgment and a deep understanding of digital journalism are essential, as is an ability to work effectively across multiple teams. Applicants should propose one idea for a multi-media story to run in 2021 that they believe is suitable for <em>The Economist</em>, and send their proposal and a CV to <a href="https://www.economist.com/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#0272706d6867617671686d604267616d6c6d6f6b71762c616d6f"><span  data-cfemail="c5b5b7aaafa0a6b1b6afaaa785a0a6aaabaaa8acb6b1eba6aaa8">[email&#160;protected]</span></a> by March 31st 2021.</p><p >The Economist Group values diversity. We are committed to equal opportunities and creating an inclusive environment for all our employees. We welcome applicants regardless of ethnic origin, national origin, gender, race, colour, religious beliefs, disability, sexual orientation or age.</p><div id="" ><div><div id="econ-1"></div></div></div><p ><strong>Mission: </strong><br/>To manage and deliver high-quality, digital-first editorial projects that integrate, advance and amplify all elements of our journalism, from our core written articles to pictures, data interactives, audio, video, webinars and beyond.</p><p ><strong>Areas of responsibility include:</strong><br/>• Planning digital coverage of big news events and internal packages<br/>• Project-managing big digital projects to publication and beyond<br/>• Driving innovation in how we conceive, conduct and present our digital journalism <br/>• Developing internal playbooks for specific news events such as elections <br/>• Maintaining and populating a calendar of special projects<br/>• Liaising with product, audience and other colleagues on specific projects <br/>• Liaising with the rest of the digital team and the wider editorial team on big digital packages</p><p ><strong>Objectives:</strong><br/>• Creating a calendar of ambitious digital projects that can be delivered at a regular cadence throughout the year <br/>• Ensuring that the activities of our various editorial teams on digital projects are properly co-ordinated and aligned, and tailored to work across multiple digital platforms<br/>• Ensuring that editorial collaborates effectively on these projects with non-editorial teams such as product, engineering and marketing<br/>• Ensuring that we capture the benefits and lessons of digital-first projects so that features and processes can be replicated<br/>• Developing ideas that take advantage of, and spur, improvements to our editorial, product and technical capabilities</p><p ><strong>Key relationships include:</strong><br/>• Digital editor<br/>• Head of audience<br/>• Editorial department heads<br/>• Head of content products</p>
