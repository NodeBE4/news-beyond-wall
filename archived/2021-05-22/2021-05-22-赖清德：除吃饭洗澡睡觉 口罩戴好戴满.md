[赖清德：除吃饭洗澡睡觉 口罩戴好戴满](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210521-1148464)
------

<div>
<p>台湾疫情严峻，警戒维持在第三级，台湾副总统赖清德今天在面簿发文表示，请提醒家人朋友，除了吃饭、洗澡、睡觉，一定要把口罩戴好戴满，以保平安！</p><p>据中时电子报报道，赖清德还说，防疫警戒第三级，出门务必戴口罩；单独一人驾驶可以不用戴口罩（开窗则需戴）；两人以上都要戴上口罩。</p><p>赖清德呼吁，依据传染病防治法规定，针对未戴口罩者经劝导不听者，可处3000元以上（新台币，143新元）、1万5000元以下罚锾。落实防疫，人人有责。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>台湾今天新增312例确诊病例。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
