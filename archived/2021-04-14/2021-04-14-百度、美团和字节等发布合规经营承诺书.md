[百度、美团和字节等发布合规经营承诺书](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210414-1139327)
------

<div>
<p>中国市场监管总局明确要求互联网平台企业在一个月内全面自检自查，彻底整改“二选一”等问题后，百度、京东、美团、360、字节跳动等头部平台发布承诺书，表示将构建公平竞争的市场环境，推动平台经济健康发展，绝不实施“二选一”。</p><p>据北京日报客户端报道，从今日起连续三天，中国市场监管总局将集中公布34家互联网平台企业《依法合规经营承诺》，请社会各界予以监督。这34家互联网平台企业昨日参加了市监局关于整改“二选一”等问题的会议。</p><p>目前，百度、京东、美团、奇虎360、口袋时尚（微店）、新浪微博、字节跳动、叮咚买菜、拼多多、唯品会等平台均已经发布承诺书，承诺绝不不滥用市场支配地位迫使他人接受“二选一”。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
