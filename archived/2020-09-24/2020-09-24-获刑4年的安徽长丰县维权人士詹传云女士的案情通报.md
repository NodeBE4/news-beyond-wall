<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的安徽长丰县维权人士詹传云女士的案情，现通报如下：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>詹传云：</b>女，<span lang="EN-US">1967</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日出生，安徽省长丰县岗集镇人，长丰县岗集镇南洪社居委张小圩组<span lang="EN-US">60</span>号村民，个体裁缝，维权访民，中国在押维权人士。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因所辖地区政府将其宅基地拆迁、承包地被征收却未获合理补偿，随于<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日到长丰县政府投诉，但因触及到地方执政者的利益，多次投诉而无果，故被迫进京上访，以期合理公正的解决要求；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因其多次投诉，尤其是进京上访维权，遂被安徽省长丰县当局视为维稳打击的对象；曾被地方维稳人员及驻京截访人员多次殴打，甚至因到长丰县信访办投诉而被警方施暴殴打致伤；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因在北京市信访局投诉，被闻讯赶去的岗集镇信访办主任张自文及数名截访人员殴打致伤，之后被强行遣返原籍；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，曾被长丰县警方刑事拘留，后被正式逮捕，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被安徽省长丰县法院以“妨碍公务罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刑满出狱后，因继续向上级部门信访控诉，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日和<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日曾再遭维稳成员的殴打致伤；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其被长丰县警方从家里带走，并被以涉嫌“敲诈勒索（政府）罪”刑拘；一个月后又被长丰县检察院以同罪由予以批捕；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其案在安徽省长丰县法院开庭审理，因其在最后陈述时坚称无罪，终被长丰县法院以“敲诈勒索罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
