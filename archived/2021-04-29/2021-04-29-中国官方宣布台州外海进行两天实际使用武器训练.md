[中国官方宣布台州外海进行两天实际使用武器训练](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210429-1143068)
------

<div>
<p>中国官方宣布，浙江台州外海大陈岛以东海域从今天（29日）起进行为期两天的实际使用武器训练。</p><p>据新浪军事微博账号报道，浙江海事局昨天通过中国海事局网站发布航行警告，公布受影响海域从今早7时至明晚7时举行训练，并禁止船只驶入。</p><p>解放军东部战区也在昨天公布，东部战区海军某驱逐舰支队淄博舰、厦门舰、安阳舰等多艘驱护舰组成编队，奔赴某海域开展实战化训练。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>中国央视军事26日也在微博公布，东部战区海军航空兵某轰炸机团，在东海某海域开展实战化下半夜远海突击训练。多型轰炸机挂载多种武器，采用无线电静默方式批次起飞，组成编队直奔目标海域，超低空飞行隐蔽突防，多波次对“敌”舰艇目标实施模拟导弹攻击。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
