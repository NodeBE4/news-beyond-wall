class="t-content__body u-clearfix"><p><span style="font-size:12pt"><span style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family:DengXian">连昨日新增的</span>6<span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family:Calibri,sans-serif"><span lang="ZH-CN" style="font-family:DengXian">港府昨天宣布，公众地方戴口罩的措施持续，但由星期五凌晨起，会放宽限聚令和食肆堂食人数至</span>4<span lang="ZH-CN"
