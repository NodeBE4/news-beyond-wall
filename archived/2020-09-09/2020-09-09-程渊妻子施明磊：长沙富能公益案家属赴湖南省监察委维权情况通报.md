<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p></p><p>今日，<a name="_Hlk50569045" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">长沙公益</a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">案家属吴有水、施明磊及谢燕益来到湖南省监察委，对湖南省国安厅厅长、长沙国安局李传思（局长）、林圣新、吴钊、张鲁浩、赵倩、张磊；长沙市检察院韩冰囡、王勋爵（检察长）李维宁（处长）、夏俊旭、蒋彬；长沙中院赵喆、黎军（副院长）；司法局尹小英（局长）等</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">17</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">人提出控告。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天来湖南省监察委控告，监察委的接待中心设置在长沙市八一路<span lang="EN-US">240</span>号（湖南省信访中心）。信访的官卡一道接一道：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、先是健康码（如果没有智能机的人就被挡在外面了）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、是前台登记处，身份证号、手机号被工作人员抄写到小本本上作档案用（估计是打标签用）这里是下午<span lang="EN-US">3</span>点才上班，而这里只能领一张表；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、第三道官卡设置在二楼，要拿出控告资料，工作人员看完估计又挡回去一大波。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">、第四道官卡设置在楼梯口，我们<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个人登记，带一个小孩，保安拦住不让孩子上去，要我把小孩留在二楼再上楼，我说你给我带小孩吗？小孩当然要跟着监护人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">最终，只同意上去<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个大人带着孩子。让我想起了<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个人吃<span lang="EN-US">9</span>人饭的事。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">湖南省监察委在<span lang="EN-US">4</span>楼，<span lang="EN-US">408</span>的田主任接待了我们。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">我们介绍 长沙公益案三人已经被任意羁押<span lang="EN-US">415</span>天，至今律师不能会见，合法的所聘的<span lang="EN-US">6</span>位律师被强迫解除，换上官派律师，官派律师被公检法作为秘密不告知家属。家属们已向两级检察院、湖南省国安厅各级部门多次反馈和控告，这里将是家属们在湖南控告的最后一站。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">湖南省监察委田主任表示国安的案子他们很少处理，几乎没有先例，因此不确定该控告他们是否会受理，另外国安办案比较特殊，也许长沙公益案<span lang="EN-US">3</span>人做了什么事，你们家属也不知道。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
