<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：广东惠州公益维权人士肖育辉因在微信发起谴责缅甸军方的签名，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日凌晨<span lang="EN-US">2</span>点被传唤到派出所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">根据网络公开的信息显示，肖育辉在多个微信群组，发起谴责缅甸军方发动政变的联合声明。<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，包括清远、深圳、揭阳等地的网友因为参与签名被约谈。肖育辉也先后被广州洛溪派出所、惠州市公安局横沥派出所、惠州市国保约谈。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">22</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">日凌晨三十分，肖育辉户籍地横沥派出所要求他马上到派出所报到，肖称在距离<span lang="EN-US">30</span>公里的惠州市区，而且深夜无法前往。<span lang="EN-US">20</span>分钟后，圆通桥派出所再次致电，要求肖育辉马上到派出所报到，否则将不允许他在辖区内居住。肖育辉只好于凌晨<span lang="EN-US">2</span>点到达圆通桥派出所，在被迫写下保证书后才获许回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
