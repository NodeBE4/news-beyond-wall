font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月起，因当局责令地方国保在其住所外面值班，每日跟踪监视其上下班，致其日常隐私与公民权利遭到严重侵犯；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
