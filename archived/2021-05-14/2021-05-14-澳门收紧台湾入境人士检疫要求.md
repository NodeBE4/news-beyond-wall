[澳门收紧台湾入境人士检疫要求](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210514-1146691)
------

<div>
<p>因应台湾冠病疫情升温，澳门已由今日凌晨零时起，收紧台湾入境人士的检疫要求。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，澳门应变协调中心昨日宣布，因应台湾地区疫情变化，自14日凌晨零时起，所有入境前14天内曾到过台湾地区人士，须按照澳门卫生当局要求，在指定地点接受14天医学观察，以及最少七天自我健康管理，措施不适用于已入境但未完成医学观察人士。</p><p>协调中心还指，在台湾地区登机到澳门时，须持有的核酸阴性结果证明，亦由目前的七天内缩短至需于72小时内作出。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
