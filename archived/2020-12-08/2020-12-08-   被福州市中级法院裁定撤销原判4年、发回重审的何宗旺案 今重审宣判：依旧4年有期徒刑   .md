[被福州市中级法院裁定撤销原判4年、发回重审的何宗旺案 今重审宣判：依旧4年有期徒刑](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/4-4-2020-12-8-4-2020-9-1-4-2014-37-2015.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年的福建福清维权人士何宗旺案于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被福州市中级法院裁定：撤销原判 发回重审。代理此案的律师是广东的闻宇律师和山东的张金武律师。福州中院裁定原审部分认定事实不清、证据不足，发回闽清法院重新审理。今获悉重审已宣判：依旧<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年有期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何宗旺：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">福建省福清市的公民，长期致力于为弱势群体发声。<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年在北京经营药材生意的他因为转发金水桥事件的帖子被刑拘<span lang="EN-US">37</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年开始参与线下围观，<span lang="EN-US">4.29</span>苏州拜祭林昭，到郑州旁听贾灵敏案庭审。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">两度赴庆安围观徐纯合案，期间被拘留十天。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">“<span lang="EN-US">709</span>”系列案的潍坊案中被捕。潍坊看守所内遭受连续六天的疲劳审讯，关押近三个月后脊椎囊肿爆裂，导致下肢瘫痪且大小便失禁。潍坊看守所将其送院后，通知其家人取保接回福建老家治疗，留下后遗症。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，何宗旺在福州市第一看守所门口迎接刑满获释的严兴声时被抓捕，关押在福州市第二看守所。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">当局先抓捕再寻找罗织罪证，他们以何宗旺在<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月闽清水灾过后进入灾区了解灾区情况为由，以“编造、故意传播虚假信息罪”执行逮捕，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月将其转往闽清看守所关押。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，何宗旺被闽清法院以“编造、故意传播虚假信息罪、寻衅滋事罪”两个罪名分别判处<span lang="EN-US">2.5</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，合并执行<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年有期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，原判决遭福州中院裁定撤销，发回重审。今重审依旧维持<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年有期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对何宗旺案的进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
