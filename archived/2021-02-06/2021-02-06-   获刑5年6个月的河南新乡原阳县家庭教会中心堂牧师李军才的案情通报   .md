<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的河南新乡原阳县家庭教会中心堂牧师李军才的案情，现通报如下<span lang="EN-US">:<o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李军才<span lang="EN-US">:</span></span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">河南省新乡市人，基督徒，河南省新乡市原阳县家庭教会中心堂牧师，中国在押良心犯。<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></b></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因反对中共当局强拆十字架，拒绝把教堂内“爱神爱人”标语改成“爱国爱教”及在教堂内建国旗台，随遭河南省当局打压逼迫；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，李军才被新乡市原阳县警方以涉嫌“妨害公务罪”刑事拘留，另有三名教会同工（即神职工作人员）亦被抓捕，随后几日，其所在中心堂十字架迅速被当地宗教局、公安局及城管等多个部门联合强拆，教堂内“爱神爱人”等标语被清除，教堂内被强行竖立起五星红旗；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一个多月后，被抓三名同工均获取保候审，而其却被一直羁押不放，后被原阳县检察院正式批捕，指控其涉嫌“职务侵占、妨害公务和销毁账目”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其案件已在两度退回公安局补充侦查后，再次移送检察院，进入审查起诉阶段；据悉，其儿子称，当局对其父亲的一切指控均属莫须有罪名，旨在对其拒绝与官方合作态度进行刻意“打击报复”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在被关押长达<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个多月后，河南新乡原阳县家庭教会牧师李军才于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月被以“职务侵占”、“妨害公务”及“销毁账目”三罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期从<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">2024</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日。另三人均判缓刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前李军才被羁押于新乡市原阳县看守所（河南省新乡市原阳县葛埠口乡丘庙村，邮政编号：<span lang="EN-US">453500</span>。）<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
