[山东济南维权人士于新永二审宣判：驳回上诉，维持原判](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/10/blog-post_41.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本文获悉：山东济南维权人士于新永<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日因维权被当局无辜关押在济南市看守所至今，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日“开庭”居然不允许旁听包括家人。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，于新永<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日上诉，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日济南市中级法院徐法官告知律师二审不能公开开庭，只做书面审理。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日二审宣判：驳回上诉，维持原判。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">于新永：<span lang="EN-US">1963</span>年出生，山东省济南市人，网名“济南老于”，前山东省冶金工业总公司工作人员，自由职业者，警察非法暴力监督组发起人之一，民间抗霾志愿者，民主维权公民，中国在押政治犯。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因个性为人实在仗义，热情智慧，而广受友人赞许和信任；近年来，因追求民主理念，积极参与帮助蒙冤访民维权活动，要求政府信息公开，起诉山东省多个政要部门等，故而遭到山东省当局的严厉打压被迫；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，曾因拟发起成立“山东维权联盟”，而被山东警方定为重点维稳对象；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因国内爆出多起警察非法开枪打人、打死人新闻，而与他人共同发起成立“警察非法暴力监督组” ，以关注呼吁诸如张六毛案、舒向新案、山东临沂少年派出所死亡案、徐明死亡案等社会热点案件，故而触及当局敏感神经，曾被警方多次约谈和警告；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">-2017</span>年，曾因参与民间抗霾自愿者聚会，并率先起诉了济南市环保局，而遭到当局的干预与遏制；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被济南市警方突然从家中带走传唤，并以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，被济南市检察院又新添涉嫌“侮辱罪”正式批捕，控其曾经撰写的文章侮辱了前任和现任的国家领导人，涉嫌侮辱罪，从而被逮捕；其被提审过程中，主要被审问的是<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年前其与他人纪念六四学运之事，被指有人举报他们在<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年前曾非法聚会，纪念<span lang="EN-US">6.4</span>学运并拍摄视频上传网络等；<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，其案被济南市历下区检察院以“侮辱罪”、“寻衅滋事罪”起诉至历下区法院；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其案将在济南市历下区法院开庭审理。目前被羁押于济南看守所（济南市仲宫镇）。后被以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
