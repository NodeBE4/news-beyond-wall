<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">楞珠扎巴：西藏歌手。楞珠扎巴于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被拘留，距离他<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月发行歌曲“黑帽子”不到两个月。<a name="_Hlk55251161">在经过一年多的审前拘留之后，他于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月被判刑，被剥夺了辩护判权。</a>楞珠扎巴最终判处<span lang="EN-US">6</span>年徒刑，理由是他在西藏那曲市比如县演唱批评政府镇压政策的歌曲。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
