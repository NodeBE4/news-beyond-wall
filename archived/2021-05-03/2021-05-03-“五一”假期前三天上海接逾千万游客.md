[“五一”假期前三天上海接逾千万游客](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210503-1144024)
------

<div>
<p>上海市文旅局公布，2021年“五一”假期前三天，上海共接待游客1030.31万人次。上海全市宾馆旅馆平均客房出租率为73.2%，较2020年同期增长48个百分点，较2019年同期增长13个百分点。</p><p>据央视新闻报道，上海旅游大数据监测，上海170家主要景区（点）累计接待游客466万人次，较2020年同期增长166%，较2019年同期增长6%。</p><p>其中，东方明珠8.3万人次；朱家角古镇10.1万人次；上海国际旅游度假区接待游客30.26万人次；崇明世界级生态岛接待游客38.83万人次。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>开放式文化旅游区域共接待游客823.73万人次。其中，外滩、豫园和小陆家嘴等区域分别接待游客140.85万人次、56.18万人次和92.65万人次，分别同比增长261.06%、572.85%和171.37%。</p><p> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
