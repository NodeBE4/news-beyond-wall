[香港近百万民众已接受首剂冠病疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210503-1143920)
------

<div>
<p>香港冠病疫苗接种计划自2月26日正式展开，至5月2日已是第66日。接种计划开展以来，约95.21万名民众已接种第一剂疫苗，约53.98万名民众已接种第二剂疫苗。</p><p>据香港中通社报道，香港特区政府公布，已为民众接种共约149.19万剂疫苗。当中约95.21万名民众已接种第一剂疫苗，包括约43.88万名接种科兴疫苗，约51.33万名接种复必泰疫苗。约53.98万名民众已接种第二剂疫苗，包括约32.01万名接种科兴疫苗，约21.96万名接种复必泰疫苗。</p><p>截至昨天下午8时的过去24小时，约有3.75万名民众接种了疫苗和约1.95万名市民在网上预约接种疫苗。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
