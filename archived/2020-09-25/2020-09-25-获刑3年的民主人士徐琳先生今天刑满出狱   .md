<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">25</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年的民主人士徐琳先生今天刑满出狱。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><a name="_Hlk51962320"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐琳</span></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">先生早上已经回到广州番禺家里与妻儿团聚。由广州维稳部门派人从监狱接回。徐琳曾创作《站在正义一边》等颂扬自由民主等普世价值的歌曲。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年被当局以寻衅滋事的罪名构陷入狱<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。今天是<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年刑期坐满出狱，徐琳拒绝认罪，未获任何减刑。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">徐琳：<span lang="EN-US">1964</span>年出生，湖南省株洲市人，诗人，哲学研究者，广东网络作家，独立中文笔会成员，民主异议人士，中国政治犯。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年代末即开始撰写时政文章，诗歌及创作歌曲，至<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年始又在广州，深圳等地积极开展街头举牌，演讲，组织公民集会等活动<span lang="EN-US">;</span>近年来，因与维权公民刘四仿合作，创作并出品了多个以自由，人权，法治为内容的歌曲，颇受自由民主爱好者喜爱与欢迎，遂引起广东当局强烈不满，并成为维稳严控的对象，曾遭多次非法拘禁和秘密关押<span lang="EN-US">;</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因将浙江异见人士朱虞夫所作诗歌“是时候了”改编谱曲为“就是现在”，引起网友广泛关注和转载而被广州警方于同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪“刑拘，羁押于广州市番禹看守所，指控其罪行比广州上街举牌还要严重，具有明显煽动群众上街意向，情节十分严重<span lang="EN-US">;</span>后于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日又被广东省当局改为秘密监视居住它处，期间其家属多次要求当局出示书面通知及关押地点，竟都遭拒<span lang="EN-US">;</span>同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，曾参与民主人士王登朝发起的<span lang="EN-US">'</span>莲花山公园千人公民集会<span lang="EN-US">'</span>，计划于活动现场发表政治演讲，不料事前却遭当局绑架，后被秘密关押<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月<span lang="EN-US">;</span></span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，曾因发布“<span lang="EN-US">709</span>”大抓捕事件而创作的歌曲“正义律师之歌”触怒当局，其任职公司因受当局施压而遭失业，并被绑架至广州软禁<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，中共<span lang="EN-US">19</span>大召开前夕，其在湖南老家照顾患病父亲时突然被广州市南沙区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，其广州住所被查抄，搜走计算机，手机，书籍，笔记本等个人物品；次日，与其一起创作歌曲的搭档刘四方也被江西省宜春市警方以同理由抓走；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，被广州市南沙区检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕，关押在广州市南沙区看守所；据悉，被押期间遭酷刑，从<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日每天都被警方三班倒提讯；一直零口供；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，被广州市南沙区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日。其在广东省佛山市高明监狱服刑。今刑满出狱。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
