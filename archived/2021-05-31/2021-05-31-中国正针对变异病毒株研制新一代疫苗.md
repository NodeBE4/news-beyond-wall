[中国正针对变异病毒株研制新一代疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210531-1150649)
------

<div>
<p>中国疾病预防控制中心研究员邵一鸣说，中心已经在针对变异株研制新一代疫苗。</p><p>据央视新闻报道，中国灭活疫苗的技术路线特别适合快速应对变异株。整个疫苗工艺不做丝毫改变，只是在投料端把原来的早期流行株换成现在对我们有威胁的变异株。</p><p>他指出，经过一个生产周期，这一头出来的就是变异株的疫苗，所以说百姓不用担心。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>邵一鸣说，正在分析已接种疫苗大约半年的病例资料，初步判断大约一半的人对病毒仍有一定防御能力，会根据追踪实验去确定已接种人士要在何时接种加强针，认为目前最重要是完成首次疫苗接种，尽快建立免疫屏障。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
