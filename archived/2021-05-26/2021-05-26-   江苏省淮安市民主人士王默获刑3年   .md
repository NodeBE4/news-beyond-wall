[江苏省淮安市民主人士王默获刑3年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/05/3.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月遭拘捕的江苏省淮安市民主人士王默获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，目前关押在江苏省南京监狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王默：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1972</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，江苏省淮安市人，网名“菜王默刀”，人称“黑犬”，民间知名新浪“转世党”，南方街头运动践行者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因追求民主常在网上及南方街头发表积极言论而为民间所熟知，是近年走出网络开始参与现实抗争的典范；曾先后参与多起，诸如“曲阜薛福顺非正常死亡事件”、 “新余刘萍等三人开庭声援围观”、“郑州十君子声援团”、“焦作张小玉事件调查”等大型抗争活动，遂遭到警方多次打压和被行政拘留； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，曾因在江西省新余刘萍等三人开庭时举牌声援，被警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因在广东省广州市窖口地铁站旁举牌寻找民主维权人士张圣雨时，被警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，曾因在河南省焦作市参加张小玉事件调查活动，而被警方抓走并刑拘<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，再次因在广州市多地拉横幅声援香港民主抗争、“真普选”，被警方从广州市增城区新塘镇带走，并被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，被广州市检察院更换罪名，以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式批捕，被关押在广州市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被广州市中级法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，并送往韶关市乐昌监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">获释一个月后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，又被江苏省淮安市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，被羁押于江苏省淮安市看守所。后音信全无。近期维权朋友去探访王默父亲，获悉王默已被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，现在关押到江苏省南京监狱。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
