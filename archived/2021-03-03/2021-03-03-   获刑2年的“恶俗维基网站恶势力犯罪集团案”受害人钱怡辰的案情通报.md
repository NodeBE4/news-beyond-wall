[获刑2年的“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”受害人钱怡辰的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/2_3.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”受害人钱怡辰的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">钱怡辰：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1997</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，上海市人，上海市杨浦区许昌路<span lang="EN-US">630</span>弄<span lang="EN-US">14</span>号<span lang="EN-US">102</span>室公民，音乐人，电音极道头，常用网名<span lang="EN-US">Kolaa</span>、<span lang="EN-US">Kolaa_</span>可乐子、<span lang="EN-US">Kolaa_Kolaako</span>、骷髅翁，恶俗维基管理员，“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”受害人之一，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年起，开始进入音<span lang="EN-US">MAD</span>圈，并因其编辑技术高超而很快获得了许多称赞；之后又开始以惊人速度学习恶俗，很快即被聘为恶俗维基网管理员；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因多家“恶俗圈”网站频发习近平之女习明泽、姐夫邓家贵的信息（含户籍和照片等个人信息），其所在“恶俗维基”等网站亦将此类信息挂于网上，遂立遭中共当局的严厉打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，公安部迅速成立项目组，要求对全国该网站会员实施大抓捕；之后经严格审讯，发觉他们大多年龄尚小且行为基本无罪，故各地警方将所抓之人纷纷予以释放，唯广东省茂名市警方急于立功，就声称要给中共建政<span lang="EN-US">70</span>周年献大礼，移花接木嫁祸“恶俗维基”，把该警局所属网站会员（含其在内）尽都抓捕，并上报公安部谎称“破获了一起与反华势力相勾结的恶势力集团大案”；同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，其被广东省茂名市警方跨省抓捕，后被指定居所监视居住和刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被茂名市检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人“涉恶势力犯罪集团案”被广东省茂名市茂南区法院开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，此案<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人均被宣判，而钱怡辰被该法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪” 判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日；因其等均表不服，已经上诉；截至目前，二审正在等待中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
