[获刑19年的哈萨克族公民恰特合·达吾列提案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/19_16.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年的哈萨克族公民恰特合·达吾列提案情，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">恰特合·达吾列提：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1988</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁哈萨克斯坦自治州人，伊犁哈萨克斯坦自治州察布查尔锡伯自治县捷仁布拉克镇<span lang="EN-US">67</span>团<span lang="EN-US">8</span>连<a name="_Hlk56462139">哈萨克族公民</a>，中国在押良心犯。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">被抓捕前，一直是遵纪守法的好公民，并因身有严重疾患长期与病魔做着殊死搏斗；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，曾被新疆建设兵团农四师医院诊断为慢性粒细胞白血病；后因病情严重，于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被转到新疆医科大学第一附属医院住院治疗<span lang="EN-US">8</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，曾两度前往北京市住院治疗，但又因无力承担高昂的治疗费用<span lang="EN-US">,</span>只能中途放弃治疗；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，其病情再次复发，不得不在新疆医科大学第一附属医院住院治疗，每月药费<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万元；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，恰特合·达吾列提被伊犁州察布查尔锡伯自治县警方突然以不明罪由抓捕，被送入当地“教育培训中心”（又称“再教育营”）接受中共洗脑式教育；同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，旋即被察布查尔锡伯自治县检察院予以批捕；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，被伊犁哈萨克斯坦自治州察布查尔锡伯自治县法院以不明罪名重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2037</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其被判刑前因身患白血病严重，医生建议做干细胞移植手术，但向狱方申请保外就医以求手术治疗，遭拒；又悉，因其被抓捕判刑，其家人（妻子、两个孩子及其母亲）生活陷入困境。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前疑似在新疆伊犁哈萨克斯坦自治州新源监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
