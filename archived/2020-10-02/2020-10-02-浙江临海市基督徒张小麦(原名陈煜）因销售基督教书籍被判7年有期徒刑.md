[浙江临海市基督徒张小麦(原名陈煜）因销售基督教书籍被判7年有期徒刑](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/10/7.html)
------

<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：浙江临海市基督徒张小麦<span lang="EN-US">(</span>原名陈煜）因在开设网上书店“小麦书房”销售基督教书籍<span lang="EN-US">,</span>于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月被捕<span lang="EN-US">,2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日一审判决，以非法经营罪判<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年有期徒刑。并处以罚金<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万，所扣押<span lang="EN-US">12864</span>本书予以销毁。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在张小麦于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月被中共当局以「网上销售非法境外宗教出版物」被捕后，公安部曾通报全国，要求各地国保根据从「小麦书房」搜到的发货订单接触收货人，就其购买书籍的取向和「当地非法宗教活动的情况」进行取证，有上万人被问询。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中共当局对宗教镇压日益严厉，出售基督教书籍竟然判刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，令人骇然。判决书同时裁定，被告除了被罚<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万元外，还须追缴售书违法所得的<span lang="EN-US">20</span>万元上缴国库，无法追缴的，由被告退赔，并须予判决生效后十天内缴纳。这是对传播基督教的基督徒的赤裸裸的镇压。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对张小麦<span lang="EN-US">(</span>原名陈煜）的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
