[被羁押的港民主党前主席胡志伟奔丧被拒](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210505-1144495)
------

<div>
<p>因香港民主派初选案还押的民主党前主席胡志伟，申请外出给92岁父亲奔丧的申请被拒绝。</p><p>据《明报》报道，胡志伟的父亲丧礼周五（7日）举行。惩教署今日（5日）宣布，决定应用传输视像信息科技，首次安排以视像方式让胡志伟参与丧礼。</p><p>香港惩教署说，据《监狱规则》第17条，署方会考虑在囚者所犯罪行、保安风险、押解路线、地点安全等各项保安因素。署方留意到丧礼的日期、时间及地点较早时被社交媒体广泛报道，网上亦有人号召丧礼当日到场支持。经保安评估后，为保障惩教人员、在囚者及公众安全，署方决定不批准有关外出申请。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>惩教署表示，近年署方致力发展智慧监狱，传输视像信息是其中一项主要发展范畴，决定应用有关科技，首次安排以视像方式参与丧礼，让在囚者考虑。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
