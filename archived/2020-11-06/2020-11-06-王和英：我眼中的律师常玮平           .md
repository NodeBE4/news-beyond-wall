font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">快刀短笔话凄凉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">回眸望路在何方？<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">家国不幸隐忠良！</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上面我以“盼常玮平尽快回家”八个字做藏头诗一首来表达我对常律的心愿。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日听说我的律师常玮平又被指定监视居住，至今没有出来。忧伤愤懑之余只能写点东西说说我眼中的律师常玮平。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">经我的法律顾问唐吉田律师的推荐，常玮平律师，黄志强律师和吴绍平律师在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年春天来到我家谈接手我案件的事。三位律师都是谈吐不俗，意气风发。常律最年轻个子也最高，他幽默风趣，随意洒脱，吃饭不吃主食，称减肥。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常律让我记忆最深的是<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月末从陕西老家乘飞机赶到昆山来营救我。那是因为在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日我去北戴河旅游被苏州驻京办的工作人员强行押回昆山，然后就是漫长的被限制出门。十几个人轮流两班倒在门外看守。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">8</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月末我请他们几位律师来昆山营救我出去。他们三位律师都是上午到的，由于吴律有急事先回上海。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">下午我在常律和黄律的保护下走出房门，门外看守的几个人立马进入战备状态。其中一个脖子上挂着大金链子流氓气十足的黑保安，退下金链子和手表就撸胳膊上来“碰瓷”两位律师。我急忙在中间阻隔，生怕两位律师被“碰瓷”成功。那样的话岂不是我给他们带来无妄之灾？就算是十来个人边走边“打嘴仗”，我觉得与那些人纠缠不清都是对他俩人格的侮辱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后来我们出了小区打的上车，那些人就拦在车前，那个流氓开车门叫嚣律师约架。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">没办法我们下车报警，半小时没人出警。我们又步行走到派出所报案。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">虽然那次没有解救成功，但是他们为了我据理力争无畏无惧的英雄气概让我特别感动！自此后他们几位律师在我的心里不再只是我花钱请的律师，而是我的朋友，我的邻家小弟。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">等我自由后，我与四位律师约在上海见面，晚饭时常律的妻子和儿子也过来了，常律说他与博士后妻子恋爱八年才结婚，看到常律一家人幸福快乐的样子，让我想起那首吉祥三宝的歌。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">最近一次匆匆见常律也已经是去年年末在北京了。谁都不能料到接下来没多久就传出很多人被抓捕。我熟悉的常律，黄律，刘书庆律师，许志永博士等先后被关押，大多暂时被放出来。那个春节除了疫情肆虐就是这许多朋友的坏消息让人提心吊胆……<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本以为常律在一月份被指定监视居住<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天后取保候审，出来后就没什么大事了。谁成想在<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日又被抓进去了，此前他发布视频谈到自己坐老虎凳等酷刑问题。难道此次被抓与此有关？我不想多说什么，好在公道自在人心。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
