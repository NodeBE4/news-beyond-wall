[因发布视频被抓捕的甘肃青年张盼成的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/02/blog-post_71.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">21</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉因发布视频被抓捕的甘肃青年张盼成的案情，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张盼成</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1995</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日出生，甘肃省庆阳市合水县人，网名为“子慕予兮”，中国西北农村长大留守儿童，前北京大学保安，正义公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年出生于甘肃农村，是典型的留守儿童；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年，曾以公民身份表达自己的意愿；在辞去北京大学保安工作之后，曾放胆自拍视频，发政治主张，称已为民主“做好了牺牲的准备”；被捕前，曾在视频中表示，不同意中国政府将<span lang="EN-US">600</span>亿美元未经法定程序送<span lang="EN-US">(</span>外国）人，要求言论自由，声援被失踪的董瑶琼、华涌、佳士工人和原北大学生岳昕等人，以及进学习班的百万新疆同胞；曾在视频中引用宋代名将文天祥的诗句“人生自古谁无死，留取丹心照汗青”，并说自己对国家有深沉的爱；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被抓捕刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，被北京市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕；审讯中，拒绝写保证书，并拒绝给律师签委托书；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，其案在北京西城区法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，被北京市西城区法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，刑满释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日，因其在网上发布一段在甘肃山村老家里录制的视频，谴责中共暴行，表达心声称“凛凛寒冬终将过去，春天马上要来临”，随后被当地警方带走，至今失联不予释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前疑似被羁押于甘肃省庆阳市合水县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
