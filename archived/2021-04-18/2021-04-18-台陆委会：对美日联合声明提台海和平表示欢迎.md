[台陆委会：对美日联合声明提台海和平表示欢迎](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210417-1140123)
------

<div>
<p>美国总统拜登与日本首相菅义伟在峰会后发表联合声明，强调台海和平稳定的重要性，台湾陆委会对此表达欢迎与感谢，称将持续深化与美日等理念相近国家合作，坚定维护台海与区域和平稳定。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，陆委会表示，对于美日峰会联合声明强调台海和平稳定的重要性、鼓励和平处理两岸问题及关切大陆胁迫作为等，台湾政府对国际社会关切台海情势表达欢迎与感谢，这是我方长期一贯立场，也将持续深化与美日等理念相近国家合作，坚定维护台海与区域的和平稳定。</p><p>陆委会指出，近期以来，大陆当局对周边区域及台湾的强势主张与威吓行径，已对区域安全现状产生严重性影响与危害，台湾政府反对此种非和平、非民主的内外治理方式，也再次呼吁对岸理性面对国际社会关切，放弃以军事外交打压、强迫台湾接受其不当的政治主张，才能真正改善两岸关系。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
