[中国科兴称已向全球供超6亿剂疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210601-1150900)
------

<div>
<p>中国科兴控股今天（1日）称，截至5月31日，科兴已向中国及全球近40个国家和地区供应冠病疫苗超6亿剂，全球接种科兴疫苗（克尔来福）超4.3亿剂。</p><p>据路透社报道，世界卫生组织(WHO)已为科兴疫苗获批寻求更多数据；WHO要求科兴提供有关疫苗安全性和生产流程的细节，然后决定是否推荐全球使用。</p><p>根据公司提供的新闻稿称，巴西圣保罗州政府公布了在巴西东南部小镇开展的科兴疫苗使用效果研究结果。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>研究结果显示，接种疫苗后，该地区因感染病毒导致的死亡人数下降了95%，有症状的病例数下降了80%，住院人数下降了86%。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
