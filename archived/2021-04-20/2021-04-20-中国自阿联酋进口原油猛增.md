[中国自阿联酋进口原油猛增](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210420-1140801)
------

<div>
<p>中国3月从阿联酋进口的原油增长了86%，路透社评论说，其中据信掺杂有伊朗原油。</p><p>据路透社报道，中国3月从沙特阿拉伯进口的原油同比增加8.8%，因需求旺盛，而且因港口拥堵而延迟的船货终于抵达。从阿联酋进口的原油则增长了86%。</p><p>中国海关总署周二公布的数据显示，3月沙特原油的进口量为784万吨，合每日185万桶，高于上年同期的每日170万桶。但进口量低于2月的每日194万桶。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>沙特连续第七个月保持中国最大原油供应国的地位。</p><p>炼厂集中的山东省2月有几周出现港口拥堵，原油到港推迟。</p><p>路孚特分析师预计，鉴于沙特自愿减产100万桶/日，且供应亚洲市场的阿拉伯轻质油涨价，4月沙特原油到港量将会进一步减少。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
