[南京网民造谣打冠病疫苗致死被行拘](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210420-1140794)
------

<div>
<p>中国江苏省南京市一名网民在网络发布“冠病疫苗致人死亡”的假消息，被警方行政拘留七天。</p><p>南京市公安局网络安全保卫支队今天在官方微博通报称，南京一网民近日在某短视频平台发布“新冠疫苗致人死亡”的评论消息，引起群众恐慌。经核实，该消息为虚假信息。</p><p>通报中说，网民潘某某到案后供述，她自己知道疫苗没问题，也正准备去接种。但当她在网上看到排队打疫苗的视频后，为吸引眼球故意发表“新冠疫苗不能打，打死人了”的谣言信息。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>网警提醒，希望大家尊重科学，自觉、积极、主动接种疫苗，并警告，“对于故意造谣传谣，引发社会恐慌的，公安机关将坚决依法处理。”</p><p>中国各地目前正在大规模接种冠病疫苗，据国家卫健委网站消息，截至前天，中国大陆累计报告接种冠病疫苗19212.7万剂次。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
