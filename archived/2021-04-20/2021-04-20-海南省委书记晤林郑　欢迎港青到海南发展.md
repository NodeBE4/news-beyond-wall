[海南省委书记晤林郑　欢迎港青到海南发展](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210420-1140731)
------

<div>
<p>博鳌亚洲论坛年会今天将在海南正式开幕，中共海南省委书记沈晓明昨日与出席年会的行政长官林郑月娥会面。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，沈晓明与林郑月娥会面时说，海南自贸港建设既是海南的重大历史机遇，也为香港市场主体和居民带来积极效应，期待双方加强专业服务、航空、医疗等领域合作，欢迎香港青年来海南研学和就业。</p><p>林郑月娥表示，香港与海南在国内国际双循环中，都处于重要位置，建议双方着眼优势互补、协同发展，不断拓展合作领域，加强两地产业互动，开展青年就业计划，推动琼港合作再上新台阶。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
