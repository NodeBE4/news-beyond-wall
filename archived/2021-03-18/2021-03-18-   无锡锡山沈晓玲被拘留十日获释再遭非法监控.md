[无锡锡山沈晓玲被拘留十日获释再遭非法监控](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/blog-post_74.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：无锡市锡山区沈晓玲因去北京，被无锡市公安局锡山分局枉法拘留十日，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日获释后，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">今天，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">再次遭到锡山区锡北镇丰惠苑社区雇佣保安公司非法监控。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，沈晓玲在北京游玩，被北京市公安局西城分局府右街派出所查验身份证时扣留，然后被府右街派出所直接贩卖给了无锡市驻京办，而没有按规定送往久敬庄，再被无锡驻京办和锡山区信访及公安雇佣北京黑保安绑架回无锡，到达无锡后，被居住地无锡市公安局锡山分局张泾派出所以其在被取保候审期间违反监督管理规定，未经批准离开无锡为由，对沈晓玲作出行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日并处罚款<span lang="EN-US">200</span>元的处罚。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日<span lang="EN-US">7</span>时许，无锡市公安局锡山分局张泾派出所直接到无锡市拘留所将沈晓玲强行接回家，无锡维权人士前往拘留所迎接得知情况后，再次将沈晓玲接到拘留所献花合影。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
