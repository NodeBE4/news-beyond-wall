[因言获罪获刑1年9个月的王兴杰的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/19.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网因言获罪获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">个月的王兴杰的案情，现通报如下：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>王兴杰：</b><span lang="EN-US">1956</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日出生，辽宁省省沈阳市人，咸阳市渭滨区租住居民，网络活跃公民，民主理念追求者，中国在押政治犯。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近些年，因中共当局出台了多项管控信息传播的法律法规，尤其是<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日起施行了中国《互联网群组信息服务管理规定》，遂显示出中共政府及其最高领导人习近平对国民思想言论管控已达到了空前的地步；然而，尽管当局努力设置防火墙，围堵国民利用网络获取境外信息，以维护其一党独大的稳定性，但是仍有人勇于设计翻墙软件，并提给国人到境外网站浏览时使用；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在此背景下，其因追求民主，并活跃于网络社交媒体平台之上，随将诸多国内鲜为人知的海外信息转发到多个微信群，以让更多网民知晓真实的世界及世界眼里的中国、中共及其领导人；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">—<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年，因其将一雷姓网友发来的外海涉政敏感话题发到与自己相关的微信群，遂被网警发现，进而引起中共当局的强烈不满和恐惧；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其及多个网络活跃网民（共计<span lang="EN-US">13</span>人）悉数被当地警方以在<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群传播涉及政治的“虚假信息”为罪由抓捕，其亦被辽宁省沈阳市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，又被移交至陕西省咸阳市秦都区警方，且被以同罪名正式逮捕，被指控：因其“发泄情绪”，通过<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群在网络上“起哄闹事”，散布虚假信息，“攻击政府、党和国家领导人”，破坏社会秩序，所以违法被捕；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，其一行<span lang="EN-US">13</span>人“涉境外政治信息”案在咸阳市秦都区法院开始庭审，终被一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半到<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年不等刑期，其亦被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">目前被羁押于咸阳市渭滨区看守所。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"></span></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
