[刘鹤与美财长耶伦视频通话](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210602-1151122)
------

<div>
<p>中国国务院副总理、中美全面经济对话中方牵头人刘鹤与美财政部长耶伦6月2日上午举行视频通话。</p><p>据新华社报道，双方认为中美经济关系十分重要。双方本着平等和相互尊重的态度，就宏观经济形势和多双边领域合作进行了广泛交流，对彼此关心的问题坦诚交换了意见，愿意继续保持沟通。</p><p>刘鹤不久前在5月27日和美国贸易代表戴琪通话。中国官媒新华社报道，当时双方本着平等和相互尊重的态度，进行了坦诚、务实、建设性的交流。双方认为发展双边贸易非常重要，并就彼此关心的问题交换了意见，同意继续保持沟通。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
