[四川维权人士谢俊彪被刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/06/blog-post_41.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，本网获悉：四川维权人士谢俊彪日前被成都警方刑事拘留。昨天<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日谢俊彪被双流警方以取保候审期间离开居住地为由，已决定刑事拘留，今天下午双流区国宝到家中搜查，搜查十分仔细，刑事拘留通知书还未送达家属。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢俊彪因房屋遭强拆从<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年开始上访维权，后关注“六四天网”创办人黄琦冤案，并积极参加营救活动。他因此多次被警方上门警告、传唤、拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，他在微信群中表示自己又被政府警告，说他接受媒体采访，要重点稳控。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日上午，谢俊彪到四川省人大外举牌，牌子上写“戴表（代表）不为民作主 不如回家抓老鼠”。当天下午<span lang="EN-US">6</span>点多，他在成都双流的朋友家里遭社区警察上门抓捕。后谢俊彪遭当地警方刑事拘留<span lang="EN-US">30</span>天，后于<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，谢俊彪获取保候审。其取保期马上到期，却又遭刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
