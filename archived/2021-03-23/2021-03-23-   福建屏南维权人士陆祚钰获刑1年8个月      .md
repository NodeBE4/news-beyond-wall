[福建屏南维权人士陆祚钰获刑1年8个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/18.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，本网获悉：福建屏南维权人士陆祚钰即今日宣判，陆祚钰被福建柘荣县人民法院以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月，即自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日起至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日止。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陆祚钰（女）：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1979</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日出生，福建省宁德市屏南县古汾镇长汾村人，失地农民，维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因其全家<span lang="EN-US">8</span>口共十余亩耕地被当地政府暴力强征，且因阻止反抗，全家人竟被政府雇佣的征地人员打伤，报警警方也不予理睬，其被迫走上维权抗争之路；又因其不断向各级相关部门投诉、反映当地政府的违法征地行为，尽管官方也知他们的处理解决方式不符常理，甚至属于违法行为，但认为其维权抗争行为又触碰了当权者的各方利益，随对其屡屡打压和严密监控；自从走上维权之路，其不仅为自己维权，也积极协助其他公民维权，故此，<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年曾因组织村民集体维权而被地方法院以“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，因进京上访维权，而被福建驻京维稳办人员截访抓回，并被莫名非法拘禁于屏南县一农庄，直至<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日才得以获释；因其再度进京投诉抗争，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，又被福建驻京维稳办人员从北京截回，并被屏南县警方以“冒用他人身份证”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">9</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，刚获释放的她再次被屏南县城关镇派出所警方予以传唤，随后即被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，被屏南县警方以同罪名转正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，其案在宁德市柘荣县法院以远程视频方式开庭受审，闻讯赶来参加旁听的众亲友及维权人士被拒之门外；今获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
