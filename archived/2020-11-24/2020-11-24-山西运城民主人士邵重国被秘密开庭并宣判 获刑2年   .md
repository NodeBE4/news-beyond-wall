<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：日前，山西运城民主人士邵重国被秘密开庭并宣判，邵重国获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。代理律师卢廷阁将</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">继续为其上诉辩护。</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-bidi-font-family: 宋体;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，邵重国曾因在微信群“亡共倒计时”里发贴贬斥、批评中共时任国家领导人习近平，而被运城市警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，曾因为民主公民子肃公开信代写资料，又被运城市警方处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，曾因去陕西省佳县探访友人高智晟（注：高智晟当时虽已刑满释放，但仍被严控于陕北窑洞家中），而被陕西省榆林市警方知晓后抓捕、刑拘，并被关押在陕西省佳县看守所；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被陕西省佳县检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕，<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，又被检察院以同罪由提起公诉，指控其在推特上发信息贬斥中共当局及国家领导人习近平，于违法行为；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，家属称其因涉“高智晟事件”而被陕西省佳县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>个月，并且在看守所期间曾受到虐待（如被打、不准吃饭及不给提供卫生用品等）；后刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;
