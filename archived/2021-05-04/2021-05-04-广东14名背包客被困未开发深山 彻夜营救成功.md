[广东14名背包客被困未开发深山 彻夜营救成功](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210504-1144190)
------

<div>
<p>中国广东有14名驴友（背包客）困在未经开发的石公山中，当地消防彻夜搜寻，最终将14人营救成功。</p><p>据《广州日报》报道，广东清远连山县2日下午突降暴雨，14名正在徒步穿越禾洞镇大龙山脉的游客，被困在未经开发的石公山中。</p><p>消防救援人员接报后，在崎岖和湿滑山路中彻夜搜寻。在翻过四个山头后，终于今天（3日）早上8时许，将被困游客全部找到。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>彼时，14名游客生命体征平稳，意识清醒，但因长时间未进食，饥寒交迫，体力早已透支。救援人员利用安全大绳轮流对被困人员进行搀扶和背抱，将他们全部转移到山下休息。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
