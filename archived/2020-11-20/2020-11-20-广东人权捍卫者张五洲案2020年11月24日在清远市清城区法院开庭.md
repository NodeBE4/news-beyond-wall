<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉：广东人权捍卫者张五洲案<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日在清远市清城区法院开庭。今年六四期间张五洲因在广州街头手持<span lang="EN-US">"</span>勿忘六四、撤回恶法<span lang="EN-US">"</span>标语，悼念六四和反对推行港区国安法，遭当局抓捕，后遭刑事拘留、批捕、起诉。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">家住广东清远市的湖北籍人权捍卫者张五洲女士，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日当天，前往广州著名风景区白云山，在山上栏杆上打出横幅，上书“勿忘六世，撤回恶法”、“女律师被殴目击证人”等字样，以此纪念<span lang="EN-US">31</span>年前的六四事件，以及反对中共两会刚刚通过的香港国安法的实施。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张五洲把该活动拍照及视频发微信朋友圈和其它平台发布后，<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日下午<span lang="EN-US">14</span>时<span lang="EN-US">30</span>分，广东清远市公安局清城区分局石角派出所以张五洲“涉嫌扰乱公共秩序”为由将其清远市家中传唤带走，随后被刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日来警方又来抄家。后遭逮捕、起诉。在狱中遭受酷刑虐待。张五洲从<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日到<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日在清新看守所一直戴着一百多斤的脚镣，而铐住手的手铐则铐在脚镣上，整个人只能缩成一团，这样持续了<span lang="EN-US">6</span>天<span lang="EN-US">6</span>夜，感觉是生不如死。在里面还被同仓牢头殴打。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张五洲是被关进广州白云区看守所死亡的张六毛的姐姐。张五洲为了给弟弟的不明死亡讨回公道受到打压，开始关注公益和参与一些民主、人权活动。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，广州女律师孙世华在广州荔湾区华林派出所办案时，受到该所民警殴打并遭脱衣检查侮辱。随行的张五洲和梁颂基作为目击者，在网上公开为孙世华作证。广州警方为此报复，以“寻衅滋事罪”抓捕了张五洲和梁颂基。后梁颂基被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半、张五洲<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对张五洲女士的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
