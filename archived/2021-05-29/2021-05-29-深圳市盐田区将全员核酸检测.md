[深圳市盐田区将全员核酸检测](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210529-1150246)
------

<div>
<p>深圳市盐田区新冠肺炎疫情防控指挥部今天发布通告，呼吁此前因出差、外出等原因未接受核酸检测的市民尽快前往就近的核酸检测点主动进行采样，确保应检尽检、不漏一人。</p><p>根据央视新闻报道，深圳盐田决定在全区范围开展冠病病毒核酸检测和疫苗接种“双百行动”，即实现全区全员核酸检测和目标人群冠病病毒疫苗接种两个100%覆盖。在前期已基本完成全员核酸检测的基础上，对重点人群实行“七天三检”并陆续开展第二轮、第三轮核酸检测。</p><p>据了解，自昨天起，深圳盐田区在大梅沙社康中心、盐田区人民医院盐田院区核酸采样帐篷处、盐田区人民医院本部门诊核酸采样帐篷处设立了三个临时采样点，每天工作时间为8时至12时、14时至22时，为之前因各种原因未能进行核酸检测的居民提供补采服务。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
