[广州市四地升级为中风险地区](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210529-1150212)
------

<div>
<p>自今日（29日）起，广州市荔湾区白鹤洞街、中南街、东漖街、冲口街四地区由低风险地区调整为中风险地区。</p><p>根据广州市卫健委网站消息，广州市新冠肺炎防控指挥办医疗防治组今天发布通告，根据广东省新冠肺炎防控指挥办相关通知，经广州市新冠肺炎防控指挥部同意，自2021年5月29日起，广州市荔湾区白鹤洞街、中南街、东漖街、冲口街由低风险地区调整为中风险地区，其他地区风险等级不变。</p><p>根据卫健委网站最新消息，广州市前天无新增确诊病例。新增出院病例三例。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>截至前天，累计报告境外输入确诊病例828例、境内确诊病例（含境外输入关联病例）381例。累计出院1178例，累计死亡一例。尚在院治疗30例。</p><p>截至前天，新增无症状感染者六例，其中境内三例、境外输入三例。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
