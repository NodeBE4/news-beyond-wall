[中国疫苗接种剂次超6亿](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210529-1150269)
------

<div>
<p>中国国家卫健委今天（29日）通报，中国全国冠病疫苗接种超过6亿剂次。</p><p>据新华社报道，卫健委指出，自3月27日超过1亿剂次以来，每亿剂次所需时间为25天、16天、九天、七天、五天，间隔不断缩短，这是“疫苗接种的中国速度”。</p><p>卫健委说，当前多地疫情形势复杂，防控之弦切不可松，接种疫苗后也不可大意，仍需戴好口罩，做好个人防护。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>正在参加中国两院院士大会的中国工程院院士钟南山昨天（28日）受访时指出，到今年年底，中国冠病疫苗的覆盖率能够达到80%，当前疫苗产能仍需加强。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
