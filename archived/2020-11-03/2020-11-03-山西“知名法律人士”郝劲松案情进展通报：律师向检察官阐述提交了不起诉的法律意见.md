[山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松案情进展通报：律师向检察官阐述提交了不起诉的法律意见](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/blog-post_3.html)
------

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：仍被羁押的山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松的两位代理律师蔺其磊律师和黎雄兵律师今天来到定襄县检察院要求视频会见郝劲松未果，只向检察官阐述提交了不起诉的法律意见。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">蔺其磊律师通报说：<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日上午，我和黎雄兵律师来到定襄县检察院，因为疫情的原因会见郝劲松需要在办案单位的视频室中进行，但还必须把会见材料送到五台县看守所，折腾律师来回奔波。但只能预约一周内的会见，今天被告知“本周已约满”无法安排，只向检察官阐述提交了不起诉的法律意见。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日就连续两天遭到户籍地山西省定襄县晋昌镇派出所以“寻衅滋事”为由传唤后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日再遭该派出所传唤。随后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，行政拘留期满被转刑事拘留，控罪为涉嫌寻衅滋事。羁押于忻州市五台县看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，郝劲松被定襄县公安局以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪、诽谤罪、诈骗罪正式逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被移送审查起诉。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松律师生于<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年，山西忻州人。著名人权捍卫者。郝劲松律师自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年开始先后<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次将国家税务总局、北京地铁运营公司、北京铁路局告上法庭，原他因对“打破行业‘霸王条款’起到了一定作用”而入选“<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年构建经济和谐十大受尊崇人物”。郝劲松被先后提名为<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法制新闻人物，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度十大法制人物，并以“维权战士”的身份写入《<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法治蓝皮书》。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因介入推动铁道部春运不涨价案件入选《<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国法治蓝皮书》，并当选<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国十大消费维权人物，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年因介入陕西华南虎案件和杨佳案被《南方人物周刊》评选为“中国魅力人物——公义之魅”。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，介入上海“钓鱼执法事件”。后成立山西定襄县劲松法务咨询中心。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
