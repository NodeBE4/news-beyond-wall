[港十一未经批准集结案大部分被告将认罪](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210517-1147364)
------

<div>
<p>香港法院今天（17日）将开庭审理壹传媒创办人黎智英等10人被控在前年10月1日组织及参与未经批准集结的案件，审讯预计将长达10天。一些被告披露，大部分被告将认罪。</p><p>综合香港01和香港电台报道，这10名被告为民阵召集人陈皓桓、黎智英、李卓人、何秀兰、梁国雄、何俊仁、杨森、单仲偕、蔡耀昌和吴文远。</p><p>被告之一吴文远称，大部分被告将认罪。蔡耀昌昨天则说，他已透过律师向法庭认罪，并将坦然面对任何情况。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>何俊仁也证实大部分被告会认罪，原因是此案没有太多法律观点可争拗，并预计今天很有可能会被即时还柙，自言已做好准备。</p><p>陈皓桓在开庭前也说，自己会认罪，并相信自己有可能会被还押。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
