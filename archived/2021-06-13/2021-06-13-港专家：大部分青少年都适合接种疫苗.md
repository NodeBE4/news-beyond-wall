[港专家：大部分青少年都适合接种疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210613-1155709)
------

<div>
<p>香港12岁以上的青少年明起可以接种复必泰疫苗。香港新冠疫苗临床事件评估专家委员会共同召集人孔繁毅表示，除有药物敏感，大部分青少年都适合接种疫苗。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，孔繁毅说，若青年对两款或以上药物过敏，则需要先咨询专家。他也提醒，青少年在接种疫苗后应多休息，切勿进行剧烈运动。</p><p>孔繁毅又指，外国有数据显示，每100万中有16名青少年接种复必泰疫苗后出现心肌炎，大部分人情况轻微，属于短暂反应，一般在休息一星期后复原近九成，并强调当局会加强监察情况，即时向市民公布相关个案。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
