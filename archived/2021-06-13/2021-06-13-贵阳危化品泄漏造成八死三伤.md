[贵阳危化品泄漏造成八死三伤](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210612-1155290)
------

<div>
<p>贵州贵阳经济技术开发区周六（12日）凌晨发生车辆在卸载危化品甲酸甲酯时泄漏的事故，造成八死三伤，当地村民惊醒后逃离现场，还有人称一家四口遇难。</p><p>据极目新闻报道，事发在周六凌晨零时12分，贵阳市公安局经开分局接到报警称，经开区有人在卸载甲酸甲酯时晕倒。</p><p>当地一名阮姓村民受访时说，他和家人凌晨2时许在熟睡中被一阵刺鼻的气味惊醒，起来后发现公安、消防等部门已在村里处置化学品泄漏，他赶紧和家人撤离了现场，但还是感到身体不舒服，呼吸不畅。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他也透露，事发地点是一家化工贸易公司在村里租赁的一个仓库，里面用来存放该公司的化学原料，占地数百平方米，租赁已有几年时间。仓库离他家只有数百米远。目前全村村民已经全部撤离，事发点周围数公里已全部隔离。</p><p>另一村民陈女士则说，她家有四口人遇难，事情正在调解中。</p><p>相关部门凌晨5时在现场监测显示，周边环境空气质量正常。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
