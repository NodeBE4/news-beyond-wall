[发表侮辱袁隆平言论 两人被拘](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210523-1148809)
------

<div>
<p>中国有两人在网上发表侮辱“杂交水稻之父”袁隆平，被采取刑事强制措施。</p><p>据《新京报》报道，“杂交水稻之父”、中国工程院院士、“共和国勋章”获得者袁隆平22日逝世。天津警方通报，一男子在朋友圈发表侮辱性言论。经查，该网民系天津市静海区人李某，目前已对其采取刑事强制措施。</p><p>警方提醒，英雄烈士不容亵渎，对公然侮辱英雄烈士的行为将严厉查处。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另外，“平安北京”微博也通报，在中国人民深切哀悼之时，微信用户“南望山下”在微信群发布多条侮辱袁隆平的言论，造成恶劣影响。警方接举报后，将该人查获。目前，该人已被刑事拘留，案件正在进一步工作中。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
