font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赵广军：维权老兵。户籍地是辽宁省盘锦市兴隆台区；他的出生地是黑龙江省望奎县；他当兵在锦州四十军<span lang="EN-US">81056</span>部队；他原工作单位是<span lang="EN-US">(</span>北京<span lang="EN-US">)</span>中石油长城钻探；他多年住北京上访；他被维稳的责任单位是兴隆台区。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">赵广军走上公民维权之路后，因参与公民维权活动、关注侵犯个案，十数次被关黑监狱，<span lang="EN-US">3</span>次被行政拘留，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>次被刑事拘留，两次被构陷入狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年以来，赵广军多次遭到绑架、软禁，曾经在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月开始的<span lang="EN-US">4</span>个月中，相继<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次被限制人身自由。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，赵广军在北京被盘锦警方抓走以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，关押在盘锦市看守所，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日赵广军被正式批捕，后家人收到了逮捕通知书。赵广军此次被抓捕完全是因言获罪。判决认定的主要犯罪证据是其在<span lang="EN-US">twitter</span>发布和转发的<span lang="EN-US">2400</span>余条的推文，其中<span lang="EN-US">600</span>条是涉颠覆国家政权的言论。赵广军于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US"> 11</span>月<span lang="EN-US"> 27</span>日 被盘锦市兴隆台区法院判处寻衅滋事罪，获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>个月。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
