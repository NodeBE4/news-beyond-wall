[公民记者张展被控寻衅滋事案将于2020年12月28日在上海浦东新区法院开庭审理](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/20201228.html)
------

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉：公民记者张展被控寻衅滋事案将于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日<span lang="EN-US">9:30</span>在上海浦东新区法院第九法庭开庭审理。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张展自<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日左右去到武汉报导当地疫情，一直在微信、推特、<span lang="EN-US">YouTube </span>等社交媒体平台上直播武汉的现场情况，并尽其所能地帮助受疫情影响的人维权。张展在武汉实地写了大量关于武汉疫情的真实报道。张展的真实报道遭中共当局的恼怒，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日在武汉抓捕了张展，押回上海。<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日就被上海市浦东分局刑事拘留，被控罪名是寻衅滋事，后遭批捕、起诉。现羁押在浦东新区看守所。张展在狱中一直绝食抗议，遭强制灌食、约束，身体状况十分虚弱，令人担忧。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张展（女）：<span lang="EN-US">1</span></span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">980</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，陕西省籍人，上海市浦东新区常驻居民，网络异见人士，公民记者、民主公民。硕士毕业后即在上海谋职工作。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因不满中共政府的政治现状，长期在网络平台发布批评“一党专政”、腐败滥权等言论，其做派活跃、内容尖锐而颇具针对性，故而曾多次被上海市警方约谈、传唤及威胁；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年以来，因其在微信等网络平台大量转发香港“反送中”抗议视频及文图资料，尤其是每天自己撰文发声，利用行为艺术等为声援香港举牌，随于<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被上海警方传唤，自此失联，后知其被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，其父因受警方威胁，故对外拒透其具体信息；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，终获释放；据悉，羁押期间，其曾被两次做强制性“精神病鉴定”。此前被羁押于上海市浦东新区看守所。而张展这一次遭抓捕也完全是因言获罪。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
