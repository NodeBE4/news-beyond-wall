[遭强迫失踪近3个月的上海疫苗受害者谭华的母亲华秀珍已获释回家](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/04/3.html)

<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，本网获悉：上海疫苗受害者谭华的妈妈华秀珍<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日遭上海当局强迫失踪后音信全无，后获悉其于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被上海市公安局杨浦分局以“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留，于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日释放。目前已经回到家中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">华秀珍：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上海疫苗受害受害者谭华的母亲。为女儿维权遭当局迫害 退休待遇被剥夺 已遭强迫失踪<span lang="EN-US">7</span>天。华秀珍为女儿维权遭当局迫害，退休待遇被剥夺，目前遭强迫失踪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月初华秀珍得知取消终身养老金且停发两月，第<span lang="EN-US">1</span>时间与街道办反应，街道办同意先给<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月生活费救济。谁想到第<span lang="EN-US">2</span>天改口了，让母女两人去宁波原单位要政策要救济，食宿自理。随后，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日街道办陪同华秀珍外出随后失联。在华秀珍遭强迫失踪前几天，她发文通报说：“因为我为女儿疫苗致残上访遭当局迫害枉法裁判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>，现取消我教师编制的全部退休待遇，仅发<span lang="EN-US">885</span>元低保，还要退回已发的<span lang="EN-US">14</span>万，走投无路请求国际社会援助！”后音信全无。直到今天获知其被“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留。现已释放。</span></p><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
