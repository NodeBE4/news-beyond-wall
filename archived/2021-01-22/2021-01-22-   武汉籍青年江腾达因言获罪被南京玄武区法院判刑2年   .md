mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：湖北武汉籍青年江腾达因言获罪被南京玄武区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">江腾达生于<span lang="EN-US">1994</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，湖北武汉人。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日曾被南京市公安局玄武区分局以非法利用信息网络罪刑事拘留，后取保获释。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，又被南京市公安局玄武区分局以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">江腾达被当局指控在自己创建的<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群内和群友发表了大量“侮辱中华民族、侮辱国家领导人、侮辱先烈、歪曲南京大屠杀历史、抨击共产主义、支持台湾独立等言论”，于 <span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，被南京玄武区法院判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
