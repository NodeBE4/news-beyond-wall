[云南曲靖维权人士戴宗言的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/09/5.html)
------

<div>
<div dir="ltr" style="text-align: left;" trbidi="on"><br /><div class="MsoNormal"></div><a name='more'></a><br /><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉云南曲靖维权人士戴宗言的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">戴宗言：<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日出生，网名“戴克健”，云南省曲靖市人，曲靖市马龙县盛程源建筑有限公司第三工程处经理，“讨债中国行”当事人，数万粉丝拥有者，知名维权网民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年起，曾因承建了云南省马龙县旧县中学廉租房等<span lang="EN-US">7</span>所学校工程建设，涉及项目共<span lang="EN-US">22</span>个，工程共计结算审计价<span lang="EN-US">2069.0925</span>万元，后经多次讨要欠款，当地教育局虽已支付工程款<span lang="EN-US">1738</span>余万元，但仍有<span lang="EN-US">330</span>多万元资金一直拖欠不还；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为讨要学校工程款（含民工工资和材料款），其被迫开始徒步“讨债中国行”，以期获取社会及政府更广泛的关注和支持，遂被当局视为需要维稳的重点对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日至<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，因曾为欠款工友凑钱治病而被迫到昆明医科大学第一附属医院、云南省第一人民医院、云南省中医院等几大医院门前，举横幅投诉并提出卖肾请求，从而引发社会热议和众人同情，但此举却引起云南当局的强烈不满；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，被马龙县警方以“严重扰乱公共场所秩序”为由抓捕，后被以“诋毁政府声誉”罪由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，因曾在网上宣称要进京上访并购买了昆明至北京的机票，又被马龙县警方以“越级上访”为由处行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">3</span>天；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，曾因徽乡网总监王琳琳发维权信息被安徽省颍上县警方因言获罪抓捕，其伸张正义而积极为王琳琳被抓声援呼吁，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，亦遭安徽省颍上县警察跨省抓捕，并与王琳琳、陈明灯等<span lang="EN-US">5</span>名公义维权网民于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年3月15日被颍上县法院以“寻衅滋事“和“传播虚假信息”二罪判刑，其中，王琳琳被重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年。另四人，</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">赵兰忠犯强迫交易罪、寻衅滋事罪，判处有期徒刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年，并处罚金</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">万元；朱士荣犯敲诈勒索罪，判处有期徒刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年，并处罚金</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">万元；陈明灯犯寻衅滋事罪，判处有期徒刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">个月，戴宗言犯寻衅滋事罪判处有期徒刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">个月，缓刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">3</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年。</span><br /><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
