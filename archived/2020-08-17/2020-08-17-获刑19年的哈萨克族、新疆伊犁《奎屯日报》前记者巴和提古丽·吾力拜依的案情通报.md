[获刑19年的哈萨克族、新疆伊犁《奎屯日报》前记者巴和提古丽·吾力拜依的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/08/19.html)
------

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年的哈萨克族、新疆伊犁《奎屯日报》原记者巴和提古丽·吾力拜依的案情，现通报如下：</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">巴和提古丽·吾力拜依：<span lang="EN-US">1977</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，新疆维吾尔自治区伊犁哈萨克自治州奎屯市人，哈萨克族名<span lang="EN-US"> Baktgul Oralbaikizi</span>，穆斯林信徒，前伊犁哈萨克自治州《奎屯日报》记者，中国在押良心犯。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾一直在新疆伊犁哈萨克自治州奎屯日报社任职当记者，后与其兄长及小妹先后前往哈萨克斯坦国创业；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，原西藏自治区党委书记陈全国调任新疆当一把手，因强化要加强对新疆地区的管控，要求工作及生活在海外的维吾尔族、哈萨克族等中国公民（含海外留学生）必须立刻返回中国生活，其随与众哈萨克族同胞纷纷返回新疆；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">回国后最初两年，除去其护照被地方当局没收、出国权利受限外，生活尚能平安顺利，直至<span lang="EN-US">2018 </span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，其突遭伊犁州奎屯市警方莫名抓捕，并送一家政治再教育转化营羁押，自此失联；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，其家人从奎屯市警方处得知，其被伊犁哈萨克自治州奎屯市法院判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">19</span>年，罪名不清，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2037</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月；据悉，其丈夫受牵连屡遭警方恐吓威胁，被要求与妻子尽早离婚，被其断然拒绝。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast;
