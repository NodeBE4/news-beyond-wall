[巴拿马批准紧急使用中国科兴疫苗](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210410-1138375)
------

<div>
<p>巴拿马卫生部昨天批准紧急使用中国科兴公司研制的冠病疫苗，该疫苗已被纳入巴拿马国家防疫接种计划。</p><p>根据新华社报道，巴拿马卫生部在当天发布的一份公告中称，卫生部药品局批准紧急使用中国科兴冠病疫苗，该疫苗需要注射两剂，中间间隔四周。疫苗保存和运输所需温度介于二至八摄氏度之间。</p><p>巴西卫生部药品局局长埃尔维亚·拉奥对媒体表示，中国疫苗的加入是巴拿马在实现群体免疫道路上的“又一个机会”。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>巴拿马冠病疫苗接种开始于1月下旬，目前已总计接种45万剂。</p><p>据巴拿马卫生部前天公布的统计数字，该国每日新增确诊病例数已从1月初的4000例左右降至4月7日的427例。截止7日，巴拿马累计确诊超过35万例，累计死亡6152例。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
