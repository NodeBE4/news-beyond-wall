mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日上午，辩护律师陈进学联系上了谢丰夏（谢文飞）案的主办法官张菊芝（湖南郴州市苏仙区人民法院刑庭），她说谢丰夏本人已聘请了两位律师，陈进学要求其告知两位律师的姓名并出示谢丰夏本人的授权委托书，她拒绝，她明确确认两位律师不是法律援助律师，是谢丰夏本人聘请的</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: &quot;MS Gothic&quot;; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-bidi-font-family: &quot;MS Gothic&quot;;">⋯⋯</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">此前，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，谢丰夏（谢文飞）案的辩护律师陈进学律师前往郴州市苏仙区人民法院阅卷，主办法官张菊芝外出学习不在，其同事收下律师的辩护手续。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，陈进学律师和谢丰夏的另一辩护律师任全牛再次前往郴州市苏仙区人民法院阅卷，张菊芝法官依然不在，其同事也收下了任全牛律师的辩护手续，并称不知张菊芝法官何时学习结束。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，谢文飞的两位辩护律师任全牛和陈进学来到资兴市看守所要求会见，该看守所以需要办案单位同意为由拒绝律师会见。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
