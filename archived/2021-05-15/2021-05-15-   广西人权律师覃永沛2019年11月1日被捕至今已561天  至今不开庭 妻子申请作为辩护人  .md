[广西人权律师覃永沛2019年11月1日被捕至今已561天  至今不开庭 妻子申请作为辩护人](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/05/2019111561.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：广西人权律师覃永沛<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被捕至今已<span lang="EN-US">561</span>天，至今不开庭。覃永沛妻子申请作为辩护人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据覃永沛律师妻子通报：“覃永沛<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被捕至今已有<span lang="EN-US">561</span>天。可是至今依然没有开庭公告。虽然我经常害怕得发抖，失眠，但是我不去做的话，我更怕我自己长期失眠压力太大引发身体各种问题。我<span lang="EN-US">12</span>号给法院邮寄了覃永沛给我的授权委托书，我申请了作为辩护人《刑诉法》赋予的权利，尽管被法院批准的可能性几乎为零……”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">广西人权律师覃永沛是<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被抓捕的。当时，南宁警方十数人闯入原百举鸣律师事务所（现百永鸣法律咨询服务有限公司）办公室，将覃永沛控制并带走，同时覃的办公室被警方查封。随即覃永沛被刑事拘留。后覃永沛被指涉煽动颠覆国家政权罪于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月遭正式逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，覃永沛涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权案已由南宁市公安局侦查结束，移交南宁市检察院审查起诉。覃永沛涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权案<span lang="EN-US"> 2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日移送到了法院。目前羁押在南宁市第一看守所。但迟迟不开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月末，覃永沛所属原律师事务所律师陈家鸿被玉林警方拘留，罪名为“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”。当时覃永沛曾积极作出声援及呼吁放人，目前陈家鸿律师也在羁押未开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">覃永沛是在<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被注销律师牌照。由他主持，曾代理过不少维权案件的“百举鸣律师事务所”被勒令解散后，转为法律咨询公司的形式，继续提供法律服务。覃永沛也连同多名维权律师，成立“中国律师后俱乐部”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
