[台湾新增38冠病死亡病例为历来最高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210605-1152253)
------

<div>
<p>温伟中 台北特派员<br>woonwj@sph.com.sg</p><p>台湾新增38名冠病死亡病例创下历来新高，累积死亡人数达225人。另外，最近暴发半导体封测大厂京元电子客工群聚病例的苗栗，也新增66例本土病例，引起各界关注半导体供应链“断链”和客工病例散播问题 。</p><p>该处传染源是半导体封测大厂京元电子厂的客工群聚，连日来已<span class="gmail_default">有131人</span>确诊，厂商<span class="gmail_default">今天将完成</span><span class="gmail_default">7000名员工的全面</span>快筛检验。苗栗、新竹等各半导体电子厂，都宣布进行快筛检验。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>京元电子厂昨晚宣布七个厂区产线全面停工48小时、进行清洁消毒。京元电的业务是半导体业的最后一里路，客户几乎是全球重量级晶片大厂，全面停工将造成出货恐延迟，预料将冲击全球半导体产业，晶片缺货将更雪上加霜。</p><p>　　</p><p>今天宣布新增511起本土病例，包括53起校正回归，至今累计病例总数达1万零956例，其中1145例境外移入、9758起本土病例。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
