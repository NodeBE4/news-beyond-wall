[广东新增八例冠病本土确诊病例](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210605-1152227)
------

<div>
<p>据广东省卫健委网站消息，6月4日0-24时，全省新增八例本土确诊病例，其中广州报告六例，佛山报告两例；另有本土无症状感染者转确诊病例三例。新增三例本土无症状感染者，广州报告两例，佛山报告一例。</p><p>全省新增境外输入确诊病例三例，其中两例在广州发现，分别来自孟加拉国和印度尼西亚；肇庆发现一例，来自摩洛哥。</p><p>另有境外输入无症状感染者转确诊病例两例，广州报告，分别来自阿曼和菲律宾。新增境外输入无症状感染者10例，广州报告三例，两例来自柬埔寨，一例来自日本；肇庆报告七例，三例来自喀麦隆，两例来自尼日尔，其余两例分别来自贝宁和土耳其。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>截至6月4日24时，全省累计报告新冠肺炎确诊病例2525例（境外输入1047例）。目前在院129例。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
