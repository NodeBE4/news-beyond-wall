[山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松案将于12月10日世界人权日开庭](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/1210.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，本网获悉：山西“知名法律人士”郝劲松被控涉嫌寻衅滋事罪、诽谤罪、诈骗罪案，将于<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日世界人权日在定襄县法院开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日、<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日就连续两天遭到户籍地山西省定襄县晋昌镇派出所以“寻衅滋事”为由传唤后，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日再遭该派出所传唤。随后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，行政拘留期满被转刑事拘留，控罪为涉嫌寻衅滋事。当日，郝劲松被控涉嫌寻衅滋事遭定襄县公安局刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，郝劲松被定襄县公安局正式逮捕。郝劲松的侦查期被延长了两个月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日到期，后案件已经移送检察院审查起诉，涉嫌罪名有三个：寻衅滋事、诽谤和诈骗。目前郝劲松羁押于五台县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">郝劲松生于<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年，山西忻州人。著名人权捍卫者。郝劲松自<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年开始先后<span lang="EN-US">7</span>次将国家税务总局、北京地铁运营公司、北京铁路局告上法庭，原他因对“打破行业‘霸王条款’起到了一定作用”而入选“<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年构建经济和谐十大受尊崇人物”。郝劲松被先后提名为<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法制新闻人物，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年度十大法制人物，并以“维权战士”的身份写入《<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>中国法治蓝皮书》。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因介入推动铁道部春运不涨价案件入选《<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国法治蓝皮书》，并当选<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>中国十大消费维权人物，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年因介入陕西华南虎案件和杨佳案被《南方人物周刊》评选为“中国魅力人物——公义之魅”。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，介入上海“钓鱼执法事件”。后成立山西定襄县劲松法务咨询中心。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，郝劲松向定襄县环保局实名举报当地的诚泰纺织印染公司涉嫌污染空气和水源。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对郝劲松的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
