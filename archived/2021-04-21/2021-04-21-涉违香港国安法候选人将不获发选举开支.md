[涉违香港国安法候选人将不获发选举开支](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210421-1141004)
------

<div>
<p>香港选举事务处更新立法会换届选举申报选举开支的指引，列明候选人如果因涉嫌违反《香港国安法》被调查或刑事起诉，总选举主任将不会向有关候选人支付任何款项，直至调查或法律程序有最终裁决为止。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，如果候选人被裁定牵涉非法的选举活动，将失去收取全部政府付款的资格。</p><p>选举事务处在网页上载由政制及内地事务局制订有关2020立法会选举换届选举申报选举开支的相关政府付款申索指引，与去年8月发出的指引相比，新增有关《香港国安法》的条文，列明如候选人已被中止在换届选举中，获取政府的付款，候选人需立即向政府归还多获批发的款项，任何没有归还的款项，可被视作拖欠政府民事债项处理，当局可根据法例追讨。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
