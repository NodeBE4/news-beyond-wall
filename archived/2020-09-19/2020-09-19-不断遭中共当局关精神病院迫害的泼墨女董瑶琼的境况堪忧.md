<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：不断遭中共当局关精神病院迫害的泼墨女董瑶琼的境况堪忧。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">董瑶琼父亲近日披露了女儿的近况：今年五月董瑶琼在被强关押精神病院近两个月，这次被释放<a name="_Hlk51442948">比之前病情更加严重，痴呆发傻反应迟缓不说，有时候小便失禁都不治换裤，夜晚有时疯狂喊叫，尤其下雨打雷时候疯狂尖叫 不叫任何人靠近，令人十分痛苦。</a>而且董瑶琼有可能再被抓捕精神病院。期望社会关注。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">中共当局一直把精神病院当作“合法”迫害关押异议人士<span lang="EN-US">/</span>维权人士的手段。中共的精神病院实质上是一个公开化以药杀人而不负任何责任恐怖邪恶的杀人集中营。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">董瑶琼：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1989</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，湖南省株洲市攸县桃水镇人，原上海房地产公司中介职员，推特名：<span lang="EN-US">feefeefly</span>，人称“泼墨女孩”，政治犯。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，曾因用<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>在上海市海航大厦前直播其对中共独裁专制的不满，并批评当局对百姓实施洗脑逼迫，随对中共最高领导人习近平的头像泼洒墨汁，进而引起社会广泛热议；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其被上海市警方抓捕，自此失联，其推特账号被人关闭；<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被上海市警方送回湖南省攸县老家，并以疑有“精神病”为由被变相关押于株洲市第三医院，直至<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日其父在株洲市第三医院与女儿见面之后，方知其泼墨行为已被当局定性为“攻击国家领导人”；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，取保释放，并被送到湖南省攸县桃水镇母亲住处；据悉，拘押期间其备受酷刑虐待，父亲董建彪<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日与其相见时，才知其被关押在精神病院时曾被强迫吃药，获释时已完全判若两人，由原先的活泼开朗变成沉默寡言、神情紧张且有些痴呆的样子；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 16px;">株洲三医院</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">关押两个月后出院。董瑶琼出院后比之前病情更加严重，痴呆发傻反应迟缓不说，有时候小便失禁都不治换裤，夜晚有时疯狂喊叫，尤其下雨打雷时候疯狂尖叫不叫任何人靠近，令人十分痛苦。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
