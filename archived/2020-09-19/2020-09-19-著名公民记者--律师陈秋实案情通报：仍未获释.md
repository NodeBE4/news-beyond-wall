<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">19</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：日前有消息指，失踪了</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">7</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">个月的著名公民记者</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">/</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">律师陈秋实仍被监管，暂不被起诉。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾就读于黑龙江大学法学院，<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年毕业后曾担任过餐饮业服务员、演员助理、配音员、警察媒体记者、电视编导及主持人等多种职业角色，<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年，因获取律师执业证书，自此成为执业律师；</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;
