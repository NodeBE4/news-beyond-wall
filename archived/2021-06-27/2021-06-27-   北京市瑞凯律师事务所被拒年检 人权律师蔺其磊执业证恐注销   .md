[北京市瑞凯律师事务所被拒年检 人权律师蔺其磊执业证恐注销](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/06/blog-post_42.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：成立于<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年的北京市瑞凯律师事务所被拒年检，人权律师蔺其磊执业证恐被注销。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据蔺其磊律师消息：<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日以来，北京司法行政部门朝阳区司法局以各种理由拒绝对他所在的瑞凯律师事务所和该所律师进行年度考核，致使无法办理法律业务，经营陷入困境。因不能接受北京司法行政部门的违法要求，<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，瑞凯律师事务所被北京市司法局以“未参加<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年律师事务所年度检查考核”为由注销。蔺其磊猜测，包括他在内的三个合伙人在六个月后极有可能因为“没有律师事务所接收”而被注销律师执业资格。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NtZS-wUcKyQ/YNidiPM21xI/AAAAAAABwuQ/pyX7KNjgBwYvMvbB7oAbHnEVNWtrphfHgCLcBGAsYHQ/s620/photo_2021-06-27_19-34-48.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="350" data-original-width="620" height="226" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-NtZS-wUcKyQ/YNidiPM21xI/AAAAAAABwuQ/pyX7KNjgBwYvMvbB7oAbHnEVNWtrphfHgCLcBGAsYHQ/w400-h226/photo_2021-06-27_19-34-48.jpg" width="400" /></a></div><p></p>
