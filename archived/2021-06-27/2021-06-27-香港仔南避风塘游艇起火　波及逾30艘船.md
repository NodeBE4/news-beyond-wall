[香港仔南避风塘游艇起火　波及逾30艘船](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210627-1161449)
------

<div>
<p>香港仔南避风塘凌晨发生火灾，波及30余艘船，大火焚烧约6小时后救熄，期间共疏散35人。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，消防处分区指挥官于文阳说，接报首先起火的是一艘游艇，到场后发现两艘船在焚烧，动用多座灭火轮水炮扑救，水警轮也加入救火，火警发生后两小时升为三级。</p><p>他说，这次面对的救援困难是海面水道狭窄，加上风势令火蔓延至附近船只，部分船的缆绳已断，船只飘开，导致波及30多艘船，至少10多艘沉没，实际数目有待点算确认。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>水警南分区助理指挥官余嘉伦表示，因应火警，派出四艘水警轮及六艘小艇，将22男13女疏散至水警基地，海事处稍后会清理海上的飘浮物及油污。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
