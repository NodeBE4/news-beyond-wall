<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：山东诗人、独立中文笔会会员鲁扬（本名张桂祺）因言获罪案已于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日在聊城中级法院秘密开庭。鲁扬被控涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪。对于开庭事宜，家属被严密封锁，对此一无所知。鲁扬的合法辩护权遭中共当局的非法剥夺。开庭后判刑结果未知。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">鲁扬，网名“鲁西狂徒”，他是因网络异议言论于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被警方带走，次日被抄家；后下落不明近两个星期，后获悉其于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被聊城市公安局以“涉嫌颠覆国家政权罪”宣布刑事拘留，关押于聊城市看守所。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">鲁扬：现年<span lang="EN-US">49</span>岁，本名张桂祺，网名“鲁西狂徒”，山东省莘县人，著名诗人。<span lang="EN-US">1994</span>年开始诗歌文学创作活动，创办“扬子宛诗社”，并主编出版《扬子宛诗刊》。<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年创办“中国当代诗歌论坛”，出版主编《中国当代诗歌》刊物。二年后“中国当代诗论坛”被封。期间开始在《星星》、《诗选刊》等国内著名诗歌刊物上发表诗歌。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2004</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年创办“中国自由文化论坛”，因颇有影响而被迫多次关停，后移入外网“自由中国”网站，最后又移入博讯网站。<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年因自由写作被迫于聊城外国学校辞职，后妻子工作也受到牵连，一起离开山东，南方谋生，此间由诗歌创作转入文化自由写作，大量作品发表于国内各大网站，并被海外多家网站转载。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，为支持入狱的本会前会长刘晓波而入独立中文笔会。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年受“茉莉花运动”影响，再次失业并遭遣返回山东老家。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年因举牌声援批评毛泽东遭解职的山东广播电视台特约评论员邓相超教授，在邓教授所在的济南建筑大学遭到暴徒殴打，受到中外媒体关注，同时也成为有关部门重点监控对象。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
