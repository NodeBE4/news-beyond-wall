[大理救火直升机坠海 四人遇难](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210511-1145883)
------

<div>
<p>云南大理一架救火直升机昨日意外坠入洱海，四名机组人员遇难。</p><p>据中新社报道，记者11日从云南省大理市人民政府新闻办公室获悉：昨日在大理市湾桥镇大沙坝山森林火灾清守火场、增湿作业中，一架直升机坠入洱海。</p><p>初步核实，坠入洱海的直升机内共有四名机组人员。先后搜救出两名机组人员，经现场及医院全力抢救无效死亡。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>后通过连夜搜救，11日凌晨2时55分许找到另外两名机组人员，已无生命体征。目前，各项善后工作正全面展开。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
