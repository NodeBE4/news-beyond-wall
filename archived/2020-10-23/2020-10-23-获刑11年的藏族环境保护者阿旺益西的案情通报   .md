[获刑11年的藏族环境保护者阿旺益西的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/10/11.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年的藏族环境保护者阿旺益西的案情，现通报如下：</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">阿旺益西：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1980</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，西藏自治区拉萨市林周县人，拉萨市林周县旁多乡村民，生态环境保护者，藏族人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p></o:p></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年始，中共西藏当局即在西藏拉萨所辖的林周县旁多乡境内对各种矿产资源进行初步勘探，并为防止当地藏人进行抗议，曾特意宣布将为无业藏人提供工作机会等，但在正式开工采矿，且顺利修建<span lang="EN-US">“</span>旁多水利枢纽工程<span lang="EN-US">”</span>之后，当局不仅不兑现之前的承诺，反而随意开采矿区，使得当地生态环境遭受严重破坏（如）；迫于无耐，当地藏人遂多次向相关部门提出反映与投诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，西藏当局为确保水利工程及采矿等计划顺利实行，开始强令旁多乡<span lang="EN-US">119</span>户（近<span lang="EN-US">700</span>名）村民集体搬迁；因当地藏民拒不搬迁，虽数次向主管部门提出投诉请求，均毫无果效，随于<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月被迫开展和平示威活动，试图阻拦破坏生态环境的采矿项目，但又遭到中共军警的残酷镇压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">很快，阿旺益西与另<span lang="EN-US">5</span>名村民维权代表均被拉萨市警方野蛮抓捕，自此失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，在被超期拘押长达<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年多之久后，其一行<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人<span lang="EN-US">“</span>请愿游行案<span lang="EN-US">”</span>在拉萨市中级法院开庭受理，均被以<span lang="EN-US">“</span>试图分裂西藏，并组织领导了以保护环境为名的抗议活动<span lang="EN-US">”</span>罪由分别重判<span lang="EN-US">8-12</span>年不等刑期，其中，阿旺益西被秘密判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月。目前在西藏自治区第一监狱（原拉萨市扎其监狱）服刑。</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><o:p></o:p></span></p>
