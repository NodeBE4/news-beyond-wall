[山东省聊城临清维权人士杨明玉遭刑事拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/04/blog-post_53.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：山东省聊城临清维权人士杨明玉于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪遭山东临清市警方刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杨明玉</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日出生，山东省聊城地区临清巿人，上访维权人士，零八宪章签署者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年因发生工伤事故而遇不公，自此加入上访群体，也因此曾被判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，被处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年，被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">11</span>次。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">由于在上访过程中屡遭不公与不幸，深感只有追求民主宪政才是唯一的出路，随毅然投入到推进中国民主宪政的事业中，尤其是<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年，曾多次积极参与举牌要求官员财产公示、呼吁公民基本权利等活动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">以上行为最终招致当局的不满与忌恨，随于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被深圳市警方秘密拘捕；<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被深圳巿警方以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式逮捕；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，其案在深圳市中级法院开庭审判；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，终被深圳市中级法院以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。一直被羁押于深圳市福田区看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日刑满释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
