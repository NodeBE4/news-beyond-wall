[韩正与林郑会面：香港要组织好今明两年系列选举](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210423-1141659)
------

<div>
<p>中国副总理韩正今天（23日）在广州分别与前来参加粤港澳大湾区建设领导小组会议的香港特首林郑月娥和澳门特首贺一诚会面时强调，香港要组织好今明两年系列选举。</p><p>据新华社报道，韩正相信，全国人大关于改革香港选举制度的决定和全国人大常委会对香港《基本法》附件一和附件二的有关修订，为落实“爱国者治港”原则、确保香港长治久安提供了坚实的法律保障，香港特区要抓紧做好本地立法相关工作。</p><p>韩正强调，香港和澳门要继续把疫情防控，作为推动经济复苏和民生改善的首要工作，中央政府将一如既往给予全力支持。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他也呼吁香港和澳门发挥好港澳独特优势，共同推进大湾区建设，保持香港、澳门的长期繁荣稳定，促进“一国两制”成功实践不断向前发展。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
