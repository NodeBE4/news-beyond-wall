[广州增13例无症状感染者 越秀区全员检测](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210530-1150403)
------

<div>
<p>中国广东5月29日新增13例本土无症状感染者，越秀区定于5月30日至6月1日开展全员核酸检测。</p><p>据广东卫健委公布的消息，13例本土无症状感染者中，广州报告12例，佛山报告一例。</p><p>全省新增境外输入确诊病例一例，广州报告，来自喀麦隆。新增境外输入无症状感染者五例，广州报告四例，两例来自柬埔寨，其余两例分别来自孟加拉国和喀麦隆；深圳报告一例，来自柬埔寨。新增出院四例。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另据中国央视报道，广州市越秀区通告，定于5月30日至6月1日对全区常住人员分街道分社区就地开展全员核酸检测。</p><p>通告提醒居民预约采样的时间，做到“应检尽检”。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
