[巴基斯坦部长称中国大使在爆炸案中逃过一劫](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210422-1141273)
------

<div>
<p>巴基斯坦一家酒店的停车场昨天（21日）发生爆炸案，中国驻巴基斯坦大使农融当时正好下榻在这家酒店，但巴基斯坦部长艾哈迈德（Sheikh Rashid Ahmad）证实，农融在爆炸发生时不在这家酒店，因此逃过一劫。</p><p>据路透社报道，这起爆炸案发生在巴基斯坦西南部城市奎达，目前已造成四人死亡，11人受伤。巴基斯坦塔利班宣称对这起爆炸案负责。</p><p>奎达为俾路支省的首府。省内政部长兰戈（Ziaullah Lango）也证实，农融平安无事。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>俾路支省政府发言人沙瓦尼（Liaquat Shahwani）在推文中说，农融当天早些时候在奎达与省首席部长卡马尔（Jam Kamal）会面。</p><p>兰戈称，农融将在今天结束在奎达的访问行程。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
