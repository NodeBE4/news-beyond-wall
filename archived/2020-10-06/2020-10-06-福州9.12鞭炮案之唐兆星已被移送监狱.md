<div>

mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一串鞭炮抓捕三十多人，重判四人，合计刑期<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>个月<span lang="EN-US">,</span>数个家庭被迫一次次陷入困境，父母妻儿骨肉分离。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">一个月后大多数人被以各种方式释放。林应强、何宗旺、唐兆星和林兰英四人被以＂寻衅滋事＂口袋罪刑拘、批捕（何宗旺另案处理）。</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，林兰英刑满出狱。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日<span lang="EN-US">,</span>福州市中级人民法院对何宗旺编造、故意传播虚假信息罪、寻衅滋事罪以“原判认定部分事实不清、证据不足”作出二审裁定：撤销原判，发回闽清法院重新审理。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
