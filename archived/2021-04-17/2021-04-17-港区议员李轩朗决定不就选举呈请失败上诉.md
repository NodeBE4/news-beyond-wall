[港区议员李轩朗决定不就选举呈请失败上诉](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210417-1140104)
------

<div>
<p>前年香港区议会选举在九龙城土瓜湾南选区当选的李轩朗，早前因涉嫌选举舞弊被裁定失去区议会议席，李轩朗今日表明不会就选举呈请败诉上诉。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，高等法院上月25日裁定李轩朗于选举呈请中败诉，并宣告他在2019年区议会选举中并非妥为当选。根据法例，法庭裁决暂缓生效，直至上诉至终审法院的14工作天限期届满为止。</p><p>李轩朗今日表示，经研判法律观点和社会形势后，决定放弃将案件上诉至终审法院。他解释，上诉程序所费不菲，有关资源应更直接用于支援其他“受压迫者”身上。而即使上诉成功，他亦难以保证可在区议员宣誓法实施后可以“过关”留任。因此，他已通知律师团队决定不拟提出上诉。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>随着上诉限期即将结束，下周一（19日）将会是李轩朗出任九龙城区议会议员的最后一日。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
