[薇娅、贾玲、柳青等入选商界木兰十大年度人物](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210417-1140077)
------

<div>
<p>由《中国企业家》杂志社评选的“2021年度中国具有影响力的30位商界木兰”榜单前天揭晓，共有10名企业家获得“商界木兰十大年度人物”，包括滴滴出行总裁柳青、大碗娱乐的贾玲以及网红电商主播薇娅。</p><p>进入“商界木兰十大年度人物”榜单的女企业家还有新希望的刘畅、立讯精密的王来春、华策影视的赵依芳、小红书的瞿芳、字节跳动的张楠、B站的李旎、联想集团的乔健。诺亚财富汪静波等20位企业家则获得“年度商界木兰”。</p><p>商界木兰榜单至今已连续发布13届，184位商界女性先后登上榜单，其中格力电器董明珠、阿里巴巴彭蕾、依文集团夏华、当当网俞渝、龙湖集团吴亚军、知名导演王潮歌、万丰集团陈爱莲、慈铭集团韩小红等16位商界木兰获得“钻石木兰”称号。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
