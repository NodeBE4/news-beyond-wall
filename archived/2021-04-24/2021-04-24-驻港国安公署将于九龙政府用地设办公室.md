[驻港国安公署将于九龙政府用地设办公室](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210423-1141676)
------

<div>
<p>香港特区政府将九龙区大角咀一幅政府用地，批予中国中央政府驻港国家安全公署，以设立永久办公室及附属设施。</p><p>据香港电台今天（23日）报道，这块地总面积约1万1500平方米。港府强调，根据《香港国安法》，特区政府有关部门须在驻港国家安全公署依《香港国安法》履行职责时，提供必要的便利和配合。</p><p>港府也说，驻港国家安全公署将依据《香港国安法》及相关香港适用法规，推展本项建筑工程并承担所需费用。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
