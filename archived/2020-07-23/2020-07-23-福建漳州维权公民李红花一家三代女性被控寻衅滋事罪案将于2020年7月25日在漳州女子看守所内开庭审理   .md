font-size: 12.0pt;">李红花多年不停的维权，先后<span lang="EN-US">16</span>次被龙海市公安局行政拘留，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年被劳教<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月因参加福州一看门口放鞭炮迎接维权公民出狱，被刑拘一个月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
