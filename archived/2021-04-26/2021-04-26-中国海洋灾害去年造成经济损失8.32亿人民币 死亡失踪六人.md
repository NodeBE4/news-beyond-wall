[中国海洋灾害去年造成经济损失8.32亿人民币 死亡失踪六人](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210426-1142329)
------

<div>
<p>中国自然资源部海洋预警监测司26日发布的《中国海洋灾害公报》指出，2020年，中国各类海洋灾害共造成直接经济损失8.32亿元（人民币，下同，1.70亿新元），死亡或失踪六人，与近十年相比均为最低值。</p><p>据中新社报道，数据显示，2020年，中国海洋灾害以风暴潮和海浪灾害为主，海冰、赤潮、绿潮等灾害也有不同程度发生。</p><p>同期，中国沿海共发生风暴潮过程14次，七次造成灾害，直接经济损失8.1亿元；共发现赤潮31次，累计面积1748平方千米。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
