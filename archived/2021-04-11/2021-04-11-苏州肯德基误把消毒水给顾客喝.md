[苏州肯德基误把消毒水给顾客喝](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210411-1138572)
------

<div>
<p>中国江苏一家肯德基餐厅误把消毒水给了一名女顾客饮用，导致女子有胃痉挛。餐厅随后向女子提供1万5000元（人民币，下同，3075新元）的误工费补偿。</p><p>据江苏省广播电视总台旗下的“荔枝新闻客户端”昨天（10日）报道，这名女子小杜因口渴，在江苏苏州向肯德基要了杯水，喝下后发现她喝的是消毒水。</p><p>小杜饮用后嗓子和胃部出现严重不适，经医生诊断患有急性糜烂性胃炎和胃痉挛。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>肯德基餐厅最初承担1500元的治疗费用，并再赔偿1000元，同时要求小杜签一份保密协议。不过，在执法部门介入调查后，肯德基决定，将在看好病的基础上给予小杜误工费补偿。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
