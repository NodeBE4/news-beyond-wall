[合肥市维权人士裴莉9月去北京上访  两个多月后竟遭警方传唤后拘留5天](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/9-5.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：合肥市维权人士裴莉<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月去北京上访，两个多月后遭警方传唤威胁。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日早晨安徽省合肥市维权人士裴莉被合肥市公安局包河分局常青派出所以涉嫌寻衅滋事传唤。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">上午包河公安分局治安大队警察刁鹏给裴莉做笔时她才知道是因为<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日去国家信访局反应问题，没想到是中国国际服务贸易交易会全球服务贸易峰会在北京举行。北京开峰会国家信访局开门接待，访民去反映问题就是没有错，但是在安徽省合肥市包河区政府领导眼里认为有错。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刁鹏警察在给裴莉做笔录时还恶狠狠的对她说再去北京试试看。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2006</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年裴莉家里没人合法别墅被包河区政府以违法建设为由非法强行拆除，其多次向包河区政府要依法按法律程序给违法建设认定书和行政处罚决定书，包河区政府就是不给。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">11</span>：<span lang="EN-US">00</span>点原包河区区委书记胡启生，原政法委书记张良才，区信访局胡局长，常青街道领导，律师等在包河区信访办接待了其，当时区委书记胡启生答应给其认定书，其再去找胡启生书记，没想到他调到巢湖市任市委书记，难怪他爽快答应！</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日包河区信访局认定裴莉为<span lang="EN-US">"</span>无理访<span lang="EN-US">"</span>没有法律依据，没有人找裴莉当面谈过，更没有给其书面文字。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">难道其的合理要求合肥市包河区政府认为是无理要求吗？聂树斌的冤案法院也是判决裁定了，如不是他母亲不断去北京<span lang="EN-US">"</span>无理访<span lang="EN-US">"</span>他的冤案能清白吗？多年来其无家可归，法院不管，政府不管。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">9</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日裴莉去国家信访局反映问题<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日事隔快两个月合肥市公安局包河分局，根据《中华人民共和国治安管理处罚法》第二十六条第四项之规定，对裴莉予以行政拘留五日。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">裴莉没想到去国家信访局正常逐级反映问题无端的被合肥市公安局包河分局非法限制人身自由拘留五日。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
