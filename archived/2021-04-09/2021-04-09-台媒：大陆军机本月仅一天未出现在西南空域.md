[台媒：大陆军机本月仅一天未出现在西南空域](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210409-1138196)
------

<div>
<p>据台湾媒体报道，大陆军机本月密集出现在台湾西南空域，从本月至今仅一天未出现。据统计，今年已经进入台湾周围空域达83天。</p><p>根据《自由时报》报道，根据广播及航迹记录，大陆军机于今天12时32分、12时52分、13时31、40、43分五度进入台湾西南空域，高度为6200至7100公尺。</p><p>台湾空军广播驱离内容为“中华民国空军广播，位于台湾东南空域高度6200公尺的中共军机注意！你已进入我ADIZ，影响我飞航安全，立即回转脱离！”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>“台湾西南空域”粉丝专页记录，本月累计出现在台湾周围空域八天，本月广播驱离纪录共计29次。今年累计进入台湾周围空域83天，广播驱离纪录共计239次。  </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
