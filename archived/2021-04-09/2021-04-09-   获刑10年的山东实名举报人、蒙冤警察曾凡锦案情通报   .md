<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年的山东实名举报人、蒙冤警察曾凡锦案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾凡锦：<span lang="EN-US">1955</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日出生，山东省菏泽市人，山东省郓城县育才路东段<span lang="EN-US">123</span>号公民，山东省郓城县公安局原经侦大队指导员，实名举报人，蒙冤警察。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，曾因和<span lang="EN-US">35</span>名警察联名向上级有关部门实名举报时任山东省郓城县县委书记刘国生严重贪污腐败，滥用职权操控黑白两道，强拆强占农民土地及打死打伤、肆意拘留农民等问题，并将此事发于网上，随遭被举报贪官刘国生等人严厉打压与疯狂报复；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其被不明身份数十人突然夜闯家中非法抄家和绑架，并送菏泽市东郊一处郓城县检察院私设审讯点关押，三天后被莫名刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">关押期间，该检察院副院长亲自带队，为让其在已捏造好的莫须有罪名签字认罪，采取连续<span lang="EN-US">50</span>天“车轮战”式审讯、长时间坐老虎凳和不给吃喝等多种酷刑对其折磨，甚至不惜另抓<span lang="EN-US">8</span>名与其关系不错的同事警察，以威逼利诱，酷刑折磨、逼疯逼残等方式强逼他们指控和捏造其犯罪事实；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，其被转正式逮捕，后被菏泽市郓城县法院被以“贪污罪”枉法冤判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后因其不服上诉，经菏泽市中级法院二审裁决，减为<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日获释后，其继续实名举报，并就刘国生、郑建生等官员的贪腐问题和其在狱中曾遭长期刑讯逼供，导致生命几经濒危等事实依法提起申诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，刘国生因贪腐落马锒铛入狱，同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日终被山东省泰安市中级法院以“受贿罪”从轻判刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年；然而其早先为举报贪官而提起的申诉控告，却因山东当局官官相护，始终毫无结果；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">为求公道正义，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，其遂网上发布《抬棺决斗宣言》，并于同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月抬棺向山东司法机关发起生死决斗和法理辩论；此举一出，立时引来社会热议，但也旋即引起山东当局的极度不悦与强力打击；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，再度被当地警方抓捕，后被移交至山东省巨野县看守所刑拘；一个月后，被巨野县检察院以涉嫌“敲诈勒索罪”予以批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，被山东省菏泽市巨野县法院以同罪名一审枉法重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年；二审维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2027</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
