<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的湖北民主人士陈剑雄刑满获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈剑雄的朋友袁兵今天通报说：“刚刚接到陈剑雄姐姐电话：陈剑雄<span lang="EN-US">x</span>服刑三年半，今日刑满获释，已被户籍地所在维稳人员于今日上午<span lang="EN-US">08</span>：<span lang="EN-US">30</span>在湖北咸宁监狱接走。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp; </span></span>陈剑雄是我的兄弟与“同案”，嫉恶如仇，拒不妥协，是位意志坚强，性格刚烈的民主维权人士，为此多次被刑拘，并被判刑二次，此次出狱必被当局严加看管，行动多有不便。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 105.0pt;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈剑雄（陈进新）：因十九大维稳，民主人士袁兵、陈剑雄（陈进新）、梁一鸣<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日遭湖北赤壁警方抓捕后，均遭刑事拘留，都被关押在赤壁看守所。<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日下午，袁兵、陈剑雄、梁一鸣被警方分别带走，警方还出动警力数人前往两人家中进行搜查。三人被抓捕的原因与前两天他们前往公安局及信访局讨要说法有关。而三人信访问题产生则是因为不堪当地维稳系统最近严重骚扰所致。袁兵和陈剑雄都是广东民主活动的积极参与者。因为积极参与街头民主活动，袁兵和陈剑雄等人均曾被当局判刑，两人于去年年底和今年上半年袁兵陆续释放出来。后<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，梁一鸣取保获释。而袁兵、陈剑雄被批准逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，陈剑雄被判<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal" style="tab-stops: 105.0pt;"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
