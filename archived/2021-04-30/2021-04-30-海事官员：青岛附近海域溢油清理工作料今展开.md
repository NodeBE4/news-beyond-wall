[海事官员：青岛附近海域溢油清理工作料今展开](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210430-1143307)
------

<div>
<p>一位海事官员透露，负责在青岛附近清理船只意外溢油的船舶和设备已准备就绪，一旦天气情况有所好转，清理工作预计今天（30日）较迟时候就能开始。</p><p>据路透社报道，这名中国海事局官员说，被调派到事发海域清理溢油的船只数量已增加至21艘。</p><p>新华社早前报道，一艘巴拿马籍杂货船“义海”轮（SEA JUSTICE）本月27日早上9时许与利比里亚籍锚泊油船“交响乐”轮（A SYMPHONY）在青岛朝连岛东南海域相撞，导致油船的一个货舱受损，海面也发现溢油。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>山东海事局随后通报，现场指挥部调派了12艘清污船参与溢油处置。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
