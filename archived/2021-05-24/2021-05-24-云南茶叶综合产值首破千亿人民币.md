[云南茶叶综合产值首破千亿人民币](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210524-1149069)
------

<div>
<p>中国云南省茶叶综合产值去年首次突破千亿元（约2000万新元）。</p><p>据新华社报道，记者从云南省打造世界一流“绿色食品牌”工作领导小组办公室获悉，云南省2020年茶叶综合产值达到1001.4亿元，同比增长7%。</p><p>云南省农业农村厅绿色食品处负责人介绍，在茶产业绿色发展支持资金奖补政策下，全省有机茶园认证面积81.8万亩，有机成品茶认证产量8.4万吨，有机获证产品1014个。同时，茶农来自茶产业的收入增长20.6%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>在茶产业基地建设方面，云南省按照程序，2020年认定了345个“绿色食品牌”茶产业基地，总面积129.3万亩。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
