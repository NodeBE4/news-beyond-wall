[中国已有280家新三板公司完成股票发行融资](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210516-1147208)
------

<div>
<p>中国今年以来有280家挂牌公司在新三板完成股票发行融资，累计发行股票金额超过107亿元（人民币，下同，22.14亿新元）。</p><p>据新华社报道，中国全国中小企业股份转让系统（新三板）披露数据，截至本周，今年以来有280家挂牌公司在新三板完成股票发行融资。</p><p>在交易方式方面，本周（5月10日至14日）新三板挂牌公司成交20.49亿元，其中精选层成交5.40亿元。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>在指数方面，几大指数本周表现平稳，截至14日收盘，三板成指报985.90点，三板做市指数报1107.13点。</p><p>截至5月14日，新三板挂牌公司总计7540家。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
