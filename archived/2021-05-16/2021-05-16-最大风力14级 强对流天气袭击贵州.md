[最大风力14级 强对流天气袭击贵州](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210516-1147175)
------

<div>
<p>中国贵州省开阳、镇远等区域将出现冰雹大风等强对流天气，最高风速达到14级。</p><p>据中国央视报道，贵州省气象台监测统计，5月15日7时至16日7时，贵州省开阳、镇远等12县域内出现冰雹，最大冰雹直径为瓮安县珠藏镇25毫米；此外，93个观测站出现八级以上大风，其中开阳宅吉风速达44.5m/s（14级）。</p><p>毕节市、贵阳市、黔东南、黔南、铜仁、遵义6个市（州）17个县（区）开展高炮（火箭）防雹作业。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>岑巩县龙田出现大暴雨（103.4毫米），镇远、余庆、施秉、石阡、思南、印江、玉屏、岑巩、湄潭、碧江、松桃、江口、三穗、万山等16县域内的104个观测站出现暴雨，大雨有294个观测站，大雨以上降水主要集中在铜仁市、黔东南州北部、遵义市南部。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
