<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年的青海藏族词曲创作人康卓才丹的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">康卓才丹</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：青海省黄南藏族自治州泽库县多禾茂乡多禾日村人，藏族词曲创作人，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，曾因参与了<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年青海省黄南州藏人学生的“挺藏语”示威游行活动，随被黄南州泽库县警方予以拘捕，直至<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月才被获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">之后其虽被准予获释返家，但因当局恐其再次组织、参与学生抗议活动，遂对其仍进行严密监控，截至<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年，其身份证件均被没收，欲返回学校亦被禁止，且其在社会上的所有活动都几乎被限；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，又因创作了一首颂扬西藏精神领袖达赖喇嘛的歌曲，并由当地歌手才果广为演唱，之后其两人即遭黄南州泽库县警方抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后很快被当地泽库县检察院以涉嫌“煽动分裂国家”等罪予以批捕，并被关押于泽库县看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，其案在青海省黄南州泽库县法院进行庭审，终被一审以“煽动分裂国家罪” 、“对外泄露国家机密罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前疑似在黄南藏族自治州泽库县监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><br /><p></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
