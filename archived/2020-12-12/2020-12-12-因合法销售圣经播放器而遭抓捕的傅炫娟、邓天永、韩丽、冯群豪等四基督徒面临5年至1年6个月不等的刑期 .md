[因合法销售圣经播放器而遭抓捕的傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等四基督徒面临5年至1年6个月不等的刑期](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/516.html)

<div>
<p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">12</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">12</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">7</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，中共当局抓捕了合法销售圣经播放器的傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等四基督徒，目前面临</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">5</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年至</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">个月不等的刑期。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">这样一家合法企业因销售圣经播放器，竟然导致傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等四基督徒被逮捕。中共当局于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，再深圳宝安区西乡街渔村横林大厦<span lang="EN-US">701A</span>单元以所谓非法经营罪将傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪抓走。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，四人因涉嫌“非法经营”被深圳市宝安分局公安局拘留；经宝安区检察院批准，被认定“非法经营”，<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被深圳市公安局宝安分局逮捕。目前傅炫娟、邓天永、韩丽、冯群豪等<span lang="EN-US">4</span>位基督徒都被关押在深圳九围看守所。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">宝安区检察院起诉书认定，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，深圳市生命树科技发展有限公司涉嫌非法经营金额为<span lang="EN-US">3661847.84</span>元。检察院认定，傅炫娟，邓天永，冯群豪，韩丽违反了《中华人民共和国刑法》第二百二十五条，应追究其非法经营的刑事责任，建议判处傅炫娟有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，邓天永<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年有期徒刑和罚款，冯群豪<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年有期徒刑和罚款，韩丽<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月有期徒刑和罚款。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">他们家人和律师都被威胁，不得透露案情。目前未有判决消息。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
