<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年、现狱中病危的残疾维权人士姜志林的案情及个人简介，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">姜志林：四川省南充市籍人，新疆维吾尔自治区博乐市达勒特镇哈日莫墩后村村民，网络活跃人士，微信常用网名“南国林子”，被微博和微信平台网友誉为“网络红人”，残疾访民、维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">随其母亲从四川省南充市迁移至新疆维吾尔自治区博乐市地区生活，自此一直从事农业生产；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2001</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年左右，曾因承包田权属、宅基地确权，以及乡村振兴战略中当地政府未能执行自治区新水饮标准、城乡道路建设不落实，而导致严重影响其及当地村民生活，屡次投诉均无果效，遂被迫走上逐级上访之路；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因上访要求一直得不到解决，反而屡遭当地执法者威胁、殴打与拘留，故使其维权抗争意识更加坚定；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因长期关注维权事务，并陪同新疆各地的上访人士奔走，呼吁群体维权与集体抗争，故而触碰到新疆当局既得利益者的敏感神经，随被新疆警方一再追打报复、拦截关押、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2015</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，曾因在网上为安徽的“安妮失学事件”和为郭飞雄、许志永、张海涛等在押良心犯呼吁发声，而被博乐县警方以涉嫌“编造、故意传播虚假、恐怖信息罪”刑拘，一个多月后被取保候审，但又遭当地警方及村干部多次软禁和贴身跟踪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，曾因欲去乌鲁木齐市与朋友见面，而被所在村支部书记在火车站拦截后再次软禁；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，又因要到乌鲁木齐市看病，而被多名不明男子再次阻挠，后被达勒特镇警方带走拘留；同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，被博乐市警方以涉嫌“敲诈勒索罪”、“诬陷罪”刑事拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被博乐市检察院以同罪名予以批捕，并被关押于博乐市看守所，其母受牵连亦于同期遭软禁失联；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，其案在新疆博乐市法院秘密庭审，终被一审以“敲诈勒索罪”、“诬陷罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年；二审维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其原本四肢肌肉萎缩，还患有肺结核等多种疾病，现狱中已出现病危，失去说话能力，体重仅有<span lang="EN-US">30</span>余公斤；其母要求了解儿子病情，遭狱方粗暴拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
