[元宵节凌晨，江苏省如东县发生了强拆](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/02/blog-post_27.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，正是元宵节，按中国人习俗本是全家喜庆团聚的日子。有关法律法规也规定节假日不得强拆民房。但为了与民争利，当地官员根本没有将这些规定放在眼里。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">元宵节凌晨<span lang="EN-US">3</span>点，在人们熟睡时，一伙身份不明的人将江苏如东县掘港镇北场村一组<span lang="EN-US">700</span>平方米的房屋强行拆除，现场一片狼藉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">该房屋原是供销社，因单位改制卖给了五位职工，即高志中，刘清培，罗修文，周玄，王娟所共有。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，强拆受害人王娟等向<span lang="EN-US">110</span>报警，民警到现场后未开具受理单，扬长而去。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">江苏省如东县已经发生了多起强拆事件而引起行政诉讼，因法院未按《行政诉讼法》第六十六规定对违法强拆的行政机关负责人进行处置，导致违法者有恃无恐，强拆还在继续，甚至于元宵节也不停止。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
</div>
