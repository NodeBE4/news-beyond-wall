[网民称辽宁舰和美军舰昨现身台东部海域](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210427-1142523)
------

<div>
<p>有台湾网民声称，中国大陆辽宁舰和两艘美国军舰现身台湾东部海域。</p><p>面簿专页“新‧二七部队 军事杂谈”今早（27日）发布卫星照片，声称昨天在台湾东部海域、日本石垣岛以南200公里处，除了有美国伯克级驱逐舰外，还有美国提康德罗加级巡洋舰，而辽宁号航舰在提康德罗加级的右舷，与伯克级共同在辽宁号间保持航行距离近10公里。</p><p>专页随后再发文称，美军今早出动RC-135W电侦机，自冲绳起飞后，经台湾东部转往巴士海峡，进入西南海域，同时也有KC-135T加油机伴飞支援，过程中也和海面上的友舰，共同监控航行接近宫古水道的辽宁号航舰。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
