[获刑10年的新疆哈萨克族企业家哈德力别克·努尔兰的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/10.html)

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">11</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">14</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">10</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年的新疆哈萨克族企业家哈德力别克·努尔兰的案情，现通报如下：</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">哈德力别克·努尔兰：<span lang="EN-US">1990</span>年代出生，新疆维吾尔自治区阿尔泰地区青河县查干郭勒乡人，哈萨克斯坦族，穆斯林，乌鲁木齐市酷巴巴网络科技有限公司创办人，企业家，中国在押良心犯。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期曾就读于东北大学，<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年毕业；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年，曾前往新疆乌鲁木齐市创业，后创办酷巴巴网络科技有限公司；</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因宗教信仰而做礼拜（即穆斯林进行敬拜时的祷告方式），被阿尔泰地区青河县当局很快知晓，随被青河县警方带走拘留，并被送进“再教育营”进行洗脑式教育，自此失联；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因其在“再教育营”拒绝接受洗脑教育，遂被警方以“从事非法宗教活动”为罪由转为正式逮捕；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，被新疆维吾尔自治区阿尔泰地区青河县法院重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月；</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，其被判刑前后，家属均未收到任何法律文书，家属欲为其聘请辩护律师遭拒，探视权被剥夺，至今不让会面。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在新疆克拉玛依市监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
