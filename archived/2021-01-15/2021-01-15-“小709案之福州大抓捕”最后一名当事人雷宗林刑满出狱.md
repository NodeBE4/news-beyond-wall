[“小709案之福州大抓捕”最后一名当事人雷宗林刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/01/709.html)
------

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日，本网获悉：今天（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日）上午十点半许，在<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年“小<span lang="EN-US">709</span>案之福州大抓捕”中，遭福州当局重判三年十个月的残障人士雷宗林刑满出狱，雷是该案中最后获释的当事人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">雷宗林父母、妹妹及友人早早到达福建闽江监狱迎接雷，见到雷后双方情绪激动，一家人相拥而泣。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">长年的牢狱生活使得雷宗林营养不良，本就瘦弱的身体显得更加单薄，健康状况堪忧，但今天精神还不错。他感谢亲友和公民社会对他的关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">雷宗林简介：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">福州市晋安区宦溪人，双下肢残疾，其身残志坚，先后从事过养殖、种植、修车等行业谋生。<span lang="EN-US"> 1998</span>年一场台风摧毁旧宅，重建家园时重新修建的房屋被地方官员以违建为由拆毁。<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年到京申冤遭构陷被判一年刑期，在这期间一千多平米猪场也被恶霸强占，生活无所依托。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">雷宗林在维权过程中觉醒，参加福州维权人士“每周一聚”抱团取暖，公民互助活动。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月赴南昌江西高院声援“乐平冤案”和福建老乡屠夫。多次在福州马尾等地街头举牌为屠夫、望云和尚、王宇律师等公义人士鼓呼；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月与福州一众维权人士集体举牌纪念“六四”。雷在长期的维权中曾遭遇当局惨无人道的“活埋”，强制关入精神病院等迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本案回顾：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月杭州<span lang="EN-US">G20</span>峰会期间，风声鹤唳，福州当局对各类民间维权活动的打压达到高潮，雷宗林与林炳兴、江智安、严兴声、廖俊、张秀屏、熊凤莲、吴宏福、贺清敏、林依妹、蒋碧秀、林善忠、卓道明、罗红梅、石立琴共十五位福州维权人士相继被捕，雷宗林被另案处理，由林洪楠律师代理。雷被判有期徒刑三年十个月，是该案刑期最长者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
