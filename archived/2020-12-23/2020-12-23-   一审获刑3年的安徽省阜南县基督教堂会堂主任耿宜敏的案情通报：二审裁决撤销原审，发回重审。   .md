[一审获刑3年的安徽省阜南县基督教堂会堂主任耿宜敏的案情通报：二审裁决撤销原审，发回重审。](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/3.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，其案在安徽省阜南县法院开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被该法院一审以“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；其不服，提起上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;
