<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年的新疆维吾尔族商人买吾拉尼•努尔买买提的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">买吾拉尼•努尔买买提：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">新疆维吾尔自治区博尔塔拉蒙古自治州博乐市人，原新疆中国电信员工，互联网技术个体工商户，博乐市易信通信设施安装有限公司创办人，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早期曾为新疆中国电信公司一职员，后离职开创属于自己的小本生意，主要负责互联网相关技术的运营；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，曾在新疆博乐市成立和注册“博乐市易信通信设施安装有限公司”，收益较丰；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，随着中共政府对新疆地区各少数民族（尤以维吾尔族、哈萨克斯坦族、回族等居多）要求民族独立自治、要求信仰自由等要求的极端管控和打击，新疆出现大规模维吾尔族及其他少数民族穆斯林被关进“再教育营”现象，在此氛围下，其亦被博尔塔拉蒙古自治州博乐市警方莫名抓捕，自此失踪；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后经其家人长期搜寻，知其先被博乐市公安局送至一个“再教育营”关押，但因表现不佳，拒不配合洗脑教育，又被以涉嫌“分裂国家”罪由予以逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月，被博乐市地方法院一审以“分裂国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年；其不服上诉；后经博尔塔拉蒙古自治州中级法院二审裁决：驳回上诉，维持原判；据悉，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日已被移交到监狱服刑；中国政府曾就其被抓判刑向联合国工作小组提供信息称，其之前无业，其家人表示愤慨；刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2026</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
