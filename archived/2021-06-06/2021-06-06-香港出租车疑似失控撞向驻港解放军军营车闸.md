[香港出租车疑似失控撞向驻港解放军军营车闸](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210606-1152569)
------

<div>
<p>香港一辆出租车昨日疑似失控，撞向驻港解放军军营的车闸，涉事出租车男司机被带入军营协助调查。有多名军装及便衣探员到军营调查，男司机其后交由香港警方带返警署调查。</p><p>据香港01报道，事发周六（5日）傍晚6时许，一辆出租车疑似失控，撞向驻港解放军军营的车闸，车闸被撞后屈曲凹陷，出租车车头轻微损毁，部份零件飞脱。</p><p>消息人士透露，有人于事发后曾向解放军声称自己被人追杀，希望解放军人员保护，警方正调查事件成因及动机，以及司机事发时精神状态等，案件由香港中区刑事调查队跟进。至凌晨零时，涉事出租车由拖车拖走，有穿着全套保护衣物的人员在现场喷洒消毒剂消毒。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
