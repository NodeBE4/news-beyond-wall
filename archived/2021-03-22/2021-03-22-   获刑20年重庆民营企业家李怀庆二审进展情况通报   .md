[获刑20年重庆民营企业家李怀庆二审进展情况通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年重庆民营企业家李怀庆二审进展情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据李怀庆妻子包艳今天通报：“<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，我和王宇律师一起来到保定市人民检察院，向邓灰林、谭平专案组提起控告！李怀庆案，在重庆警方构陷、迫害李怀庆的大量新证据已提交最高法院及相关部门的情况下，二审理应依法公开开庭审理。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">重庆民营企业家、亿万富翁李怀庆，因在微信上说几句话，就被当局以煽动颠覆国家政权，及诈骗、敲诈勒索、非法拘禁等四项罪名羁押。在被关押近三年之后，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日在重庆市第一中级法院中法庭一审宣判，获刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年，令人震惊。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李怀庆：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1966</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年出生，重庆市人，复转军人，“随手公益”的志愿者，重庆富华典当公司老板，民营企业家，民主异见人士，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因个性急公好义，曾长期关注和资助弱势群体；曾因默默多年资助大凉山地区贫困儿童及家境困难的昔日战友，积极参与“大爱清尘”活动救助尘肺病工友，并为此先后捐资近<span lang="EN-US">30</span>万元，不计回报，而深受弱势群体爱戴欢迎；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，被重庆市警方突然以“涉黑” 罪名刑拘，其妻儿及公司职员等亦受牵连被抓，在被莫名其妙数时小时审讯、恐吓、威逼之后才得释放；同年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，其名下公司、妻子包艳及与此案完全无关的其胞姊资产、账户（包括现金及房产等，总值上亿元）均被重庆警方以“打黑”名义冻结；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后其罪名被重庆市检察院更换为涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”，指控其在<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月—<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，曾七度在微信“以造谣、诽谤等方式煽动颠覆国家政权”，包括转发“红四方面军在四川杀人百万”文章，又转发过有“道义抗争”、“暴力革命”等字眼的录音，属于违法行为；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其罪名遂于“涉黑罪”变成“颠覆国家政权罪”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在其长达近两年的羁押过程中，其律师及其家属均未收到过任何有关其涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”的逮捕通知书或文件说明；后李怀庆涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪案于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日在重庆市第一中级法院开庭审理，未宣判。目前李怀庆被羁押于重庆江北区看守所。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日重庆民营企业家李怀庆被庆市第一中级法院以煽动颠覆国家政权罪、诈骗罪、敲诈勒索罪、非法拘禁罪四项罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">20</span>年徒刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
