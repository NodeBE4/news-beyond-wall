[福建维权人士陆祚钰被寻衅滋事案一审二次开庭定于2021年1月4日](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/01/202114.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：福建屏南维权人士陆祚钰被寻衅滋事案一审二次公开开庭，将于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点至<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日共二天，在福建柘荣法院第一法庭举行，本案由纪中久律师代理。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据家属介绍陆祚钰本次还是以远程视频的方式参与庭审，家属对此种方式表达了强烈不满。原因是：本案第一次开庭时陆听不清庭上各方发言，无法查看、质证公诉人出示的证据。另外，该案第二天，陆又以远程视频方式参与了在宁德法院的一场行政庭，法官不管陆是否听到法庭上的声音，强行推进庭审程序。陆祚钰被逼的脱衣抗议，法官才宣布休庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">值得一提的是，本次再次开庭，是在辩护人、家属持续的抗争和公民社会的高度关注下才获准。我们敦请柘荣县法院依法保障当事人参与诉讼的自辩权及各项权利。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，刚行政拘留获释的陆祚钰再次被屏南县城关派出所传唤，随后即被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留<span lang="EN-US">, </span>羁押于宁德市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，陆被屏南检察院以寻衅滋事罪批准逮捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
