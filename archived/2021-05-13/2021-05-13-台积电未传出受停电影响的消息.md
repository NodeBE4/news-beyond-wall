[台积电未传出受停电影响的消息](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210513-1146500)
------

<div>
<p>台湾多区大停电，外界关注台湾半导体厂商是否有受到影响。晶圆代工厂联电回应时说，公司目前没有任何影响，仅出现稍微压降的情况，台积电则没有传出受停电影响的消息。</p><p>综合台湾《联合报》和中时新闻网报道，负责电力供应的台湾电力公司网站目前无法连上，新竹科学园区回报暂无影响，南科高雄园区有停电状况，目前约40多家小型厂商受波及。</p><p>兴达发电厂位于台湾高雄市永安区与茄萣区交界处，总装置容量位于台湾第三位，仅次于台中发电厂及大潭发电厂。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>南科管理局公布，南科40多家使用22.8kV馈线的小型厂商受波及，至于台积电、联电、群创等大厂都是使用161kV馈线，目前未传出受影响的消息。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
