[澳洲富邑葡萄酒料获利下降 受中国关税影响](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210513-1146460)
------

<div>
<p>澳大利亚富邑葡萄酒集团(TWE)周四预计全年营业利润将下降，归因于中国对澳洲葡萄酒征收高关税。</p><p>据路透社报道，该公司预计，2021年获利(不计自生和再生资产税(SGARA)、利息和税项)介于4.95亿澳元（5.10亿新元）至5.15亿澳元之间，低于上年的5.335亿澳元。</p><p>但高盛分析师说，这一区间高于市场预期。该股今日在大盘低迷的情况下仍上涨约3%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>疫情抑制了餐馆高档葡萄酒的消费，加上中国的高关税政策，中国市场实际上已对澳洲葡萄酒关闭，导致富邑经历了18个月的困难期。</p><p>富邑周四在投资者日表示，长期而言目标是获利平均录得较高的个位数增幅。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
