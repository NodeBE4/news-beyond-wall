<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天（<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日）下午我依旧在临沭县刑警队院内视频会见室通过网络视频会见丁家喜。这第五次会见时长一百分钟，就辩护意见等进行沟通。得知看守所伙食条件有所改善：有时早餐玉米粥换为大米粥，午餐晚餐的菜由白菜换为小黑菜或土豆。菜中的肉量明显多些，甚至还见到鸡蛋花了。但看守所以检查整顿为由暂停了食品销售。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天会见还得知：丁家喜脱发明显；双目的不适有所改善；因烟台期间长期受酷刑、目前临沭看守所放风时间短等原因，丁家喜腿部仍浮肿；因看守所长期仅供冷水，老丁手部疑似关节炎；还患过敏性肠炎，基本上是隔一天一次腹泻。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both; text-align: center;"><a href="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ikQbzmv81-M/YG8rwktZf-I/AAAAAAABvUk/AqLNyH8ZFl0FvxgiXAJJJUX_IJtR39zzQCLcBGAsYHQ/s420/13749265815254.jpg" imageanchor="1" style="margin-left: 1em; margin-right: 1em;"><img border="0" data-original-height="415" data-original-width="420" src="https://1.bp.blogspot.com/-ikQbzmv81-M/YG8rwktZf-I/AAAAAAABvUk/AqLNyH8ZFl0FvxgiXAJJJUX_IJtR39zzQCLcBGAsYHQ/s320/13749265815254.jpg" width="320" /></a></div><p></p>
