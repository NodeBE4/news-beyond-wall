[在各界的声援下  六四天网创始人黄琦被羁押四年后 终获与身患绝症的老母亲视频会见](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/09/blog-post_79.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">17</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉：在各界的声援下，六四天网创始人黄琦被羁押四年后，终获与身患绝症的老母亲视频会见。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">今天，被判<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年重刑的六四天网创办人，目前在巴中监狱服刑的黄琦，在被囚禁近<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年后，今天上午终于获准通过视频与老母亲蒲文清于黄琦会见。身患重症的蒲文清，已被医生宣告无药可治，今日和儿子见面后，又让她重燃活下去的希望。她说：“我还是想力争活下去，等他回来。”</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">蒲文清在与朋友的微信中透露了这一消息，并表示目前黄琦在医疗及生活上都能得到保障。外界对此仍感担忧，为了能够了解到黄琦在监狱里的真实现状，我们希望当局能够立即安排母子见面。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>黄琦：</b><span lang="EN-US">1963</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，四川省内江市人，网名难博，“六四天网”网站创办人、中国天网人权事务中心负责人，独立中文笔会荣誉会员，著名维权公民、人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因长期关注社会热点话题，<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年曾成立“天网寻人事务所”，<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年曾设立“六四天网”网站，并惯于在网上发表时政敏感性文章，从而累遭当局严控、传唤、刑拘和判刑；曾因言获罪，先后三次遭到警方的拘捕，且前两次都被判刑；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2000</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，曾因国外异议人士在其网页上发表有关<span lang="EN-US">1989</span>年天安门事件的文章，而被四川成都警方刑拘，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日被正式逮捕，<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日被四川省成都市中级法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；狱中曾遭法警、狱警和其他罪犯多次毒打，导致脑积水，脑萎缩，双侧脑室扩大，导水管变窄等严重疾病；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，曾改组六四天网为中国天网人权事务中心，同年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日荣获第六届中国人权青年奖；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，曾因发表有关四川地震灾难问政府的报导（即网上发文揭露汶川地震出现的“豆腐渣”工程），而第二次被成都市警方刑拘，<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被正式逮捕；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日被成都市武侯区法院以“非法持有国家秘密罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，<span lang="EN-US"> 2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日刑满释放；然而此时因在狱中长期遭受煎熬和非人待遇，已罹患肾脏绝症；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，因对一份内部文件内容曝光，其第三次被四川省绵阳市警方从成都家中带走刑拘，自此失联，同年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日被以“为境外非法泄露国家机密罪”正式逮捕，并被关押在绵阳看守所；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月初，其案被移交四川绵阳市法院审理；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，被四川省绵阳市法院一审以“故意泄露国家秘密罪”、“为境外非法提供国家秘密罪”共判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">12</span>年；其不服上诉，后经绵阳市中级人民法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2028</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
