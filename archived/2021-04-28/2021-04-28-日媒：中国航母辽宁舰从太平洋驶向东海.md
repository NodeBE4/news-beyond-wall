[日媒：中国航母辽宁舰从太平洋驶向东海](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142745)
------

<div>
<p>日本防卫省统合幕僚监部昨天（27日）发布消息称，发现中国海军航母“辽宁舰”等共六艘舰艇从太平洋经冲绳本岛与宫古岛之间海域北上，驶向了东海。这六艘舰艇本月初也经相同海域南下。防卫省正在东海警惕中方的动向。</p><p>据共同社报道，防卫省介绍，发现六艘舰艇26日晚在宫古岛以南约160公里处朝着东北方向行驶。海上自卫队的护卫舰和P-3C巡逻机进行了监视。</p><p>日本防卫省说，27日上午，一架预警直升机从辽宁舰上起飞。由于该机飞近直至距离尖阁诸岛（中国称钓鱼岛）大正岛周边日本领空东北方向50至100公里附近，航空自卫队战机紧急升空应对。“中方未进入日本领海，也没有针对自卫队舰艇和飞机的危险行为。”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
