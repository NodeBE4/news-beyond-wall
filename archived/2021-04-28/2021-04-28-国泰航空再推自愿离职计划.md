[国泰航空再推自愿离职计划](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142819)
------

<div>
<p>香港国泰航空向员工发布内部信件，指疫情下营运继续面对挑战，再向机师、机舱服务员及机场员工等推出自愿离职计划。</p><p>据《信报》报道，国泰在信中称，由于全球各国持续实施严格的旅游限制及检疫政策，国泰只能维持基本航班，近期虽已稍为放宽限制，但公司的经营环境持续严峻，预期在短至中期内营运不会有明显改善，基于回应部分员工的要求，目前是时机推出自愿离职计划。</p><p>接受方案的机组人员将获得相当于三个月的工资补偿，或以全年底薪三分二计算，以六个月底薪为上限，另外也会发放补贴，5月12日截止申请。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>国泰表示，会继续谨慎管理业务，在管理成本和保留现金间取得平衡。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
