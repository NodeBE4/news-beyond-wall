[港交所上季收益近60亿港元创季度新高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142787)
------

<div>
<p>香港交易所首季收入及其他收益为59.56亿港元（10.18亿新元），创季度新高，同比升49%，交易及结算费增加以及集体投资计划的公平值收益1.59亿元，反映全球股票及固定收益市场的广泛表现。</p><p>据香港电台报道，期内，现货收入及其他收益18.2亿元，同比升56.6%。当中交易费收入12.79亿元，同比升88%，主要是股本证券产品日均成交额急升91%带动；联交所上市费收入则减少600万元至2.27亿元。</p><p>股本证券及金融衍生产品收入10.11亿元，同比升12.6%；商品分部收入3.61亿元，同比跌10%；交易后业务收入22.48亿元，同比升28%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另外，上季营运支出11.02亿元，同比升9%，主要是雇员费用及资讯技术成本增加。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
