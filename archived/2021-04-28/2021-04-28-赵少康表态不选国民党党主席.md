[赵少康表态不选国民党党主席](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142827)
------

<div>
<p>台湾在野的国民党今天（27日）举行中常会，中广董事长赵少康以《和平、奋斗、救台湾》为题演讲时表态，称无论修不修改党主席选举办法，他都不会参选国民党主席</p><p>据《自由时报》报道，赵少康日前宣布有意角逐国民党主席、2024总统大选，并曾多次炮轰国民党中常会，引发中常委串连要求赵到中常会报告，赵也欣然接受。</p><p>赵少康说，原本他是抱着“救党救国的心情”要参选国民党主席，但既然党章的选举办法规定一定要一年党龄才能选国民党主席，虽然事实上他党龄很久，已经20几年，但他希望大家不必麻烦，不论修不修选举办法，他都不会参选国民党主席。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
