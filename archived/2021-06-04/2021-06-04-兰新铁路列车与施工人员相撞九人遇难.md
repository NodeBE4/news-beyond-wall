[兰新铁路列车与施工人员相撞九人遇难](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210604-1151856)
------

<div>
<p>一列从乌鲁木齐开往杭州方向的K596次列车今天凌晨5时19分许在兰新线k361＋401公里处与铁路施工人员相撞，经初步核查，现场施工人员九人遇难。</p><p>据中国央视新闻报道，事故发生时，兰州铁路局集团武威工务段金昌线路车间的50多名工作人员正在线路上施工。施工的线路是兰新铁路往新疆方向的下行线路，而发生人员伤亡的是上行线路。</p><p>据了解，事发地点是一个半径500米左右的转弯路线，事发时是施工快要结束的时候。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>事发线路目前已恢复通行，事故原因正在进一步调查。　　</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
