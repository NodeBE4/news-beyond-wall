[因言获罪被判刑1年3个月的安徽维权人士刘瑞虎的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，本网因言获罪被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月的安徽维权人士刘瑞虎的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘瑞虎：<span lang="EN-US">1965</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日出生，安徽省阜阳市阜南县人，阜阳市阜南县洪河桥镇曾沃村郑湾东队<span lang="EN-US">28</span>号村民，重型货车司机，网络活跃人士，维权公民，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因维权多次在各级信访局上访（其中仅安徽省信访局就去过至少<span lang="EN-US">5</span>次），但均毫无效果；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月，曾因中央巡视组在合肥市进行接访，其将申诉材料提交给巡视组，以期得到合理公正的处理结果；却很快被阜南县公安局防暴队及洪河桥镇派出所所长李成抓走，并以在微信里散发对阜南县不利信息为由被拘留<span lang="EN-US">4</span>次；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因在北京向中央信访局递交申诉材料，而被阜南县警方四处追寻，欲用软禁方式阻止其再去北京信访维权<span lang="EN-US">;</span>因其及时逃脱，被迫长期滞留异地，有家不能归；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因在网上维权，如发表《村干部的保护伞》、《求救信：基层干部黑暗势力强大，以权栽赃陷害维权人》等文章，<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，遂被浙江省里安市公安局马屿派出所警方从里安市京豪酒店以”辱骂和抹黑县领导“为由抓捕，并临时关押在里安市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，又被移交至安徽省阜南县警方，旋即被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，被阜南县检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其寻滋案被阜南县检察院向阜南县法院提起公诉；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被安徽省阜南县法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；其不服判决，已上诉至安徽省阜南市中级法院；据悉，其妻受连累，在家遭现已辞职的村书记欧打致伤。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于阜南县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
