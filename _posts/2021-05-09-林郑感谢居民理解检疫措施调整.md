[林郑感谢居民理解检疫措施调整](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210509-1145403)
------

<div>
<p>香港行政长官林郑月娥昨晚在社交专页表示，安排七栋发现有变种病毒株确诊个案的大厦约2200名住客到检疫中心接受21天检疫，是由于当时无法估计病毒传播有多广。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，林郑月娥说，一旦有多位居民被感染，社区爆发会一发不可收拾，市民恢复常态生活的盼望也会落空。</p><p>林郑月娥表示，经过卫生防护中心和其他单位的努力，特别是追踪确诊者的接触者和多层强制检测，当局有足够信心调整检疫措施，有关居民可回家自行监察并继续接受病毒检测。她感谢每一位居民的理解和配合。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
