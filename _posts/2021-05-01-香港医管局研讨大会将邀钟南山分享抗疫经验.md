[香港医管局研讨大会将邀钟南山分享抗疫经验](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210501-1143541)
------

<div>
<p>香港医院管理局研讨大会下周一及周二将在湾仔会展举行，医管局行政总裁高拔陞表示，会邀请中国工程院士钟南山、政府专家顾问袁国勇及许树昌等专家分享抗疫经验。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，高拔陞说，公立医院病人数目近年不断增加，现时照顾约200万名长期病患者，预计20年后会增至300万，加上病人年轻化，对公营医疗服务构成重大负担。</p><p>高拔陞还表示，局方将研究建造智慧医院，在手术室中应用5G通讯及射频识别技术，提升医疗服务及保障病人的安全，同时减轻前线医护人员的工作压力。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
