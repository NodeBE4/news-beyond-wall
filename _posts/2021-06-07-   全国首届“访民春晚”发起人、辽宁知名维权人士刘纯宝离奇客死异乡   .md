[全国首届“访民春晚”发起人、辽宁知名维权人士刘纯宝离奇客死异乡](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/06/blog-post_0.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日，本网获悉：全国第一届“访民春晚”发起人、辽宁知名维权人士刘纯宝，因<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月底在北京遭到驱逐，辗转躲避到甘肃平凉华亭县的访友家暂住，不料，于昨天晚上<span lang="EN-US">20</span>点突然离世，客死异乡。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">辽宁省营口市鲅鱼圈区芦屯镇的维权人士刘纯宝，因房屋拆迁未获得依法补偿安置而上访，还遭到绑架、枪击，造成刘纯宝十指九残、及右腿功能残疾等。在长达二十多年的维权过程中，刘纯宝经常在北京为全国各地访民办案整理材料，并为春节期间流落京城的访民，发起温暖人心的“访民春晚”，为推动中国的普世价值做出了很大奉献。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据黑龙江维权人士马波说，刘纯宝于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日晚，从浙江省随姓方的访民来北京，当天晚上刘纯宝在开阳桥附近的汉庭酒店住宿，被汉庭洒店辖区警察查房盘问，折腾一宿没能休息好，于次日（<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日）换了一小旅馆，又被辖区派出所警察伙同刘纯宝户籍地警察多次谈话，地方警要强制把刘纯宝带回当地，刘纯宝被迫躲避他乡。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘纯宝于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>号去了银川堂弟家呆了一天，于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>号离开，继而去甘肃省平凉市华亭县，为了不被“大数据”监控，暂住在曾经帮助过的访友罗巧玲家中。到<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">8</span>点<span lang="EN-US">46</span>分，刘纯宝还给马波发信息，称妹妹和儿子都给转了路费，说准备<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日回家。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">18</span>点<span lang="EN-US">10</span>分，罗巧玲突然给马波打电话，大声哭着说刘纯宝不行了，在抢救！当天晚<span lang="EN-US">19</span>点<span lang="EN-US">57</span>分，刘纯宝的遗体被送到殡仪馆。刘纯宝还没能走上回家的路上，人生永远定格<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><span style="mso-spacerun: yes;">&nbsp;
