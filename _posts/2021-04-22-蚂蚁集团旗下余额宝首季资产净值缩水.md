[蚂蚁集团旗下余额宝首季资产净值缩水](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210422-1141383)
------

<div>
<p>天弘基金管理有限公司称，其管理的蚂蚁集团旗下货币市场基金余额宝在2021年第一季缩水。</p><p>根据路透社引述余额宝今天公布的第一季财报，截至第一季末，余额宝的资产净值为9724亿元人民币(1990.9亿新元)，较上年同期的1.19万亿元减少18.3%。</p><p>蚂蚁集团本月被中国金融监管部门再次约谈，主要是要求蚂蚁对标整改方案深入有效整改。整改方案要求蚂蚁纠正支付业务不正当竞争行为，断开支付宝与“花呗”“借呗”等其他金融产品的不当连接，主动压降余额宝余额等。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>余额宝的净资产额下降可能也与股市上涨有关，中国蓝筹股指数在2月中旬触及纪录高位，削弱了货币市场基金的吸引力，并促使更多散户投资者将资金从余额宝转到股市。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
