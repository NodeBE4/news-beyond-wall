[张建宗称有迹象显示香港疫情反扑](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210418-1140278)
------

<div>
<p>香港政务司司长张建宗称，正当香港疫情持续缓和之际，昨日确认首宗社区变种病毒个案，令人忧心。有迹象显示疫情反扑，来势汹汹。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，张建宗在网志表示，特区政府高度关注，严阵以待，全力追踪源头及紧密接触者，通过强制检疫、强制检测及限制与检测宣告等措施，尽早截断病毒传播链。</p><p>张建宗说，疫苗接种计划将扩大至涵盖16至29岁人士，此组别可由本月23日上午9时起预约。接种复必泰疫苗的最低年龄为16岁，接种科兴疫苗的最低年龄则为18岁。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他表示，香港要真正走出疫情，始终要靠普及有效的疫苗接种，亦有其重要性、必要性及迫切性。他呼吁市民及早积极接种疫苗，尽早建立免疫屏障。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
