[成都发生持刀伤人事件 四人受伤](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210418-1140293)
------

<div>
<p>中国成都市发生一起持刀伤人事件。一名31岁男子因生活琐事与母亲发生争吵，后持刀伤害他人。</p><p>据界面新闻报道，成都市公安局天府新区分局 4月17日接报警称：当地发生一起持刀伤人事件。</p><p>民警立即赶至现场，将犯罪嫌疑人杨某抓获。杨某性别男，31岁，是四川资阳市人。受伤的四名群众有三名男性一名女性，经医院救治后暂无生命危险。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>经审查，杨某交代了因生活琐事与母亲发生争吵，后持刀伤害他人的犯罪事实。目前，案件正在进一步调查处理中。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
