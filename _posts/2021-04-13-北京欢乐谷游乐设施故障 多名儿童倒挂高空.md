[北京欢乐谷游乐设施故障 多名儿童倒挂高空](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210412-1138875)
------

<div>
<p>中国北京欢乐谷海洋馆内一高空游乐设施发生故障，多名儿童被倒挂在高空足足一分钟。</p><p>据北京日报报道，北京欢乐谷海洋馆内一高空游乐设施“飞鲨战队”4月10日晚发生故障。现场视频显示，事故发生后多名儿童被倒挂在高空足足一分钟，孩子在上面大哭。游乐场员工紧急维修设备，悬停大约一分钟后恢复正常。</p><p>北京欢乐谷 4月12日发布情况说明，“飞鲨战队”项目因设备安全保障自动感应预警，导致项目临时停运40秒。问题出现后，北京欢乐谷立即对现场游客进行了安抚，但由此影响了游客的品质体验，北京欢乐谷深表歉意。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>经查，原因为编码器误报警。经检测处理、反复试运行后，正常恢复开放。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
