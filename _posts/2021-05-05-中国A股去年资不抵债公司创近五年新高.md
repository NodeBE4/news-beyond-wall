[中国A股去年资不抵债公司创近五年新高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210504-1144276)
------

<div>
<p>中国媒体统计发现，去年中国A股中资不抵债上市公司数量创近五年新高，农林牧渔行业带息债务规模和净债务规模均翻倍增长。</p><p>据证券时报报道，该媒体旗下新媒体数据宝发布《A股资产负债率排行榜》和《A股带息债务排行榜》显示，2020年A股非金融上市公司累计负债45.71万亿元（人民币，下同，9.43万亿新元），同比增长10.19%。</p><p>资产负债率中位数为40.93%，较去年降低0.51个百分点。404家非金融上市公司资产负债率超过70%，38家公司资不抵债，资不抵债公司数量创近五年新高。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>分行业来看，除金融外的26个申万一级行业中，13个行业的资产负债率中位数同比上升。房地产、建筑装饰、公用事业、商业贸易、采掘和钢铁这些重资产行业的资产负债率最高，中位数均超过50%；国防军工、计算机、食品饮料和医药生物行业的资产负债率水平最低，中位数不超过35%。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
