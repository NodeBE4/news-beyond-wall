[谢文飞代理律师为代理事宜与郴州苏仙区法院交涉遭拒](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/blog-post_70.html)

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，本网获悉：谢文飞代理律师为代理事宜与郴州苏仙区法院交涉遭拒。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">陈进学和任全牛两位律师今天上午在郴州苏仙区法院见到谢丰夏<span lang="EN-US">(</span>谢文飞<span lang="EN-US">)</span>案承办法官张菊芝，她仍坚持谢文飞本人已委托两个律师，并咬死说不是法律援助律师，但拒绝告知两个律师姓名和电话，谢丰夏的哥哥致电，张菊芝也拒绝告知，完全耍流氓。律师随后去苏仙区检察院和郴州市检察院投诉控告，计划下午去资兴市看守所要求会见。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢文飞：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">本名谢丰夏。<span lang="EN-US"> 1977</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日出生，湖南省郴州市桂阳县人，网名“谢文飞”、“南街谢文飞”、“南街谢文飞<span lang="EN-US">@njxwf </span>”，人权捍卫者，南方街头运动践行者，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">近年来，因经常活跃于广州街头，勇于为推动中国的民主宪政事业积极发声和献身，故而遭到广东当局的多次打压和拘留；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因前往围观广州公民利瓦伊国申请“六四游行”被拘提起诉讼案开庭，而被广东省佛山市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，关押于佛山市禅诚区看守所，事由是“纪念林昭和曹顺利”；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因与民主维权公民王福磊、王默等在广州举牌，发起寻找民主异见人士张圣雨的活动，被石围派出所警方带走，且被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，曾因穿着黑衫在广州市街头拉横幅声援香港民主抗争、“真普选”，被广州警方从增城区新塘镇住所再次带走，并以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留；审讯期间，遭酷刑折磨，被广州市越秀看守所警方实施“定镣”处罚，时间长达<span lang="EN-US">104</span>个小时；<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月，被广州市检察院更换罪名以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”正式批捕，并被转关至广州市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其案正式开庭受审；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被广州市中级法院以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，刑满释放。此前在广东省河源监狱服刑。<span lang="EN-US"> <o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢文飞于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日晚上在苏仙区租房处被郴州警方强制带走，次日苏仙区公安分局以涉嫌寻衅滋事为由将其刑事拘留。后谢文飞托获释出来的监友传话，其遭指控罪由仅是网络言论，并明确拒绝接受官方指派律师，希望更换辩护律师。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">谢文飞案于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日被办案单位苏仙区公安分局移送该院审查起诉，该院已决定以谢文飞“涉嫌寻衅滋事”罪名下周向苏仙区法院起诉。谢文飞现羁押在郴州市下辖的资兴市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
