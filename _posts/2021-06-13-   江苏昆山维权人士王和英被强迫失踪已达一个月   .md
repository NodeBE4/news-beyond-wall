[江苏昆山维权人士王和英被强迫失踪已达一个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/06/blog-post_13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：江苏昆山维权人士王和英<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日被强迫失踪 ，至今已满一个月。王和英原定于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日举行“昆山驿站”茶庄开业典礼，各地维权朋友纷纷前来道贺，当地警方将此活动定性为非法集会。据知情人士透露，王和英被关押于江苏和昆山国保掌控的宾馆中，每天被谈话做笔录，面对各种要挟和恐吓。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王和英，</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">现年<span lang="EN-US">48</span>岁，出生山东，现居江苏昆山县，“无地、无房、无家产” “三无人员”。因被政府强占耕地、强拆住房，得不到公正，<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年开始维权。<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>年和<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年曾多次协助许志永、腾彪等人在北京进行打击“黑监狱”活动；帮助其他访民出庭辩护。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年夏天为了帮助董瑶琼，向数个部门申请信息公开；关注戈觉平等并申请信息公开；关注江苏淮安毒疫苗案、昆山“爆炸案”等热门事件并申请信息公开；以及围观同道中人的开庭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">从<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月初开始，王和英开始自愿探望全国各地在押的政治犯和良心犯，写出相关报道让世人人记住他（她）们的付出。已探望过戈觉平、吴其和、朱承志、于新永、黄琦、追魂（刘进兴）、姜野飞、刘艳丽等人。王和英女士堪称大陆草根维权最杰出的人权护卫者之一。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">十几年间，王和英不断遭受政府官员打击迫害。她曾被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">6</span>次；刑拘<span lang="EN-US">34</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年被劳教一年，期间年仅<span lang="EN-US">14</span>岁女儿无人照顾被迫辍学一年；被拘禁法外“黑监狱”近百次，共七百多天，其未成年女儿被非法拘禁两次，共三十多天。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年两会前，王和英被打伤致听力严重下降、确诊为神经性耳聋。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对王和英的境况，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
