[敏昂莱会见中国驻缅大使  称愿保持沟通](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210606-1152557)
------

<div>
<p>缅甸领导人敏昂莱5日在内比都会见了中国驻缅甸大使陈海。</p><p>据中国驻缅甸大使馆网站报道，敏昂莱介绍了缅甸国内局势，表示缅方致力于国家稳定和经济民生发展，维护民主与法制。缅方愿同亚细安共同努力，致力于维护缅甸国内局势稳定，协调落实相关共识。</p><p>敏昂莱说，中国是缅甸的重要邻国，缅方愿同中方保持沟通。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>陈海表示，中方始终珍视并重视中缅传统友好，真诚希望缅甸局势尽早恢复和平稳定，支持亚细安同缅方落实达成的共识。中方愿继续为此发挥建设性作用。</p><p>双方还就中缅关系、冠病疫情联防联控等交换了意见。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
