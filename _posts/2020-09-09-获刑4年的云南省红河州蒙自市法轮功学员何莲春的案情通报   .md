<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉获刑</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">4</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">年的云南省红河州蒙自市法轮功学员何莲春的案情，现通报如下：</span><p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何莲春：云南省红河州蒙自市法轮功学员。<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年出生在石屏县宝秀镇，后嫁到蒙自县文澜镇高家村。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">此前何莲春曾接连两次遭冤判共<span lang="EN-US">17</span>年，在狱中熬过了<span lang="EN-US">15</span>年半。<a name="_Hlk50567709"><span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月</a>，出狱后她到昆明打工，<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月份又被警察绑架、构陷，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被非法判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">何莲春<span lang="EN-US">2001</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，她与其他四名法轮功学员在蒙自城区贴法轮功真相资料时，被「<span lang="EN-US">610</span>」（专门迫害法轮功的非法机构）国保警察构陷，于<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被红河州中级法院判重刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年，提前<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半出狱。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，她再次被蒙自县「<span lang="EN-US">610</span>」绑架到洗脑班「转化」迫害，由于她不放弃修炼，又被红河州中级法院判重刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">服刑期间何莲春曾遭酷刑虐待。在<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日何莲春被送入云南省第二女子监狱，关押在六监区期间，严管达五年多。期间，她被罚坐「小凳子」（长约<span lang="EN-US">20</span>多公分、宽约<span lang="EN-US">8</span>公分多），不准洗澡、洗衣，一天只给一瓶水（<span lang="EN-US">500ml</span>），近一年被限制次数上卫生间，而且经常遭到包夹（监管法轮功学员的刑事犯）的殴打、穿「紧束衣」、两次被开批斗会侮辱。何莲春进行了二十多次绝食抗议，遭到上百次野蛮灌食、灌药，饭食里投放不明药物。每次插胃管都是从她鼻子里硬插，导致她满鼻腔、满嘴都是血，每次她都要吐很多的血。由于插的次数多了，她的鼻腔、口腔、喉咙粘膜溃烂，吞咽非常困难。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月才刑满出狱。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日，何莲春去探望法轮功学员王汇真，在返回住地的途中，被追到昆明来的蒙自市国保大队警察绑架，说是为中共<span lang="EN-US">70</span>年「建国」阅兵维稳。<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，她被建水县检察院非法批捕。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，何莲春被构陷到法院。她的所谓「罪行」是：她去看法轮功学员王汇真后，被绑架时，一部手机被查获，里面存有法轮功资料。因此被判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2023</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
