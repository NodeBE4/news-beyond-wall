[河南维权人士孟跟东（孟晓东）获刑3年3个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/02/33.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，被河南睢县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留的河南维权人士孟跟东（孟晓东），于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被河南省商丘市睢县法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，其被河南省商丘市睢县检察院神速以同罪由正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，终被河南省商丘市睢县法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
