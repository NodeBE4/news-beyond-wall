[获刑2年的“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”受害人张圣相的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/2_4.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年的“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”受害人张圣相的案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张圣相：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1993</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，辽宁省沈阳市人，沈阳市沈河区东堡路<span lang="EN-US">801</span>号<span lang="EN-US">352</span>室公民，贴吧常用名：富士山君、堂岛之龙，健身教练，<span lang="EN-US">MAD</span>作者，<span lang="EN-US">S</span>触吧前吧主，动画设计师，恶俗艺术家，音<span lang="EN-US">MAD</span>圈捍卫者，“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”受害人之一，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼喜欢动漫，但因体重超胖被人耻笑故而性格十分自卑；后又因动漫初期作品遭人嘲笑，随痛下决心到沈阳帝豪斯健身俱乐部<span lang="EN-US">(</span>大悦城店<span lang="EN-US">)</span>健身<span lang="EN-US">2-3</span>年，终见成效，自此信心大增，性格渐入佳境；大学毕业后，曾供职多个职业； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，开始加入恶俗维基网站，很快成为该媒体平台上恶俗动漫兼音乐制作的骨干成员，并在恶俗圈内知名度较高；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年，曾因多家“恶俗圈”网站热发习近平之女习明泽、姐夫邓家贵的相关信息（含户籍、照片等个人信息），其所在“恶俗维基”亦将此类信息挂于网上，遂立遭中共当局严厉打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-8</span>月，由公安部迅速成立项目组，针对全国该网站会员实施全面大搜捕；抓捕之后，经严格审讯，感觉他们大多年龄尚小且行为基本无罪，故各地警方又将所抓之人纷纷释放；唯独广东省茂名市警方立功心切，声称要给中共建政<span lang="EN-US">70</span>周年献大礼，随移花接木嫁祸“恶俗维基”，把该警局所辖该网站成员（含其在内）尽都抓捕，同时向公安部谎称“破获了一起与反华势力相勾结的恶势力集团大案”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，其被广东省茂名市警方跨省抓捕，后被暂短指定居所监视居住和刑拘；同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被茂名市检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人“涉恶势力犯罪集团案”在广东省茂名市茂南区法院开庭审理；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，此案<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人均被宣判，张圣相被一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日；因其等多人均表不服，随即上诉；截至目前，二审正在等待中。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
