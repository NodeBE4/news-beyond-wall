[Linux 5.8 释出](https://www.solidot.org/story?sid=65124)
------

<div>
Linus Torvalds 在内核邮件上<a href="https://lore.kernel.org/lkml/20200802220243.GA20003@Gentoo/T/#m1e38263ea10c7990c538ca2ac2bfa779e9d7eeec"><u>宣布</u></a>释出了 Linux 5.8。主要新特性包括：内核空间动态数据竞争检测器 <a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/816850/"><u>Kernel Concurrency Sanitizer (KCSAN)</u></a>；<a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/760714/">内核事件通知机制</a>；私有 procfs 挂载； ARM64 支持 <a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/804982/" target="_blank">Shadow Call Stack 和 Branch Target Identification</a>；支持<a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/797309/" target="_blank"><u>块层内联加密</u></a>；<a href="https://lwn.net/Articles/818714/" target="_blank">BPF iterator</a> 机制，等等。详细可阅读 <a href="https://kernelnewbies.org/Linux_5.8"><u>KernelNewbies 5.8</u></a>。                      <img src="https://img.solidot.org//0/446/liiLIZF8Uh6yM.jpg" style="display:block;margin:5px 0" referrerpolicy="no-referrer">
