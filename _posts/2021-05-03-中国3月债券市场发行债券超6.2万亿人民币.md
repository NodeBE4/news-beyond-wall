[中国3月债券市场发行债券超6.2万亿人民币](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210503-1143949)
------

<div>
<p>中国人民银行近日发布的数据显示，3月份，中国债券市场共发行各类债券超6.2万亿人民币（1.28亿新元）。</p><p>据新华社报道，中国央行公布的金融市场运行情况显示，3月份，中国国债发行4640亿元，地方政府债券发行4771.5亿元，金融债券发行9738.9亿元，公司信用类债券发行1.6万亿元，信贷资产支持证券发行1014.4亿元，同业存单发行2.6万亿元。</p><p>数据显示，3月份，中国银行间债券市场现券成交19万亿元，日均成交8273.2亿元，同比下降25.51%，环比上升29.19%。交易所债券市场现券成交2万亿元，日均成交855.6亿元，同比下降2.71%，环比上升3%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
