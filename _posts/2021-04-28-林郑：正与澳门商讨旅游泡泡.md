[林郑：正与澳门商讨旅游泡泡](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142788)
------

<div>
<p>香港行政长官林郑月娥今天（27日）出席立法会质询环节时表示，上周在海南曾与澳门特首贺一诚探讨港澳旅游泡泡事宜，目前港府正积极跟进。</p><p>据《信报》报道，林郑月娥说，完全明白旅游业在疫情下受到的冲击，当局希望可逐步有序开放本地游，若情况许可会进入第二阶段。</p><p>就两地旅游泡泡安排，她表示，上周与贺一诚曾进行商讨，但澳门与大陆接近全面通关，故需要研究若港澳通关，如何确保港人不能经澳门进入大陆。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另外，她说，疫苗是最有效早日回复正常生活的方法，与大陆及大湾区探讨免检疫的工作不断进行中，但双方达成共识才可推出，目前未有具体公布，而现时香港疫苗接种仍不算理想。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
