[中国连续大量购买美国玉米 推动价格攀升](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210519-1147880)
------

<div>
<p>中国周二再次购买了大批下一季美国玉米，表明其对谷物的旺盛需求远远没有结束。</p><p>据彭博社报道，周二中国购买了136万吨美国玉米，之前一天的购买量更是达到历史第三大。根据美国农业部的数据，中国购买的9月开始的下一季玉米总量已经超过800万吨，轻松创下历史纪录。</p><p>StoneX首席大宗商品经济学家苏德曼（Arlan Suderman）在一份报告中分析，中国继续“利用近期价格下跌的机会。乙醇生产商也正忙于安排夏季的需求，几乎没有迹象表明高玉米价格正在促使需求更加理性。”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>芝加哥市场7月交割的玉米上涨0.9％至每蒲式耳6.5825美元（8.76新元），延续了周一的涨势。小麦和大豆下跌。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
