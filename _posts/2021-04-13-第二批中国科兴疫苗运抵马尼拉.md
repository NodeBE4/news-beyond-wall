[第二批中国科兴疫苗运抵马尼拉](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210412-1138793)
------

<div>
<p>当地时间11日下午，菲律宾政府采购的第二批中国科兴疫苗运抵马尼拉。</p><p>综合中新社与菲新社报道，菲政府应对新冠疫情总协调人卡利托加尔韦斯（Carlito Galvez Jr）率多名政府官员，前往机场接收这批疫苗。</p><p>加尔韦斯说，这是菲政府从中国科兴控股生物技术有限公司采购疫苗的一部分。他还说，“新冠病例正在上升，我们正在与时间赛跑，（菲律宾）希望立即为民众接种疫苗。”</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>菲律宾3月1日正式启动疫苗接种，现已批准紧急使用包括中国科兴新冠疫苗在内的多款疫苗。菲律宾政府采购的首批中国科兴疫苗于3月29日抵菲。此前，中国已先后两次向菲援助科兴疫苗。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
