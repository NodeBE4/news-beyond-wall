[遭绑架到法轮功洗脑班关押的广州法轮功学员朱宇飙律师的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/04/blog-post_25.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉遭绑架到法轮功洗脑班关押的广州法轮功学员朱宇飙的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">朱宇飙：<span lang="EN-US">1971</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月出生，广东省人，原广州地铁小额贷款有限公司职员，原广东金粤律师事务所、广州市广大律师事务所、广东恒益律师事务所执业律师，广东省第一个公开为法轮功信仰者作无罪辩护的正义律师，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">学生时代，曾就读于广东省中山大学法学专业；法学硕士毕业后，先后曾就职于广东金粤律师事务所、广州市广大律师事务所和广东恒益律师事务所，作执业律师；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因为经常热心为弱势群体当事人作代理辩护律师，并以低价或折价收费，甚至不计报酬，所以深受社会大众及业界同仁称许、好评；但因其练习和信仰法轮功而屡遭政府打压逼迫，遂被迫多次转换律所，直至最终其律师执业证被广东司法当局吊销；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2005</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年—<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年，曾分别为三名法轮功信仰者做无罪辩护，因其法庭辩护词精彩绝伦，堪称经典，顿令中共法庭大为震惊和难堪，随后即被广东司法当局称为“反革命”，将其列为广东省公安厅要维稳严控的“头号人物”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，其突然被广州市警方野蛮抓捕，后被枉法处劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，送广州市第三劳教所关押；关押期间，曾遭多次酷刑折磨，如“坐老虎凳”、“架飞机”、用湿毛巾盖脸窒息、水淹、在冷天灌凉水、鞭打手指及脚趾、不准睡觉，等等； <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，劳教释放后，因其律师证被广州当局故意拖延办理，对其工作和日常生活造成极大不便；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日—<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其第二次被广州市海珠区新港派出所警方莫名抓捕、刑拘，家遭查抄，后被广州市海珠区法院以“利用邪教破坏法律实施罪”枉法判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，并送广东省北江监狱服刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，虽是其刑满获释日，但又被转送到广东省三水洗脑班非法关押<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日，再次被广州市海珠区警方十余人闯入抄家，搜走几本其个人阅读的法轮功书籍之后，随即将其绑架到一不知名的法轮功洗脑班关押；据悉，自其被抓至今，家人一直无法知其更多信息。目前被羁押地点不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
