[中国股市连跌三天后上涨](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210617-1157490)
------

<div>
<p>中国股市周四上涨，结束了三日连跌。科技股则因有关中国推进芯片发展的报导而大涨。</p><p>据路透社报道，沪深300指数上涨0.4%，至5101.89点。沪综指上涨0.2%，至3525.60点。创业板指数收升 2.012%。上证科创板50成份指数收升4.77%。中证半导体指数跳涨8.6%，顶级芯片制造商中芯国际上涨7.5%。</p><p>在此之前，彭博社引述消息人士报导，中国经济政策的主要决策者、副总理刘鹤将主持第三代半导体发展的推进工作，并负责制定相关的政策支持。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>不过，对中国国内股票估值的担忧仍然是投资者关注的焦点。</p><p>北京凌通盛泰投资管理中心董事长董宝珍分析说，最近调整的根本原因是高估值，因为许多中国机构投资者从昂贵的股票中转出，为年终排名做准备。他说，A股市场极高估值和极低估值并存，这需要修正。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
