[中国点餐浪费者或被收取厨余垃圾处理费](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210422-1141365)
------

<div>
<p>中国将可能立法，规定点餐浪费者被收取厨余垃圾处理费。</p><p>综合中国央视新闻和澎湃新闻报道，全国人大常委会法制工作委员会发言人臧铁伟今天（22日）在记者会上介绍，经过初次审议和修改的反食品浪费法草案，将完善食品浪费的定义，明确反食品浪费工作的牵头部门和有关执法主体，以及进一步完善食品捐赠相关规定。</p><p>草案也将完善对造成明显浪费的消费者收取处理厨余垃圾相应费用的规定，增强可操作性，保护消费者权益，并且强化反食品浪费宣传教育。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>全国人大常委会将于4月26日至29日召开会议，反食品浪费法草案二次审议稿将提请此次会议继续审议。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
