[获刑1年8个月的山东枣庄维权人士释道果（原名张超）于2021年3月23日刑满获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/03/182021323.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月的山东枣庄维权人士释道果（原名张超）于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日刑满获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">释道果（原名张超）：男，<span lang="EN-US">1970</span>年生人，法名释道果，山东枣庄维权人士，早年在北京务工期间觉醒，多年致力于网络民主理念宣传，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年因给在京维权人士发提醒搜捕信息被关押<span lang="EN-US">37</span>天，后剃度出家云游四海，修行、研究佛学期间依然不忘网络启蒙和落难同道救助，以致每有落脚寺庙即被驱逐。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年张超的女儿考入大学后，他希望能把女儿送出国外，以脱离当局的摆布。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日张超预定了往返德国的机票，欲前往考察了解情况。但在<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日张超突然遭到枣庄市山亭分局的抓捕，随即被以涉嫌寻衅滋事的罪名刑事拘留。后遭逮捕、起诉。释道果寻衅滋事案于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">9</span>点在山东省枣庄市山亭区人民法院开庭，未当庭宣判。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
