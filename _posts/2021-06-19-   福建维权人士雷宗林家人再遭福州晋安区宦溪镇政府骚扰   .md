[福建维权人士雷宗林家人再遭福州晋安区宦溪镇政府骚扰](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/06/blog-post_37.html)

<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：福建维权人士雷宗林<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">15</span>日刑满出狱，一个星期后便被全方位监控，<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时有人蹲守在他的住处。由于老问题没得到解决，新打压又开始。雷宗林对居住权、生存权、家财被抢劫要求归还、强拆等问题进行控告。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">11</span>日晋安区宦溪镇政府维稳人带领<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人到雷示林父亲工作处进行骚扰，要雷宗林停止法律行动。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">雷宗林</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：福州市晋安区宦溪人，双下肢残疾，其身残志坚，先后从事过养殖、种植、修车等行业谋生。<span lang="EN-US"> 1998</span>年一场台风摧毁旧宅，重建家园时重新修建的房屋被地方官员以违建为由拆毁。<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年到京申冤遭构陷被判一年刑期，在这期间一千多平米猪场也被恶霸强占，生活无所依托。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">雷宗林在维权过程中觉醒，参加福州维权人士“每周一聚”抱团取暖，公民互助活动。<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月赴南昌江西高院声援“乐平冤案”和福建老乡屠夫。多次在福州马尾等地街头举牌为屠夫、望云和尚、王宇律师等公义人士鼓呼；<span lang="EN-US">2016</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月与福州一众维权人士集体举牌纪念“六四”。雷在长期的维权中曾遭遇当局惨无人道的“活埋”，强制关入精神病院等迫害。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月杭州<span lang="EN-US">G20</span>峰会期间，风声鹤唳，福州当局对各类民间维权活动的打压达到高潮，雷宗林与林炳兴、江智安等共十五位福州维权人士相继被捕，雷宗林被另案处理，雷被判有期徒刑三年十个月，是该案刑期最长者。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
