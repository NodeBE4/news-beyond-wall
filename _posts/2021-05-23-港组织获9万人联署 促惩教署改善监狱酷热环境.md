[港组织获9万人联署 促惩教署改善监狱酷热环境](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210523-1148827)
------

<div>
<p>天气酷热，香港关注囚权的组织“石墙花”已经收集逾9万人联署，呼吁香港惩教署改善监狱酷热环境问题。</p><p>据《明报》报道，“石墙花”昨晚在面簿发文说，至晚上11时，即签名运动开始了29小时，已有90451人联署，预期很快达到10万人联署的目标。石墙花表示，希望香港惩教署正视监狱酷热环境问题，落实人性化管理。</p><p>石墙花希望冀募集十万人联署，要求惩教署改善监狱酷热环境。“石墙花”发起的联署要求惩教署改善监狱酷热环境，例如新增预防中暑物资、提供冻水饮用、增加囚友洗澡次数等。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>立法会前议员邵家臻建议惩教署提供冻水及室温水，允许家属购入电动手提风扇等，这些均不用署方付额外开支。他指出，过去多年来一直有提出该建议，但是惩教署不愿改善，他认为署方没有视在囚者为监狱持份者。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
