[蔡英文将亲上火线 谈党内排黑](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210505-1144486)
------

<div>
<p>民进党"政二代"赵介佑的黑道背景引发对民进党“黑道入党”的质疑声音，身兼民进党主席的台湾总统蔡英文今日在中常会后，将对此议题发表谈话。</p><p>据联合报报道，蔡英文今天将亲上火线，于民进党中常会后，同党秘书长林锡耀在中央党部10楼记者室发表谈话。蔡英文过去在党中央发表谈话，地点多选在九楼常会会议室外，此次谈话地点为蔡英文就任总统后少见。</p><p>民进党台北市党部主委吴怡农今天上午也针对党内排黑议题举行记者会，说明党部排黑条款及人头党员的三大改革方向。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>吴怡农指出，未来新进党员排黑有三点具体作为，包括入党需有联络电话、手机；新进党员推荐人也要对党员有基本认识；党部也会透过司法公开资讯，确认党员，是否遵守入党规范。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
