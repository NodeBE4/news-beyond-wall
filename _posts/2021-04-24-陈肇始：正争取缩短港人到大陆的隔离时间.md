[陈肇始：正争取缩短港人到大陆的隔离时间](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210424-1141883)
------

<div>
<p>香港食物及卫生局局长陈肇始表示，正争取缩短港人到大陆的隔离天数。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，陈肇始在一档电视节目上表示，香港接种疫苗的情况需要更进取，并指若市民已接种两剂疫苗，并又过14天，理论上已有抗体，属于安全。</p><p>她还说，在此基础上与大陆当局商讨港人到大陆的隔离安排，正争取缩短隔离天数。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>对于此前香港行政长官林郑月娥宣布，将于本月底把回港易扩至广东省以外大陆省市回港的香港居民，在回港时不需14日隔离的安排，陈肇始则指，当局下周将公布相关细节。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
