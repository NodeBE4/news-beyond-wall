[消息：美菜已秘密提交美国首次公开募股申请](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210526-1149587)
------

<div>
<p>知情人士透露，连接餐馆和蔬菜种植户的中国初创企业美菜已秘密提交美国首次公开募股(IPO)申请。</p><p>据彭博社报道，知情人士说，这家总部设在北京的公司眼下正在与美银、华兴资本、摩根士丹利等一道筹备上市事宜，不久便会开始探寻投资者兴趣。筹资规模和公司估值等细节尚未最终确定。目前相关讨论仍处于早期阶段，不能确保上市计划肯定会推进。</p><p>彭博今年2月曾报道，美菜先前曾经考虑规模约3亿美元的上市筹资计划，但是公司前首席财务官王灿的突然辞职使得计划遭到了延缓。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
