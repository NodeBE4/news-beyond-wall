[中国外管局：今年以来人民币汇率总体稳定](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210423-1141595)
------

<div>
<p>中国国家外汇管理局副局长王春英今天表示，今年以来人民币汇率总体稳定，呈现出窄幅波动、双向波动特征；中长期来看中国外汇市场会保持平稳运行，基础还是稳固的。</p><p>综合央视新闻和路透社报道，王春英在国新办发布会上称，一季度售汇率总体稳定，企业跨境融资稳中有增；结汇率略有增长，企业外汇存款稳步增加。</p><p>她还指出，在涉外经济保持活跃的情况下，一季度外汇跨境收支顺差1005亿美元（1335亿新元），企业合理安排相关资金，一部分结汇，一部分以存款形式持有。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>外管局稍早公布数据显示，3月银行结售汇顺差197亿美元；1至3月银行累计结售汇顺差885亿美元。3月银行代客涉外收付款顺差393亿美元，1至3月银行累计代客涉外收付款顺差1213亿美元。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
