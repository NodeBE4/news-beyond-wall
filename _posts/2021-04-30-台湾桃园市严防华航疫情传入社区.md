[台湾桃园市严防华航疫情传入社区](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210430-1143292)
------

<div>
<p>台湾华航机师染疫扩大到机场防疫旅馆，华航诺富特饭店的员工确诊，桃园机场员工最常搭乘的桃园机场捷运也将防疫规格拉到最高。</p><p>据联合新闻网报道，桃园市长郑文灿上午前往紧邻饭店的机场捷运A14a机场旅馆站视察，郑文灿要求落实防疫措施，包括入境旅客、有在外站过夜或离开飞机的机组员，均不得搭机捷，违规者重罚，捷运站也每小时都消毒一次。</p><p>郑文灿上午亲自视察清洁人员消毒，郑文灿表示，华航诺富特饭店由中央流行疫情指挥中心执行净空，市府负责的机捷部分，14a站有20个工作人员，每天平均600个乘客进出，依防疫规定启动全线大消毒，两天内完成机捷21个车站消毒，车站、车厢及设施则提高消毒频率，每个小时一次，桃捷员工也必须做好防护措施保护自己和乘客，做好疫情的防火墙，把防疫每个可能环节都守住。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
