[遭当局拘留的北京基督教锡安教会两名传道人郄佳富、黄春子已获释](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/05/blog-post_98.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，本网获悉：四月底北京最大的基督教家庭教会<span lang="EN-US">---</span>锡安教会两名传道人郄佳富、黄春子遭当局无端拘留，目前都已获释。黄春子于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日获释，会弟兄姐妹前去探望，描述春子状态很好，感谢大家为她守望祷告。郄佳富传道也于<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日被释放到家了。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日夜<span lang="EN-US">23</span>点<span lang="EN-US">40</span>分，锡安教会传道人郄佳富，在他的住所被辖区沙河派出所警察以传唤名义带走。事后派出所给出“非法集会”的理由，但没有进一步透露细节。<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日凌晨<span lang="EN-US">1</span>点，郄佳富妻子收到派出所通知，根据《治安管理处罚法》第<span lang="EN-US">54</span>条，郄佳富被处以行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天，并处<span lang="EN-US">500</span>元罚款。但警方让郄佳富的妻子签字后，却不给正式拘留证。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">与此同时，锡安教会另一名传道人黄春子，也自<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日失联，由于其独自居住，直至<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日才被发现。当局没有联系黄春子的亲属，也没有告知其下落。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">锡安教会是北京最大的基督教家庭教会。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日，锡安教会北京总堂遭当局查封，并被当局罚款<span lang="EN-US">120</span>万元人民币。但锡安教会并没有就此放弃，而是化整为零，在北京及其他城市，建起数百聚会点，坚持聚会传播信仰，信徒人数不仅没有因当局打压而减少，反而实现成倍增长，达数万人。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
