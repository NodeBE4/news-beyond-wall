With its </span><span>headquarters in London, </span><em>The Economist</em><span> has offices and people across the globe, and is valued by its customers for its world-class insights and rigorous journalism. We’ve published major features from <a href="https://www.economist.com/1843/2020/01/20/trapped-in-iran">Iran</a>, <a href="https://www.economist.com/1843/2020/04/22/the-coronavirus-cruise-on-board-the-diamond-princess">Japan</a> and <a href="https://www.economist.com/1843/2021/02/19/she-was-trafficked-into-a-giant-brothel-now-she-runs-it">Bangladesh</a>, and are equally interested in new ideas from <a href="https://www.economist.com/1843/2019/07/10/how-donuts-fuelled-the-american-dream">America</a> and <a href="https://www.economist.com/1843/2020/07/26/the-nhs-in-peril-how-britain-fought-the-first-covid-19-wave">Britain</a>. This is an exciting opportunity to work at </span><em>The Economist </em><span>and be part of a team that is expanding its publication of ambitious narrative journalism.</span></span></span></p><h2>How you will contribute:</h2><ul><li>This is a high profile and wide-ranging commissioning and editing role, with a focus on long-form features</li><li>Generate ideas and seek out suitable writers, working closely with them to see a story through</li><li>Copy editing, headline writing and proofreading</li><li>Work closely with other 1843 editors based in London, helping to shape stories at different stages of the editing process</li><li>Commission and edit shorter, timely features to a high standard on tight deadlines</li></ul><h2>The ideal candidate will be:</h2><ul><li>A rigorous, organised and experienced editor with a passion for commissioning and editing long-form features</li><li>An innovative thinker, brimming with ideas for stories and writers, and with a keen interest in the forces shaping America and elsewhere</li><li>An excellent line editor with attention to detail</li><li>Have a clear vision of how to build on the success of <em>1843 magazine</em></li><li>Have a good understanding of digital journalism, and how to engage online readers</li><li>Having visual flair and feel for design is also a plus</li></ul><p ><span>The successful applicant will work with a highly professional and dynamic team.
