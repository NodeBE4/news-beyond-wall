[上海一企业厂房火灾致八人死亡](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210423-1141583)
------

<div>
<p>上海市金山区人民政府新闻办公室官方微博23日上午通报，金山区林盛路171弄116号胜瑞电子科技（上海）有限公司失火厂房明火今早已被扑灭，八名失联人员已找到，均无生命体征。</p><p>据新华社报道，22日下午1时30分，位于上海金山区林盛路171弄116号的胜瑞电子科技（上海）有限公司厂房发生火灾。两名消防救援人员在搜救被困人员过程中牺牲，其他六名遇难者的相关善后工作正在进行。</p><p>现场正再次进行确认工作。周边大气、水体监测结果无异常，消防污水及时收集存储，下一步将按规范进行处理。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>目前现场善后清理工作正在抓紧开展，事故原因正在调查中。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
