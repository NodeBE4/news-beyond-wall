[香港航空证实将内部重组](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210608-1153418)
------

<div>
<p>疫情令全球航空业界深受打击。香港航空证实将会进行内部重组，并会在未来数天向员工公布有关详情。</p><p>据星岛日报报道，香港航空表示，为确保香港航空在充满挑战的未来作好准备，公司必要进行内部重组，以达致更精简及更具效率的架构，详情会在未来数天向员工公布。</p><p>消息指，香港航空将会暂停营运A320客机，占公司整体飞机逾三成。同时，除了透过停飞方案外，据了解，管理层亦正研究另一个节流方案，或裁减全体员工一半，即约1000人。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>根据港航网页资料，港航目前拥有32架飞机，当中12架为A320客机。在没有疫情前已深受财政压力的港航，近期不时传出财困的消息，曾于疫情期间两度裁员。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
