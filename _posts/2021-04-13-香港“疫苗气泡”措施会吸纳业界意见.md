[香港“疫苗气泡”措施会吸纳业界意见](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210413-1139081)
------

<div>
<p>香港食物及卫生局局长陈肇始表示，会在本周约见饮食业界商讨“疫苗气泡”放宽社交距离的执行细节，包括如何处理不接种疫苗的员工和顾客安排。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，陈肇始强调，新措施是为业界放宽营业限制提供诱因，当局完全不会干预业界如何处理不接种疫苗员工的做法，但也不希望雇主与员工出现对立。</p><p>陈肇始接受商台节目访问，被多次追问推出“疫苗气泡”前，是否有与业界商讨。她说，当局一直有听取业界意见，如果业界认为执行新措施有困难，现有安排仍会继续。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>至于推出措施前，是否有咨询专家意见，陈始肇表示，措施由政府内部作整体敲定，会吸纳专家意见。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
