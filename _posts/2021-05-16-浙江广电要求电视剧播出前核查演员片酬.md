[浙江广电要求电视剧播出前核查演员片酬](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210515-1146969)
------

<div>
<p>浙江广电局多举措推动电视剧片酬管理，其中包括在电视剧播出前，均提醒播出机构对演员片酬作进一步核查，确保片酬合法合规。</p><p>根据中国国家广播电视总局官网发布浙江局消息，浙江广电局的新举措还要求播出机构学习有关规定，充分认清片酬管理工作的重要意义。</p><p>同时加强日常监管。建立“一剧一承诺”制度，对已备案公示剧目，组织制作方主要负责人学习规定并作出书面承诺。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>对于浙产电视剧，广电局要求在播出前，均提醒播出机构对演员片酬作进一步核查，确保片酬合法合规。</p><p>此外，广电局也要求抓好警示教育，对典型片酬案例进行剖析，开展警示教育等。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
