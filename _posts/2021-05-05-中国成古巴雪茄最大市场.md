[中国成古巴雪茄最大市场](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210505-1144470)
------

<div>
<p>古巴国营雪茄厂商哈伯纳斯（Habanos S.A.）周二声明，中国去年取代西班牙，成为古巴雪茄的最大市场，但不断升高的中国需求无法弥补其他地区销售下滑，全球销售下降4%至5.07亿美元（6.77亿新元）。</p><p>据路透社报道，哈伯纳斯声明，去年疫情大流行引发的旅行限制，使得仰赖观光业以及免税品销售国家的需求遭受严重冲击。古巴的手卷雪茄被许多人视为全世界首屈一指的逸品。</p><p>根据声明，欧洲仍是哈伯纳斯雪茄最大的区域性市场，占其销售的50%，其次为亚太地区占到16.2%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>古巴官媒Cuban News Agency周二引述多名哈伯纳斯高管的话说，欧洲与亚太地区的销售分别上升了2%与10%。不过非洲与中东地区销售下滑31%，美洲地区减少21%。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
