mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，扎西加在个人微信朋友圈内发布一张含有雪山狮子旗和洛桑孙根<span lang="EN-US">(</span>即洛桑森格，藏人行政中央司政<span lang="EN-US">)</span>照片，获得其微信朋友的点赞和评论。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: &quot;MS Gothic&quot;;
