<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，本网获悉：被以寻衅滋事罪获刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月的山西媒体人杜二伟已于<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">杜二伟</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：生于<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年，山西省忻州市代县人，曾于生活晨报、山西青年报、新华社山西分社、发展导报、中国经济时报、中国现代企业报、民主与法制时报等新闻单位任职；前《发展导报》舆论监督部主任、前新华社山西分社《决策参考》工作部主任、前《中国现代企业报》企业观察周刊主编、前《民主与法制时报》经济版主编，新闻媒体人。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因在网上发表事政言论，在微信上就中共当局“七不准”、“国进民退”、“延迟养老” 及习近平修宪等内容发表批评性言论，而于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日被山西省太原市警方强行传唤，并被问及“驱逐低端人口、非洲猪瘟、修宪领导人玩无限期连任”等问题；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">审讯中曾遭酷刑虐待（如被铐在审讯椅上长达<span lang="EN-US">15</span>小时，戴背铐及脚镣等）；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，又被北京警方抓捕，并交太原市警方被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，被太原市检察院以同罪名正式批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，其因言获罪案在山西省太原市迎泽区法院开庭，检察院建议法院对其量刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年半—<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年半，未当庭宣判。后被宣判<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月有期徒刑。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日获释。此前被羁押于太原市第二看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
