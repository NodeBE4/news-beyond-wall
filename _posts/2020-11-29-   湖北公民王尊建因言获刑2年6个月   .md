[湖北公民王尊建因言获刑2年6个月](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/11/26.html)
------

<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">11</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">29</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，本网获悉因言获刑</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">个月的湖北公民王尊建的案情，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王尊建：</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">湖北籍公民。<span lang="EN-US">1972</span>年生，因言获罪于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">15</span>天，同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被刑事拘留，羁押在云梦县第一看守所，同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被执行逮捕，被控罪名为涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据湖北省孝感市中级法院在二审裁定书：当局指控王尊建于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月至<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月间，多次到敏感案件、事件的现场围观、声援；在网络上炒作、声援敏感案件、事件和特定人物，辱骂、诋毁党和国家领导人，利用网络平台破坏社会管理秩序，情节恶劣，构成寻衅滋事罪。其中包括<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>号在武汉市青山公安分局门口参与声援秦永敏涉嫌煽动颠覆国家政权罪一案；<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日转发镇江老兵维权事件；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月转发、发布大量香港社会反送中运动的相关信息；发布纪念六四三十周年的相关言论；等等。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因此，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日云梦县法院一审判决王尊建有期徒刑两年六个月，王尊建上诉后，二审维持了原判。刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
