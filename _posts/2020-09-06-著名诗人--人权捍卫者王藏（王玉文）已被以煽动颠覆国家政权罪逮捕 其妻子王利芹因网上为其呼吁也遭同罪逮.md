font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">爸爸妈妈皆被抓捕，奶奶带四幼儿尚被警方重重包围封锁家中，眼前发生的一切就是在中国大陆云南楚雄。这就是中国人权的现状！<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏，</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1985</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王藏曾于<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年底以“小王子”为笔名开始涉足网络诗歌习作，自此走上自由写作之路，并对极权文化予以批判。<span lang="EN-US">2005</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月，加入独立中文笔会，同年遭到官方威胁和长达<span lang="EN-US">2</span>个多月的监视居住。<span lang="EN-US">2006</span>年—<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年间因积极参与“中国自由文化运动”签名活动，又在《自由圣火》网站发文并设专栏、在博讯网上开设专栏发表文集等，而被贵州省遵义市警方于<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月再次强制监视居住<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月。<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月，荣获“<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>《自由圣火》写作奖”。<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，正式启用笔名“王藏”，藉以表达“来生愿做藏人”的意愿。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日早上<span lang="EN-US">4.30</span>分左右，被云南省楚雄市公安机关突然以涉嫌煽动颠覆罪由带走，后遭刑事拘留逮捕，被正式批捕的日期是<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日。现在羁押在云南楚雄市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
