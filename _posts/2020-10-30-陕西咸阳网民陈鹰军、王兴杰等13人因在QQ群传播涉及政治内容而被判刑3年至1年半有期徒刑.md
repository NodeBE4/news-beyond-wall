<p><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，本网获悉：陕西咸阳网民陈鹰军、王兴杰等<span lang="EN-US">13</span>人因在<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群传播涉及政治的内容于<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日被抓捕，<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月被咸阳市秦都区法院以寻衅滋事罪判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年至<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年半有期徒刑。判决书指控他们通过<span lang="EN-US">QQ</span>群有组织地传播“涉及政治的谣言”，攻击政府、党和国家领导人。据判决书称该组织首脑人物雷某某已经死亡，不清楚死因是否与本案有关。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">被判刑的<span lang="EN-US">13</span>名陕西咸阳网民分别是：<a name="_Hlk54988374">陈鹰军</a>、<a name="_Hlk54988521">王兴杰、卢向辉</a>、<a name="_Hlk54988577">刘芗、周昱、朱远亮、李好轩、徐海波、李泰营、周福评、潘建</a>、廖焕华、武刚。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其中，陈鹰军在庭审中只高喊口号，不做辩护，后被判刑<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。王兴杰、卢向辉被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日；刘芗、周昱、朱远亮、李好轩、徐海波、李泰营、周福评、潘建等<span lang="EN-US">8</span>人被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日；廖焕华、武刚被判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至：<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。廖焕华、武刚已获释。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
