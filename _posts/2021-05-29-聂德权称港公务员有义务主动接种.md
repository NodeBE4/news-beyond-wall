[聂德权称港公务员有义务主动接种](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210529-1150243)
------

<div>
<p>香港公务员事务局局长聂德权今天（29日）说，公务员本身有义务主动接种疫苗，而当局也有具体措施方便公务员打针，如可于办公时间前往接种、打针后如要休息，可不用获取医生证明等。</p><p>据香港电台报道，他指出，下星期一起，前线政府雇员如未有接种疫苗，便需定期每两星期做检测。</p><p>他强烈鼓励有关公务员打针，又强调现时疫情缓和，但要做好预防工作，而他有信心整体公务员的接种率一定比全港为高。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>另外，有发展商昨日公布，为响应疫苗接种计划，将会送出一个住宅单位，让已接种两剂疫苗的市民抽奖。聂德权认为，企业提出鼓励措施会有正面作用，希望不同界别及企业都继续有不同鼓励方法，至于会否有人为抽奖而隐瞒病历去打针，他说每人都要掌握相关信息数据后作出决定。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
