<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><br /></span><p></p><p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日被抓捕的北京维权人士全世欣案将于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日日上午十点北京市海淀法院山后法庭开庭审理。此前全世欣案曾三次</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，又因身穿写有“蔡奇见家属”字样的白色短袖<span lang="EN-US">T</span>恤，高喊要求与市政府相关负责人见面递交相关材料，寻求解决问题，而被海淀区警方以“扰乱公共场所秩序”罪由行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">5</span>天；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
