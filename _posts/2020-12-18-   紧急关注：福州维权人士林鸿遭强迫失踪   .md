[紧急关注：福州维权人士林鸿遭强迫失踪](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/blog-post_22.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉：福州维权人士林鸿女士于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日去北京国家信访局反映房屋拆迁的问题。<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日晚上<span lang="EN-US">21</span>点<span lang="EN-US">18</span>分，她与家人最后联系，她说“现在我在久敬庄救助中心”，后失去联系，至今下落不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">家属说：<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日前往福州新店派出所报案，要求公安帮助寻找失踪的林鸿，公安告知“林鸿自北京回来，现由于疫情原因，已将她隔离，这是政府行为，不予立案。”家属要求公安告知林鸿在哪里隔离，公安不予回应。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林鸿简介：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年家中房屋被骗拆，维权十一年至今未得到相应补偿，信访途中屡遭福州当局打击迫害。曾在北京信访时被当局截访并遭受巨大刺激昏倒休克，经抢救后侥幸脱险。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">连日来，家属在臭名昭著的福州鼓岭同乐园、石鼓山庄（福州维权人士被非法羁押地）等多处寻找无果。家人身心疲惫，非常担忧林鸿的身体状况和安危。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">与林鸿同往北京维权的张金姫和林珠妹也被福州当局从北京带回后杳无音讯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在此，我们强烈谴责福州当局对维权人士以疫情之名，行强迫失踪之实。中国《宪法》承诺保障人权，同时是联合国《世界人权宣言》、《经济、社会、文化权利国际公约》、《公民权利和政治权利国际公约》等众多国际人权文书的签署国。据此，我们敦请福州当局禁止强迫失踪、非法拘禁等严重侵犯人权行为。对当事人遭受的伤害给予安抚，同时，对违法犯罪人员追责。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
