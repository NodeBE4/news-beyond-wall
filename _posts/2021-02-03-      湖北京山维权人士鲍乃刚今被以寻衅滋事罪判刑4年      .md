[湖北京山维权人士鲍乃刚今被以寻衅滋事罪判刑4年](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/02/4.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，本网获悉：湖北荆门京山市鲍乃刚的辩护律师吴魁明今日（<span lang="EN-US">2021.2.3</span>）下午收到经办法官的电话通知，法庭今天在湖北京山市看守所宣判鲍乃刚涉嫌寻衅滋事罪一案，鲍乃刚被判刑四年。鲍乃刚表示要上诉。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日荆门鲍案在京山市法院开庭审理，迄今已超过一年半的时间。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>鲍乃刚：</b>湖北京山维权人士。男，湖北省京山县宋河镇人，复转军官，曾在绵阳市北川县经商，因为父上访申冤<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2003</span>年，被当局栽赃入监两年。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2011</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，因在网上响应茉莉花革命，被当局以“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”刑拘，后羁押于绵阳市看守所长达<span lang="EN-US">9</span>个月，之后被逮捕，同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月被取保获释送回原籍湖北。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，鲍乃刚因旁听武汉王芳涉嫌寻衅滋事案庭审遭户籍地京山县公安局行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日处罚。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，鲍乃刚向京山县人民法院对京山县公安局提起行政诉状，要求撤销京山县公安局京公（宋河）行决字<span lang="EN-US">1101</span>号行政处罚决定书，责令恶意剥夺公民权利的京山县公安局向公众公开道歉。后遭法院枉法判决。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日鲍乃刚刚到北京就被湖北京山维稳人员强行劫持，押解回当地，第二天，鲍乃刚在京山公安局接受询问并被抄家，警方拿走了鲍乃刚的电脑、手机，以及一些上诉材料等。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日鲍乃刚被京山警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑事拘留。现关押在京山市看守所。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
