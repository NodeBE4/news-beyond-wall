[特斯拉称西安维权女子车辆无故障](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210423-1141661)
------

<div>
<p>美国电动车制造商特斯拉今天（23日）指西安维权女车主的车子并没有发现故障的情况。</p><p>据中国《新京报》旗下的“贝壳财经”官方微博报道，特斯拉称，这名车主已正常使用该车辆超过一年，与车子有关的事故和相关车辆损伤，并非产品质量问题导致。</p><p>特斯拉也指车主在事故后提出的特斯拉原价退车的诉求没有法律依据，因此公司未能同意车主的诉求。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>红星新闻早前报道，这名西安车主是本周一（19日）在上海车展维权的怀孕女子李女士。她当天与河南车主张女士身上印有特斯拉商标及“刹车失灵”字样的T恤，出现在上海车展。</p><p>张女士随后站上特斯拉展台的车顶，高喊“特斯拉刹车失灵”。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
