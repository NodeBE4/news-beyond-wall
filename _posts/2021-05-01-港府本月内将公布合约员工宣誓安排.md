[港府本月内将公布合约员工宣誓安排](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210501-1143572)
------

<div>
<p>香港公务员事务局长聂德权表示，政府本月内将会公布就非公务员合约员工的宣誓安排，并正研究法定组织及政府资助机构的员工，是否界定为公职人员，应否遵循宣誓安排。</p><p>根据《星岛日报》报道，聂德权在一档节目中说，超过17万名公务员已完成宣誓，129人不签署或没有妥为签署，当中25人已经离职，当局已经去信要求其余人士解释，并会安排他们离开公务员队伍。</p><p>聂德权重申，公务员作为政治体制的重要一员，责任是支持特首及政府施政，落实好政策。他们只要不违反相关原则，所有言论及表达自由都会依法受保障，政府内部亦鼓励员工多发表意见。<br> </p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
