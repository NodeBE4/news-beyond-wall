[何柱国辞任星岛主席](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210604-1151862)
------

<div>
<p>香港星岛新闻集团原大股东何柱国昨天(3日)辞任星岛主席和执行董事职务，并将出售股数由原本约2.46亿股增至2.5亿股，每股作价维持1.5元（港元，约0.26新元）。</p><p>根据香港东网报道，何柱国就出售股权交易，跟买方佳兆业集团主席郭英成之女郭晓亭订立上述补充协议。</p><p>跟何柱国一起离开星岛董事会的还有贾红平、刘仲文、卢永雄，以及儿子何正德，接替他们执董位置的是郭英成、郭晓亭和佳兆业高层郑威，郭晓亭任星岛副主席，主席为郭英成。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>同时，何超琼等四名原有独立非执行董事亦已悉数辞任，由胡定旭、蔡加赞及韩永红三人顶上。全部均于周四生效。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
