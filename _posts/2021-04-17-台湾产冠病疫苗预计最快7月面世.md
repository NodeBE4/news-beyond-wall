[台湾产冠病疫苗预计最快7月面世](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210417-1140093)
------

<div>
<p>台湾联亚生技昨宣布完成冠病台湾产疫苗第二期临床试验收案，最后一位收案者为前卫生署长杨志良，有3874人已完成第一剂接种，第二剂接种预计在5月15日完成，盼6月底前提交第二期临床试验报告，并申请紧急授权，最快七月获准，台湾人即可开始接种台湾产疫苗；高端生技目前也预计七月可获准。</p><p>根据联合新闻网报道，联亚生技董事长特助彭文君说，第二期临床试验的核心受试者4142人参与，65岁以上高龄族群有844人，第二期临床试验主要生产三批疫苗，针对18到65岁、65到85岁的族群做验证，超收受试者也是避免第二剂有人放弃不打，影响试验结果。<br> </p>      <section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
