<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年的山东莱阳市柏林庄卫生院退休医生、法轮功修炼者王建敏的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">王建敏：</span></b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">女，<span lang="EN-US">1956</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日出生，山东省莱阳市人，莱阳市柏林庄卫生院退休医生，自由信仰追求者，法轮功信仰者，中国在押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">因笃信法轮功，故而自<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年起，即使用计算机、打印机等设备制作大量法轮功相关宣传品等，随引起中共山东当局的强烈不满，很快将其视为需要严控打压的维稳对象；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2016</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，曾因在某地进行法沦功宣传，而被当地警方知晓，随很快被抓，并被警方所称的“依法处罚”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日，又因在莱阳市联通公司大门东侧邮箱投递法轮功宣传资料信，之后即被莱阳市警方跟踪抓捕；当天，其家亦遭查抄，在搜走其大量私人物品（含各类法轮功宣传数据、计算机、打印机等）后，次日即被莱阳市警方以涉嫌“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”刑拘，送山东省烟台市看守所关押；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，被莱阳市检察院以同罪由正式批捕，指控其因“利用‘法沦功’邪教组织，以制作、传播‘法沦功’宣传品为手段，破坏国家法律实施，情节特别严重”，所以其行为已构成犯罪，应予以逮捕、判刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，因其一直拒不认罪，终被山东省烟台市中级法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">9</span>年，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">其不服上诉，但经法院二审裁决，仍然被“驳回上诉，维持原判”；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2029</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日。目前对其判决已经生效，将送何监狱服刑，暂时不明。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
