[台疫情影响旅游业  有酒店一天被退2000间房](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210514-1146682)
------

<div>
<p>台湾本土疫情严峻引发旅游疑虑，嘉义市旅宿业出现退订潮，有酒店一天退订百辆游览车，等于退订2000间房，业者接退订电话“接到手软”。</p><p>据联合新闻网报道，嘉义市旅馆公会理事长李素珍说，去年疫情曾造成旅宿业停摆数月，之后疫情缓和、搭配政府补助方案，让观光业者终可喘口气，这段时间住宿率都不错，没想到突然爆发本土案例，这几天旅宿业团客几乎已全数退订，散客退订数也大增。</p><p>李素珍说，政府将再延续观光补助政策，旅宿业也建议政府减免相关税金，但她认为政府应有长期、全面性因应对策；当然最重要还是疫情减缓，毕竟再多补助都无法补足观光旅宿基本人事管销、支出。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
