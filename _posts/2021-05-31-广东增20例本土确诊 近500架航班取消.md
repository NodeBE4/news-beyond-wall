[广东增20例本土确诊 近500架航班取消](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210531-1150657)
------

<div>
<p>中国广东昨天新增本土确诊病例20例，其中广州18例，佛山报告两例。广州与深圳近500架次的航班因此被取消。</p><p>综合新华社与界面新闻报道，广东省卫生健康委员会今天31日通报，广东省新增境外输入确诊病例三例，均在广州，分别来自柬埔寨、印度尼西亚和坦桑尼亚。新增境外输入无症状感染者三例，广州报告两例，分别来自中非和阿联酋；佛山报告一例，来自法国。新增出院两例。</p><p>截至5月30日24时，广东省累计确诊病例2455例，目前在院64例。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>据飞常准app官方微博今天的消息，截至今日9时，广州白云机场累计取消航班304架次，深圳宝安机场累计取消航班184架次。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
