[台积电上月营收或创新高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210409-1138154)
------

<div>
<p>台积电今日将公告上月营收，预计可望挑战单月新高，外资在营收公布前连四买，似有提前卡位迹象，同时市场也高度关注下周将登场的法说会。</p><p>根据台湾《经济日报》报道，台积电早盘以618元（新台币，20新元）开高，上涨5元，助攻台股早盘一度冲上17000点之上，不过，随着台积电盘中翻黑，大盘也翻黑。</p><p>台积电预估今年第一季营收127至130亿美元，约合新台币营收3549.65至3633.5亿元；台积电累计前两月合并营收2332.83亿元，市场以第一季财测来推估，台积电上月合并营收约新台币1216.82至1300.67亿元就可达标。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>美系外资报告看好台积电后市，预估最快在2024至2025年营收可达900亿美元，可望成为全球半导体产业营收最大；美系外资预估台积电第一季营收将达3617亿元（新台币，下同），毛利率51.5%，重申买进评等，目标价维持上看900元。</p><p>台积电单月营收历史新高落在去年9月合并营收1275.85亿元，法人看好台积电三月营收可望再创新高；外资近期持续买超台积电，统计已连四买，台积电ADR周四收涨近3％，今日以618元开高，盘中翻黑，一度下滑至609元，盘中跌幅0.16%。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
