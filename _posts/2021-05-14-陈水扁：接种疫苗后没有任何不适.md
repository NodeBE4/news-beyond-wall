[陈水扁：接种疫苗后没有任何不适](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210514-1146689)
------

<div>
<p>台湾疫情升温，政府呼吁民众尽快打疫苗自我保护。台湾前总统陈水扁日前表示要到到医院打疫苗，增加群体免疫力，他昨天完成接种后表示，身体没有任何不适。</p><p>陈水扁昨晚在面簿上说，在高雄长庚完成疫苗接种四部曲，包括“问诊、填表、准备、注射”，经30分钟休息观察，确认身体无恙后再离开，并称“完全没有不舒服的感觉，感谢政府”。</p><p>据此前报道，陈水扁周一在面簿贴出“全身贴满电线”近况照片，称因重度睡眠呼吸中止症，到医院检查，照片引起关注。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>由于近期台湾本土确诊人数创新高，陈水扁在经医疗团队评估身体状况后，安排在昨天打疫苗。<br> </p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
