[苏贞昌告诉林佳龙：现在不谈请辞一事](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210408-1137921)
------

<div>
<p>今天是台铁事故罹难者“头七”，台湾行政院长苏贞昌请交通部长林佳龙代表前往花莲致哀，并通电话，告诉林现阶段还是要把善后工作做好，现在不谈这个（请辞一事）。</p><p>据联合新闻网报道，林佳龙在列车事故发生当天傍晚，即透过面簿发文，表达会在善后工作后负起政治责任，后续也透过电话，口头向蔡英文、苏贞昌请辞。</p><p>对此，台湾行政院发言人罗秉成证实表示，苏贞昌昨天有收到林佳龙的书面请辞，更早之前，林也向苏贞昌口头请辞，苏贞昌在第一时间也已有表示。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>罗秉成转述，苏贞昌今天和林佳龙通电话，并告诉林，现阶段还是要把后续善后工作做好，“我们现在不谈这个（请辞）”，盼台湾民众也能支持，此刻应该专注在救灾救难、抢救、抢通和关怀照顾事宜上。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
