[人权捍卫者、艺术家追魂（刘进兴）服刑1年8个月后 今刑满出狱](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/01/18.html)

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：今天人权捍卫者、艺术家追魂（刘进兴）服刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月后 今刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">追魂<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年六四三十周年前夕发起“良心运动在中国”被南京玄武区法院以寻衅滋事罪判处刑罚，历经<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月今天出狱。追魂有“良心艺术家”之称。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日在南京被以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留，羁押<span lang="EN-US">14</span>个多月后，于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，在南京市玄武区法院锁金村第一法庭开庭审理，且以疫情为由法院只通过视频开庭。于<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日作出判决，判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月。 <span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">刘进兴：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"> 1972</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月出生，湖北省黄石市人，网名“追魂”，民间艺术家，维权公民。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2000</span>年入驻北京市宋庄艺术家小区以来，主要从事架上油画、行为艺术创作等艺术活动；曾因追求民主自由理念，同情关注和接待众多访民，组织策划具有个性特色的艺术作品展览而屡遭当局骚扰和被监视居住，当局“敏感日”被强制旅游，且被捕入狱三次；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，曾因组织策划“河床自发艺术节”，捍卫艺术家自由表达权而被当地警方行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，曾因组织策划“敏感地带”宋庄“个别人”行为艺术展，展览作品涉及太阳花运动和其他敏感作品，而被当局封展，同时因维护艺术家自由创作和表达权而被刑拘<span lang="EN-US">30</span>天，取保候审<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，曾因参与帮助艺术家邝老五行为艺术“废除劳教，释放艺术家”，而被警方以涉嫌“聚众扰乱社会公共秩序罪”刑拘<span lang="EN-US">30</span>天，取保候审<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，曾因声援香港占中、关注粟宪庭的独立影像展被取消活动等，而再次被北京警方传唤抄家，后被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，被关押在北京市通州区看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日被正式逮捕，至<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">9</span>日被取保候审获释；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日（六四<span lang="EN-US">30</span>周年纪念日前夕），去南京与另<span lang="EN-US">5</span>位宋庄艺术家在良心运动在中国巡展途中被失联；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，被南京市玄武区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，其北京的住处被查抄，搜走所有作品等；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，其案在南京玄武区法院经远程视频正式开庭，至今未予宣判；据悉，其在法庭上做了最后陈述，表明“不食牢饭”；<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">7</span>日又悉，其家属接到狱方通知，称其因狱中绝食抗争，身体状况令人堪忧。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日作出判决，判刑<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月。被羁押于南京市第三看守所。今刑满出狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
