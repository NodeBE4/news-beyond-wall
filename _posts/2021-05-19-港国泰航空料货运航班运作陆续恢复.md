[港国泰航空料货运航班运作陆续恢复](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210518-1147686)
------

<div>
<p>香港主要航空公司国泰航空周二说，已有超过80%的驻港机师及逾40%的驻港机舱服务员预约或已接种疫苗，随着更多机组人员全面接种疫苗，预期5月的货运航班运作将陆续恢复；但客运方面则仍然未见需求有实质改善的迹象。</p><p>据路透社报道，国泰航空说，因全球疫情持续，香港及多个巿场实施旅游限制及检疫措施，公司4月份因应需求疲弱而继续大幅削减运力。月内载客2.24万人次，同比上升63.2%，但相比2019年4月疫情前的水平则大跌99.3%。</p><p>至于货运方面，国泰航空4月份运载7.31万公吨货物及邮件，按年下跌13.6%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
