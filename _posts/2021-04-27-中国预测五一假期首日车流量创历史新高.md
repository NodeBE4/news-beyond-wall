[中国预测五一假期首日车流量创历史新高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210427-1142565)
------

<div>
<p>中国政府预测，当地在今年五一假期首日车流量有望突破6000万辆，达到历史新高。</p><p>据路透社报道，2019年5月1日的车流量为5700万辆。</p><p>另据中国央视新闻报道，交通运输部路网中心在今天（27日）发布的《2021五一假期全国路网运行研判》中，预计劳动节假期全国高速公路网日均流量预计5250万至5450万辆，较2020年同期（4529万辆）增长16%至20%，较2019年同期（5148万辆）增长2%至6%。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>交通运输部路网中心也预计，今年劳动节期间全国公路出行以旅游、探亲为主，中短途与长途交织。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
