[中国或立法禁违背农民意愿撤并村庄](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210422-1141310)
------

<div>
<p>中国将可能立法严禁违背农民意愿、违反法定程序撤并村庄。</p><p>据中国澎湃新闻报道，全国人大常委会法制工作委员会发言人臧铁伟今天（22日）在记者会上介绍，乡村振兴促进法草案拟充实支持城乡融合发展的内容，严格规范村庄撤并等行为。</p><p>臧铁伟指出，经过审议和修改的草案，也将进一步强调保障国家粮食安全的重要性，并增加防止耕地“非农化、非粮化”以及保障种子安全等内容，并且加大乡村振兴的支持保障力度，完善巩固拓展脱贫攻坚成果同乡村振兴有效衔接的规定。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>全国人大常委会将从本月26日（周一）至29日在北京召开会议，而乡村振兴促进法草案将提请本次会议继续审议。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
