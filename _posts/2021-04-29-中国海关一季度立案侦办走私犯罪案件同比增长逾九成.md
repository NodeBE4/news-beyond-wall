[中国海关一季度立案侦办走私犯罪案件同比增长逾九成](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142774)
------

<div>
<p>中国海关缉私部门一季度共立案侦办走私犯罪案件1058起，同比增长92.7％。</p><p>根据中国海关总署网站消息，海关缉私部门今年前三个月立案侦办涉税走私犯罪案件718起，案值244亿元（人民币，下同，49.91亿新元），同比分别增长144.2％、287.4％。</p><p>在中国海关总署缉私局统一部署下，南宁海关缉私局近日联合广西烟草部门开展打击香烟走私专项行动，打掉香烟走私团伙两个，抓获犯罪嫌疑人11名，端掉走私香烟囤积窝点1个，现场查扣走私香烟2400余条。初步查证，上述走私团伙共涉嫌走私香烟约1.2万件，案值约8000万元。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>海关缉私部门一季度立案侦办非涉税走私犯罪案340起，同比增长33.3%。</p><p>其中，侦办濒危物种及其制品走私案件64起，同比上升16.4％。侦办毒品走私犯罪案件153起，缴获可卡因、冰毒等毒品458.47公斤，查处案件数增长71.9％、缴毒数增长近五倍。截至目前，昆明海关缉私局连续查获“3.03”“3.17”“3.30”走私毒品案，缴获冰毒片剂18.92公斤，抓获犯罪嫌疑人四人。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
