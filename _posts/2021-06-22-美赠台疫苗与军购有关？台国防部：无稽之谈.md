[美赠台疫苗与军购有关？台国防部：无稽之谈](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210622-1159303)
------

<div>
<p>针对网传美国赠台湾250万剂冠病疫苗疑与军购案有关的消息，台湾国防部昨天回应，这属无稽之谈、毫无相干；至于据报美国售台鱼叉导弹要价恐破千亿元（新台币，下同，约48亿新元），国防部说，根据汇率换算约712亿元，无要价破千亿情事。</p><p>据联合新闻网昨天报道，美国售台鱼叉导弹要价恐破千亿元，国防部昨晚回应，美国去年10月已同意对台对售100套、400枚鱼叉导弹海岸防卫系统，知会国会的上限金额为23.7亿美元，以今年计划汇率30.05元换算，约新台币712亿元，并无要价破千亿元的情事。</p><p>据中时新闻网报道，国防部也说，网传美国捐赠台湾250万剂莫德纳疫苗，疑与军购案有关的消息，则属无稽之谈，毫无相干。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>国防部强调，台军所有对外军事採购均符合国土防卫需求，并依法依规定执行，且完整公告于台、美官方网站。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
