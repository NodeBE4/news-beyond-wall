[河南维权人士李玉凤女士因言获罪案已经诉至法院](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/09/blog-post_95.html)
------

<div>
<p><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;"><span></span></span></p><a name='more'></a>&nbsp;<p></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，本网获悉：河南维权人士李玉凤女士因言获罪案已经诉至法院。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据其代理律师信息：李玉凤<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日被本地警方直接在北京抓获，此后羁押至今，现已诉至中站区法院。当局对其指控的所谓犯罪事实一是接受外媒采访，发表关于监狱待遇的不实言论；一是扰乱某法院的工作秩序。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">李玉凤：女，<span lang="EN-US">1958</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日出生，河南省修武县人，基督徒，维权公民，中国在押维权人士。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1990</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代末，曾因家中惨遭两次政府不当拆迁，损失巨大，其被迫提起诉讼，打赢了官司，但因被告是当地政府，法院不予执行，故而其被迫上访维权；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">曾因不断维权，被当局进行恶意打击报复，并以“妨害公务罪”对其判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年；出狱后其继续状告地方政府，再遭逼迫，又被劳动教养<span lang="EN-US">1</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；两次入狱，备受酷刑折磨。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日因与他人前往监狱迎接“新公民运动”维权代表袁冬出狱，并率先打出横幅“欢迎袁冬从小监狱回到大监狱”，又被北京大兴区警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘，同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日被北京市大兴区检察院批捕，至<span lang="EN-US">2015</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日被取保候审释放；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">关押期间，曾被突击审讯（且大多是疲劳审讯）达<span lang="EN-US">50</span>余次，身心惨遭迫害；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日，又因在北京南站照相被北京市丰台公安刑拘一个月后，交由焦作市当局带回原籍，补充侦查再次被刑拘，后转正式逮捕，被关押在河南省焦作看守所；<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被河南省焦作市中站区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年，并在郑州女子监狱服刑；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，刑满释放；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">5</span>日，因其与同伴封西霞到北京最高法院申诉，又到河南省法院驻京办事处反映司法腐败制造冤假错案问题，随遭焦作维稳办人员抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日，被焦作市警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”正式逮捕。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月，案件已经被移送至法院。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于河南省焦作市看守所。</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对李玉凤女士的境况，本网将持续关注。</span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
