<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同时，许多赴上海声援张展案女士的维权人士被上海警方强行带走。<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日凌晨<span lang="EN-US">0</span>时许，上海彭永和律师被四名警察从友人住的酒店带走。警方称是对彭永和口头传唤。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">而维权人士李大伟、日本记者，上海维权人士毛恒风和丁德元均被抓捕，据维权人士毛恒凤说：“我今天上午去上海浦东法院，被警察抓捕到世纪广场治安派出所，看见关在一起共九个人。上午十点左右被抓。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">张展案<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日上午在上海开庭，开庭后仅两小时内，法官当庭做出判决。根据张展辩护律师任全牛的消息，法庭判张展四年有期徒刑。根据任全牛律师分享的现场录音，张展的母亲在法庭宣判结果后，在庭外放声大哭。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">6</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，被浦东新区检察院予以批捕；</span><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">9</span><span style="font-family: 宋体;
