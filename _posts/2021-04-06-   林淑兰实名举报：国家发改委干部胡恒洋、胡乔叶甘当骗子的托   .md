<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，吉林省敦化市大石头镇居民林淑兰通过邮寄，向中华人民共和国发展和改革委员会举报胡恒洋、胡乔叶甘当骗子的托，导致骗子行骗得逞。林淑兰请求国家发改委依法查处被举报人胡恒洋、胡乔叶（简称二胡）以考察林淑兰经营的空心砖厂为名，获取不当利益之违法违纪行为。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在此之前，林淑兰曾经连续向有关纪委发了十几封举报信，举报二胡的违法行为，皆石沉大海，渺无音讯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">经朋友的朋友介绍，林淑兰认识了王倩楠和高雪晶。王倩楠和高雪晶告诉林淑兰，国家发改委干部胡恒洋、胡乔叶（简称二胡）可以帮林淑兰经营的空心砖厂申请到环保补贴项目。林淑兰当时有些疑惑。高雪晶为消除林淑兰的疑惑，借二胡以国家发改委的名义到吉林省舒兰市调研农业项目之际，于<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日上午<span lang="EN-US">11</span>点许，将二胡等十三人领到敦化林淑兰经营的空心专场考察。林淑兰才信以为真。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">二胡在空心砖厂煞有介事地考察一个多小时，然后到敦化市韩城狗肉馆就餐，还收受了林淑兰赠送的两箱闷倒驴酒。饭后，林淑兰安排二胡等<span lang="EN-US">13</span>人送到敦化市财政宾馆休息，下午还去敦化市北山游玩；傍晚时，乘兴到敦化市金水湾度假村吃烤全羊。当时正赶上蒙牛集团在此举行篝火晚会，二胡即兴高唱《咱当兵的人》等歌曲。席间，胡恒洋信誓旦旦地向林淑兰承诺说：“林大姐，我下次再来时给你送钱来，让你进入延边州<span lang="EN-US">500</span>强”。林淑兰深受感动，还赠送高级礼品，两天便花销七万多元，连往返机票，都给予报销。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林淑兰对二胡的承诺深信不疑，信赖有加，才签署了《联合申报项目协议书》，以至于被高雪晶诈骗钱物<span lang="EN-US">100</span>多万元，导致林淑兰的空心砖厂债台高筑，岌岌可危，难以为继，甚至于家破人亡。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">高雪晶因诈骗而判刑，有刑事判决书为证。林淑兰认为高雪晶的诈骗之所以能得逞，得益于二胡相助；二胡为获取收受礼品、游山玩水、吃吃喝喝等不当利益甘心充当骗子的托，有损政府公信力，造成了恶劣的社会影响。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林淑兰希望国家发改委查清事实，依法处理，维护法律的尊严。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">林淑兰手机：<span lang="EN-US">13614435444</span>。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
