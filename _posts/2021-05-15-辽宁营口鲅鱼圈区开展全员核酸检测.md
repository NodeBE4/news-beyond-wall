[辽宁营口鲅鱼圈区开展全员核酸检测](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210515-1146947)
------

<div>
<p>辽宁省昨天新增两例本土确诊病例，新增三例本土无症状感染病例，均为营口市报告。营口市鲅鱼圈区现已开展全员核酸检测。</p><p>据中国央视新闻报道，辽宁营口市鲅鱼圈区疫情指挥部昨晚发布统筹推进疫情防控和经济社会发展工作指挥部令。</p><p>通知要求，即日起在鲅鱼圈区范围开展全员核酸检测，各镇(街道)作为主体，要以人员实际居住小区为单位，扎实做好排查登记、信息录入、采样检测等工作，不漏一人。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>通知要求，落实24小时人员值守，外来人员逐一扫码、测温、登记，尤其对重点人员居住小区要严格执行封闭管控。对于不遵守防控要求，引起疫情传播或造成传播风险的，将依法追究法律责任。</p><p>自发布之时起，鲅鱼圈区域内车辆、人员无特殊原因不得离区；确需离区的，需持72小时内核酸检测阴性证明。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
