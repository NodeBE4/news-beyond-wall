[中国广电总局：不给处于创作期的电视剧贴标签](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210428-1142816)
------

<div>
<p>中国国家广电总局电视剧司司长高长力28日说，广电总局将不再公布任何片单，不给处于创作期的作品贴标签。</p><p>据中新社报道，高长力在浙江杭州参加论坛时说，“十四五”期间，历史剧创作是中国电视剧发展的重要组成部分。</p><p>高长力强调，当前历史题材作品创作相对薄弱。广电总局正在大力推进重大革命、重大历史题材电视剧创作。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>他还提到，高质量电视剧作品的创作中，编剧是重要的一环。“未来，抓电视剧精品创作，就要牢牢抓住编剧这个环节。”当前缺少能够直击现实问题的编剧。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
