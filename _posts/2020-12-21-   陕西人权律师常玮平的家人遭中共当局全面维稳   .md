[陕西人权律师常玮平的家人遭中共当局全面维稳](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/12/blog-post_57.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉：自常玮平第二次遭指定居所监视居住以来，因家人持续为常玮平发声，被中共当局全方位维稳。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自常玮平父母<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">14</span>日高新分局举牌后，常玮平父母被以涉嫌“寻衅滋事”罪名多次传唤。常父亲说他被高新分局局长孟麦续从头骂到尾，并多次威胁：“你所做的事都是受你儿媳妇指使的，要对你儿媳妇采取强制措施。”这些话让常平父母非常担心，担心儿媳妇和玮平有同样遭遇，他们的孙子没人管。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日晚上，常玮平父母凤翔县老家的斜对门被装上了高清摄像头，正对他们家，村委会里设有监控室，由镇里的派出所多名警察实时监控。只要家里有人来探望，警察就会飞奔出现，对来人就行训斥盘问，要求他们登记身份证号码、工作单位，言辞间满是威胁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">与此同时，常玮平父母的自由也受到非法限制，手机被高新分局非法收走，无法与外界联系。常玮平的二姐和二姐夫在本县当老师，上周被县教育局约谈三次。这两天他二姐夫被县教育局安排全天<span lang="EN-US">24</span>小时在常玮平父母家“上班”，负责对常玮平父母的活动进行上报。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玮平的大姐夫一家也被威胁不得参与此事，否则影响他们儿子上大学考公务员。他大姐夫的手机也被非法收走。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">常玮平的岳父也被收走手机，限制在家，由专人看守，出门需要打报告。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
