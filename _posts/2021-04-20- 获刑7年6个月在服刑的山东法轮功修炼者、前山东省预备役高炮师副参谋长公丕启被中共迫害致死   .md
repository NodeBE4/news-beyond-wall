[获刑7年6个月在服刑的山东法轮功修炼者、前山东省预备役高炮师副参谋长公丕启被中共迫害致死](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/04/76_20.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月在服刑的山东法轮功修炼者、前山东省预备役高炮师副参谋长公丕启日前被中共当局迫害致死。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">公丕启：<span lang="EN-US">1955</span>年出生，山东省青岛市人，前山东省预备役高炮师副参谋长、退休上校、正团职军官，自由信仰追求者，法轮功修炼者，中国曾押良心犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1999</span>年信仰、锻炼法轮功以来，深受中共当局的打压逼迫；曾因其家人及其本人均锻炼法轮功之故而被迫提前“退休”，并遭多次威胁，被当局用开除“党籍”、撤销职务及工资待遇、收回住房、开除孩子学籍、投入监狱等方式胁迫其放弃修炼；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2005</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，曾与妻子在家中被青岛市警方以邪教罪由拘捕，后其妻被枉法判刑<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年，其虽未被判刑，但被关押在部队接受洗脑再教育，其女儿在外上学也因此断绝了生活来源，无家可归；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">关押期间，因拒绝放弃信仰而被长期关小屋，遭受酷刑虐待，直至在经历了<span lang="EN-US">7</span>个半月的磨难之后才得以释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">后因其仍然坚持信仰不动摇，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，因其去看望一名同有法轮功信仰的老者（名：宿桂花，时年<span lang="EN-US">70</span>岁），返回时被早已蹲守在其家附近的青岛市市北区警方以涉嫌“邪教罪”再次刑拘，家被查抄；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被市北区检察院以同罪名予以批捕，并关押于青岛市即墨普东看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，其案被青岛市市北区法院在青岛市即墨普东看守所内开庭受审，同年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，被以“邪教罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">7</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">2</span>万元；其不服上诉；后经青岛市中级法院二审裁决，依然维持原判，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2025</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日晚，其家属突接山东省济南监狱电话被告知，其因突发脑溢血被送医院抢救，但最终抢救无效已死亡；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，当其家人及亲友赶到济南中心医院要求探视遗体时，遭拒；又据见过其遗体的到家人透露，其遗体头部肿胀、湿漉、有伤，且耳朵有血流出，故觉其突然死亡疑点重重，应是被济南监狱迫害致死。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <span style="font-family: 宋体;
