<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：获刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年的“恶俗维基”站长、运维牛腾宇案二审进展情况，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">相关卷宗中，牛腾宇一直控诉自己在茂名茂坡派出所期间和佛山市竹安园<span lang="EN-US">103</span>号指监期间遭受严酷的刑讯逼供。孩子的控诉真是可怜！我们也代其向各级部门提起了控告。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">牛腾宇：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1999</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日出生，山西省晋城市高平市人，有“计算机天才”之称，恶俗维基服务器运维技术人员，“恶俗维基”站长，“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”主要受害人，在押中国政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年起，中国大陆及海外有网友开始频繁尝试搭建和运作被称为“恶俗圈”的网络平台，其中“恶俗维基”、“支那维基”和“红岸基金会”三大网站最为知名；此期间，因其对计算机技术的痴迷与天赋，以及网站宗旨是“揭露并记录事实”，随加入“恶俗维基”网站，并成为该网站技术维护员及站长；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因多家“恶俗圈”网站频发习近平及家人、和中共高官及家属的户籍和照片等信息，其所在“恶俗维基”网站亦将此类信息挂于网上，遂引发中共当局的极端不满与监控；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，又因其在香港爆发“反送中”大游行之后，与“恶俗维基”创办人肖彦锐相约前去香港，对香港大游行进行围观了解，返回后虽又于同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月初至<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日到日本一游，但回国一周后；<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其网站全部会员尽被各地警方突然抓捕，其亦被广东省茂名市警方抓捕，并遭毒打；同年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，被以涉嫌“帮助信息网络犯罪活动罪”刑拘，关押于茂名市第一看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日，被茂名市检察院以同罪名予以批捕，指控其曾在恶俗维基网上“共发表评论<span lang="EN-US">8</span>条，上传图片<span lang="EN-US">20</span>张，共有<span lang="EN-US">748</span>条的用户贡献，其本人有<span lang="EN-US">285</span>条贡献值”等讯息，散布虚假信息，恶毒攻击、诋毁政府和国家领导人及其家属形象等，是“恶俗维基网站恶势力犯罪集团案”主犯，故而应予逮捕和判刑；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，经茂名市检察院批准被延长侦查羁押期限<span lang="EN-US">1</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日，又被广东省佛山市警方以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”被指定监视居住，并转关至佛山市南海区看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其被解除被指定监视居住，同日又被茂名市警方再次逮捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其一行<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人“涉恶势力犯罪集团案”在广东省茂名市法院开庭受审，至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，其案<span lang="EN-US">24</span>人均被宣判，其中，其被以“寻衅滋事罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年）、“侵犯个人信息罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">5</span>年）、“非法经营罪”（判<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年），最终决定执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">13</span>万；其等均表不服，立刻上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
