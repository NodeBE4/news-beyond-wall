font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2.11</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日被拘留，拘留十天里，拆迁办主任江守岩两次带人去拘留所逼我签字拆迁（电话录音证明）。而针对这次拘留行政复议，即墨区法制办于<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">27</span>日受理却又于<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日以期限已过驳回（实际上没有过期）。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">3.12</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">8.2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日下午，我老公在巨大的压力和胁迫下签字，没有上交房权证，第二天上午，两处房子被强拆。<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>到<span lang="EN-US">4</span>号，三天的时间，多人多次去看守所逼举报人在《息诉罢访协议》上签字，我不签字，就不让回家。<span lang="EN-US">9</span>九月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日上午，他们找了管教说服我，下午，我为了活命被迫签字。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
