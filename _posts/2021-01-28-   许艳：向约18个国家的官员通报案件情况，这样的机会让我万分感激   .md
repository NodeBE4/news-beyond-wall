mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">也力所能及的向所有官员，表达了其他请求帮助的事项。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
