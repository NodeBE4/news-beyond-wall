[五一期间入境澳门旅客预计创疫后新高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210430-1143253)
------

<div>
<p>澳门政府预计，内地五一黄金周到访澳门的旅客，会较这个月录得疫情以来单日最高纪录再多两成，各口岸和警方已做好充分准备。</p><p>据香港电台网站报道，治安警说，随着内地和澳门有效控制疫情，近期内地旅客来澳人数增加，3月整体入境旅客约为76万人次，平均每日24500人次，较去年12月高14.6%，而4月18日有36500人次旅客入境，是疫情以来最高，警方会在黄金周假期加强在旅游旺区巡逻，需要时实施人潮管制。</p><p>卫生官员说，内地整体疫情防控形势与澳门相近，相信人员往来不会增加感染风险。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
