[台国安局盗秘帐职员被指潜逃大陆投敌](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210506-1144772)
------

<div>
<p>一名侵占巨额国安公款后潜逃至中国大陆的台湾国安局前职员，被发现涉嫌前往大陆投敌等罪名，台湾国安局对此向台湾高等检察署告发。</p><p>综合台湾《联合报》和《自由时报》报道，被告发的国安局前出纳组长刘冠军是一名上校，在逃到大陆前负责管理机密外交帐户，并利用职务之便，盗走近2亿元（新台币，960万新元）的公款。这起案件2000年曝光，而刘冠军在被约谈前潜逃大陆。</p><p>外界曾推测刘冠军可能潜逃到加拿大，但国安局证实他逃往大陆。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>国安局今早（6日）公布，国安局经搜集有关事证后，认为刘冠军也涉及陆海空军刑法下的“投敌罪”等罪嫌，台湾高等检察署经侦处后发布通缉令。投敌罪罪行最重者可被判处死刑。</p><p>国安局指刘刘冠军涉嫌犯下外患等罪，严重影响情报工作，危害国安甚巨，国安局也尊重检方各项侦处作为。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
