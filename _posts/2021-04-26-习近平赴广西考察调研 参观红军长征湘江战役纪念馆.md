[习近平赴广西考察调研 参观红军长征湘江战役纪念馆](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210426-1142204)
------

<div>
<p>中共总书记、中央军委主席习近平昨早前往位于广西桂林全州县才湾镇的红军长征湘江战役纪念园，向湘江战役红军烈士敬献花篮，同时参观红军长征湘江战役纪念馆，缅怀革命先烈。</p><p>据中新社报道，随后习近平来到才湾镇毛竹山村，考察推进乡村振兴、基层治理等情况。下午，习近平在漓江阳朔段实地了解漓江流域综合治理、生态保护等情况。</p><p>87年前发生于桂北的湘江战役中，数万红军将士向死而生，保全了中央红军。据中国央视报道，习近平曾专门作出批示，明确要求做好湘江战役烈士遗骸收殓保护工作，规划建设好纪念设施。2019年，广西全面完成湘江战役遗址修缮保护、纪念设施建设工作。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
