</span></span>中国公民维权同盟发起人之一田野案情，现在通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>田野：</b><span lang="EN-US">1962</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日出生，<a href="https://www.blogger.com/null" name="_Hlk49546664">吉林省松原市</a>长岭县人，农民企业家，维权公民，中国公民维权同盟发起人之一，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年，曾因在一次银行借贷中被抢走一亿多元，投诉无果随开始上访维权，并屡次遭当局打压；因长期协助困难访民维权而被多次拘捕；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因与数名访民在京请愿而被吉林省长岭县警方刑拘，关押于长岭县看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2013</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日被涉嫌以“重婚罪”正式逮捕；同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，被长岭市长岭县法院以“重婚罪”构陷判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，之后又被加刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月才解除刑期；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2017</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年六四期间，因在北京南站解救其他访民，遭保安殴打，并关押在北京的黑监狱；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日，因与访民孟凡波等六人，身穿印有吉林徒步进京维权等红字的白色文化衫，手持写着“<span lang="EN-US">5</span>·<span lang="EN-US">4-</span>吉林省维权徒步北京行<span lang="EN-US">-</span>中国公民权利”字样的红色旗帜，从长春市出发欲徒步进京，行至吉林省铁岭市时被警方围堵抓捕，后被行政拘留<span lang="EN-US">10</span>日；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">3</span>日田野被长岭县警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，被以同罪名正式逮捕，并关押于松原市长岭县看守所，被控其手机里因存有“反党反社会”言论，故此行为属于违法；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，其案在松原市中级法院开庭审理，被以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">4</span>年；其不服判决，表示上诉；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></div><div class="MsoNormal"><br /></div><div class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
