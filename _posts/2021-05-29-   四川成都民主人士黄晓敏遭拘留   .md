[四川成都民主人士黄晓敏遭拘留](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/05/blog-post_48.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，本网获悉：黄晓敏女儿今天打给朋友电话说：黄晓敏刚刚用警察的电话通知她，因“寻衅滋事罪”被拘留了，最近警方会给她邮寄相关的法律文书。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">黄晓敏：<span lang="EN-US">1962</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日出生，网名“牛鬼的儿子”，河南省新野县籍人，前新疆喀什市疏勒县农三师党校教师，六四民运参与者，自由撰稿人，独立中文笔会会员，四川泛蓝联盟召集人之一。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">早年曾生长于新疆喀什市农三师伽师总厂，后从喀什农学院农学系毕业，就职于新疆喀什农三师党校任教师；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1996</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年辞职后，前往四川省成都市居住至今，<span lang="EN-US">2012</span>年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月成为独立中文笔会会员；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">23</span>日，曾因现场采访鲍俊生等上访人员在四川成都中级法院大门外举行的“链子门”事件抗议示威活动，要求释放<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月被捕的“六四天网”负责人黄琦等，并与友人共同拍录像像，发往海内外媒体刊登，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2009</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被成都市金牛区警方以涉嫌“聚众扰乱社会秩序罪”抓捕、刑拘，同年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日被正式逮捕，并被关押于乐山市看守所；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2010</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日，被乐山市市中区法院一审以同罪名判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，二审依然维持原判，刑至<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">8</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日被释放；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">出狱后，因继续参与维权活动，接受外媒采访，在网上发表有关民主维权文章，<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">-5</span>月，又因积极介入了云南省委党校退休教师子肃发表公开信要求中共<span lang="EN-US">19</span>大开放直选、选举胡德平为总书记事件，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">18</span>日被成都警方秘密绑架，自此失联，家被查抄；其家人为知其下落被迫做寻人启事，后得知其早已被成都警方以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，其案被成都市金牛区检察院起诉至金牛区法院；至<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，被成都市金牛区法院以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，刑满释放。此前被羁押于成都市看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast;
