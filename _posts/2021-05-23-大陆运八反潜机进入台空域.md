[大陆运八反潜机进入台空域](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210523-1148823)
------

<div>
<p>中国大陆一架运8反潜机昨日（22日）进入台湾西南防空识别区（ADIZ）。今日大陆军机再次进入台湾空域。</p><p>据联合网报道，台湾空军昨日发布的大陆军机动态显示，一架运8反潜机下午进入台湾西南防空识别区（ADIZ），但未进入台湾东南海空域，也未逾越台湾海峡中线。对此，台湾空军派遣空中巡逻兵力应对、广播驱离、防空飞弹追踪监控。</p><p>另据《自由时报》报道，今日大陆军机再次进入台湾西南防空识别区。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>“台湾西南空域”粉丝专页记录，大陆军机本月累计进入台湾空域20天，台湾空军本月广播驱离纪录共计40次。今年大陆军机累计进入台湾空域120天，广播驱离纪录共计343次。台湾国防部公布数据显示，今年大陆军机累计进入台湾空域98天，共307架次，本月已达27架次。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
