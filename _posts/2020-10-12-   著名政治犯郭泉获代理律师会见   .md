[著名政治犯郭泉获代理律师会见](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/10/blog-post_13.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，本网获悉：昨天著名政治犯郭泉获与其代理律师斯伟江会见。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">斯伟江律师通报说：<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日在南京市第二看守所，会见郭泉。上次在这里见到他是十几年前，<span lang="EN-US">2008</span>年他被控颠覆国家政权，被判十年，<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年出狱。这次在年初疫情期间被抓，以煽动颠覆国家政权罪 在徐州市法院审理。对他指控，主要是微信上他的郭泉语录。郭泉满面红光，穿个短袖，身体健康。郭泉声言不惧坐牢。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>郭泉：</b><span lang="EN-US">1968</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日出生，江苏省南京市人，原南京师范大学副教授、研究生导师，中国大陆新民党创始人及代理主席，政治犯。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2007</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年曾因发表公开信要求中国进行宪政改革，宣布组建“中国新民党”，主张在中国实行多党制和军队国家化等，遭受到中共当局的打压，<span lang="EN-US">2007</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">6</span>日，被南京师范大学党委以违反宪法和教师法为由撤销其副教授职务，调任数据员；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月，又因撰文批评中国政府处理四川地震的方式不当而被拘押<span lang="EN-US">10</span>天；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">13</span>日，再次因其在网上发表<span lang="EN-US">300</span>多篇名为《民主先声》的文章，其中涉及组成以宣传民主思想为目标的新民党，以及提出以不合作方式进行公民行动的蓝色运动倡议等内容，被南京市警方刑事拘留；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日，被江苏省宿迁市中级人民法院以“颠覆国家政权罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">10</span>年，剥夺政治权利<span lang="EN-US">3</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2018</span>年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">12</span>日，刑满释放。此前在江苏省南京市浦口监狱服刑。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp; &nbsp; &nbsp;&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">1</span>月<span lang="EN-US">31</span>日，郭泉因关注湖北武汉疫情，为疫区人民发声而遭中共当局抓捕，随即被刑事拘留。后又被以涉嫌“煽动颠覆国家政权”正式批捕。</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
