<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：<span lang="EN-US">"</span>泼墨女<span lang="EN-US">"</span>董瑶琼<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">30</span>日在<span lang="EN-US">Twitter</span>发布最新视频控诉<span lang="EN-US">"</span>被精神病<span lang="EN-US">"</span>后遭全面监控的情况，随后遭当地国保上门，随后其发的三个推文已经全部消失。本人已被当局控制。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1989</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日出生，湖南省株洲市攸县桃水镇人，原上海房地产公司中介职员，推特名：<span lang="EN-US">feefeefly</span>，人称“泼墨女孩”，政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体;
