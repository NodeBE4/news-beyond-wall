[获刑11年的藏族环保人士边巴杰布的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2020/10/11_24.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年的藏族环保人士边巴杰布的案情，现通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">边巴杰布：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1980</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年代出生，又称边巴坚参，西藏自治区拉萨市林周县人，拉萨市林周县旁多乡村民，职业不详，生态环境保护者，藏族人权捍卫者，中国在押维权人士。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自<span lang="EN-US">1998</span>年始，中共西藏当局即在西藏拉萨所辖的林周县旁多乡境内对各种矿产资源进行初步勘探，并为防止当地藏人进行抗议，曾特意宣布将为无业藏人提供工作机会等，但在正式开工采矿，且顺利修建“旁多水利枢纽工程”之后，当局不仅不兑现之前的承诺，反而随意开采矿区，使得当地生态环境遭受严重破坏；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">迫于无耐，当地藏人遂多次向相关部门提出反映与投诉，但毫无效果；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2009</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月，西藏当局为确保水利工程及采矿等计划如期举行，开始强令旁多乡<span lang="EN-US">119</span>户（近<span lang="EN-US">700</span>名）村民集体搬迁；后因当地藏民拒绝搬迁，数次向主管部门提出长久居住要求，均无果效，随于<span lang="EN-US">2011</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月，被迫开展和平抗议活动，以期借此拦阻当局破坏生态环境的采矿项目，但又遭到中共军警的残酷镇压；</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12pt;">&nbsp;</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">很快，边巴杰布及另<span lang="EN-US">5</span>名维权村民代表被拉萨市警方野蛮抓捕，自此失联；</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2012</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，边巴杰布家属获悉，在被秘押长达一年多之久后，其一行<span lang="EN-US">6</span>人“请愿游行案”在拉萨市中级法院开庭受理，均被以“试图分裂西藏，并组织领导了以保护环境为名的抗议活动”罪由重判<span lang="EN-US">8-12</span>年不等刑期，其中，边巴杰布与另两名维权藏人（曲嘉唯色、阿旺益西）分别被秘密判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">11</span>年，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2022</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前在西藏自治区第一监狱（原拉萨市扎其监狱）服刑。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p>
