<div>
<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，本网获悉广州<span lang="EN-US">11.21</span>大抓捕案之胡天峰的案情，现通报如下：<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">胡天峰</span></b><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">：<span lang="EN-US">1981</span>年出生，河北省人，深圳市居民，前中国武警部队成员，前缅北雇佣军人，原深圳市某派出所警员，民主维权公民，人权捍卫者，中国在押政治犯。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">自幼父母离异，跟随母亲在颠沛流离中长大；<span lang="EN-US">2002</span>年，曾因母亲拔牙发生医疗事故，在家乡求告无门的情况下踏上了茫茫维权之路；维权期间，曾因到北京国家信访局投诉时，在信访局门口竟遭到五六个警卫暴打，随愤而参加了中国武警部队，以期能锻炼出强健的体魄为母亲的不公遭遇讨回公道；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">在部队期间，曾因听闻缅甸军方在中国边境制造恐怖袭击，炸死无数中国边境人员，而中共当局却为面子和所谓团结，不对此类事件加以重视或回击，其遂聚集了数名热血青年毅然前往边境，希望以此为国效力；但当看到实际情况后，感觉有心无力，随改变认识，毅然加入缅北雇佣军，在热带雨林里与缅军对峙八个月；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">回国后，<span lang="EN-US">2004</span>年，又在深圳市某派出所做警员，期间结识众多仁人志士，并曾对许多陷入生活窘困的民主维权人士慷慨解囊；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">辞职后，因积极践行民主自由理念，不断为底层百姓不平之事呐喊、为被拐卖儿童奔波、为国家免费教育呼吁，遂遭致中共广东当局的不满与维稳打压；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2020</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">21</span>日，</span><span face="宋体">胡天峰</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">被广州市警方突然从家中带走，自此失踪；</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">2021</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">年</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">1</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">月</span><span face="宋体" lang="EN-US" style="font-size: 12pt;">27</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;">日，被广州市国家安全局以涉嫌“颠覆国家政权罪”转为刑拘，至今尚无更多确切讯息。目前被羁押于广州市国家安全局看守所。</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">对广州<span lang="EN-US">11.21</span>大抓捕案之进展，本网将持续关注。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
