[在新疆伊犁新源县哈拉布拉监狱内服刑的新疆博乐残疾维权人士姜志林已病危](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/05/blog-post_22.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，本网获悉：上个月，在新疆伊犁新源县哈拉布拉监狱内服刑的新疆博乐残疾维权人士姜志林就已失去说话的能力，现在已经病危。<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">10</span>月，姜志林被新疆博乐市法院秘密庭审以“敲诈勒索罪”、“诬陷罪”重判有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">8</span>年。<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">26</span>日传出消息，姜志林已不能言语，体重有三十多公斤，母亲要了解病情被粗暴拒绝。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">姜志林是<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年年<span lang="EN-US">4</span>月<span lang="EN-US">24</span>日被新疆博乐警方抓捕的，于<span lang="EN-US">2017</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">8</span>日被当地检察院以“敲诈勒索罪”批准逮捕，当时他的母亲也遭软禁失联。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">据悉，姜志林下肢残疾，在博乐市务农。因自治区水利厅新水饮《<span lang="EN-US">2010</span>》<span lang="EN-US">65</span>号文件不落实，造成村民长期用水困难。姜志林为此逐级上访反映，均不但得不到解决，反而多次遭到威胁、殴打、拘留。之前姜志林因为举报村主任贪腐，被警方一再迫害打击，拘留多次，上访拦截抓捕，当地政府一直企图把姜志林构陷入狱。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">姜志林热心公益，经常以 “南国林子”的网名转发微博。他身残志坚，曾在小安妮失学事件中，大力呼吁网民关注，还不断为入狱的郭飞雄等良心犯呼吁。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
