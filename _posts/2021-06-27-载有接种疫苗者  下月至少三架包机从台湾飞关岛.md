[载有接种疫苗者  下月至少三架包机从台湾飞关岛](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210627-1161453)
------

<div>
<p>关岛旅游局主席古提瑞兹（Carl Gutierrez）透露，7月初至少会有三架从台湾飞关岛的包机，将载超过500人到关岛度假及接种疫苗，还有其他航班在洽谈中。</p><p>据联合新闻网引述关岛每日邮报（The Guam Daily Post）报道，关岛旅游局表示，这是疫情影响关岛观光以来，首次和观光旅游有关的包机飞到关岛，古提瑞兹并透露三架包机预计从台湾起飞的时间为7月6日、10日及14日，旅行社为凤凰旅游，航空公司是长荣航空，每架包机约载170位乘客。</p><p>关岛每日邮报的报道指，关岛旅游局计划放宽游客防疫措施，让想到关岛打疫苗的观光客所需付出的成本下降，藉此吸引更多人到关岛施打疫苗。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section><p>报道指出，疫苗旅游团需要额外的880美元费用，用于三项核酸检测及饭店房价，让一些潜在旅客望之却步，关岛旅游局希望能在7月初取消这项措施，让到关岛打疫苗旅客的花费大大降低。</p>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
