[中国最大铜冶炼企业预测铜价将达历史新高](https://www.zaobao.com/realtime/china/story20210425-1142025)
------

<div>
<p>中国最大铜冶炼商江西铜业预计，铜对绿色能源转型至关重要，全球经济复苏以及削减碳排放措施料提振铜需求，铜价势将逼近历史新高。</p><p>据彭博社报道，江西铜业副总经理陈羽年周四在上海一个行业会议上说，市场基本面对铜价有利，相信可以达到每吨10000美元（13269.95新元）。他没有就此给出时间表，而是列举了中国第一季度GDP，和全球流动性宽松环境下世界其他地区的经济复苏。</p><p>尽管江西铜业的铜价预测仅、略低于10190美元的历史纪录，但其他市场观察人士预计，铜价将突破这一大关，并在未来几年大幅上涨。</p><section id="imu"><div id="dfp-ad-imu1">        </div></section>      <div id="innity-in-post"></div><div id="dfp-ad-midarticlespecial">        </div>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-desktop lazyload  " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" alt style="width:100%;" pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-desktop-l_0_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>      <a href="https://subscribe.sph.com.sg/promotions/chinese-3-months-99-cents-promotion/?utm_campaign=cn3in199c&utm_medium=sph-publication&utm_source=zb&utm_content=subscribelink-free-endofarticle" target="_blank"><img class="view-mobile lazyload " data-src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" alt pinger-seen="true" src="https://static.zaobao.com/s3fs-public/freemium_images/20210209/399-default-mobile_1.jpg" referrerpolicy="no-referrer"></a>
