<p><span></span></p><a name='more'></a><p class="MsoNormal"><br /></p><p></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">2</span>日，本网获悉：仇子明是微博上拥有<span lang="EN-US">240</span>万粉丝的网络大<span lang="EN-US">V </span>，网名是“辣笔小球”。此前因质疑中印边境冲突中方死亡人数，仇子明于<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日被南京警方以寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">1</span>日，南京市人民检察院通告，以刑法修正案中新明确的涉嫌“侵害英雄烈士名誉、荣誉罪”正式批捕。发布当日正是刑法修正案中涉及侮辱英烈条款生效的第一天。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><b><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">仇子明简历：</span></b><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1982</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年生，网名辣笔小球、白衣渡江、球夜行。法学硕士，曾供职于《经济观察报》。他自称“中国最后一个调查记者”，十年前因为凯恩事件一度成为热门新闻人物。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，微博管理员发布消息称：账号<span lang="EN-US">@</span>辣笔小球 发布诋毁英烈的时政有害内容，对该账号予以禁言一年处罚；该账号使用人持有的另一个账号<span lang="EN-US">@</span>球夜行也已一同关。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">月<span lang="EN-US">20 </span>日，南京警方发布通报：仇子明因在微博上发布恶意歪曲事实真相，诋毁贬损<span lang="EN-US">5</span>名卫国戍边英雄官兵的违法言论，仇子明被警方以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪刑事拘留。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">事情原委如下：<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月中印边境爆发严重冲突，时隔<span lang="EN-US">8</span>个月后中共才公布在这场冲突有“<span lang="EN-US">4</span>名军人死亡、<span lang="EN-US">1</span>名团长受重伤”，引发广大网民对官方公布的死亡人数的质疑和评论，其中，拥有<span lang="EN-US">240</span>万粉丝的“辣笔小球”<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日在微博上发表帖文：“最大的官——团长活下来了，看来这个团长个性是‘飞将<span lang="EN-US">+</span>脱兔<span lang="EN-US">+</span>神机<span lang="EN-US">+</span>血路<span lang="EN-US">+</span>强运’。在此基础上，‘外军溃不成军、抱头逃窜’。反正，我们赢了”、 “你仔细品，牺牲的这<span lang="EN-US">4</span>位都是因为‘营救’而立功，连去救人的人都牺牲了，那肯定有没救出来的啊，说明阵亡的不仅仅只有<span lang="EN-US">4</span>人。这也是印度敢于第一时间公布阵亡人数和名单的原因，在印度看来，他们赢了，且代价更小。”<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
