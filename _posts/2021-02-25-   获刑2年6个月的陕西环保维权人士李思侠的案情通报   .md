[获刑2年6个月的陕西环保维权人士李思侠的案情通报](https://wqw2010.blogspot.com/2021/02/26.html)
------

<div>
<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">（维权网信息中心报道）<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日，本网获悉获刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月的陕西环保维权人士李思侠的案情，现在通报如下：</span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;"><b>李思侠：</b>女、陕西省安康市石泉县城关镇双喜村人，中国长庆油田陕西分公司工程师，维权代表，举报人，当地“首起恶势力犯罪团伙”主要受害人，中国在押维权人士。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2008</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾因两家尚未获取采矿权许可证的石料厂（其中一家时至今仍未获得采矿许可证）进驻家乡双喜村，并对周边村民生活造成严重影响，例如石料厂进驻不久，村中公路就被运石卡车破坏，晴天尘土飞扬，雨天满地泥泞，同时大量石渣破坏了耕地和道路，污染了水源和空气等；后因其投诉均毫无结果，无奈之下，遂于<span lang="EN-US">2013</span>年走上了实举报维权之路，并代表村民和二石料厂不断进行强烈交涉与斗争；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年，曾组织村民在新修的公路上设立限宽墩，又在村民两委（即村支部和村委会）换届选举时，为一信任度高的魏某拉选票，导致信任度低的村干部之子孙某选票因票数尚未过半而作废，随使村干部十分不悦；因其之前不断信访和抗争，同年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">20</span>日，陕西省环保督察组被迫到双喜村做现场检查，并表态村民反映的污染问题属实，他们将会于次日专门开会讨论整改方案；<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">22</span>日，其没等来整改结果，却被当地警方以“发帖举报采石场污染”为由传唤、拘留；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2018</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">9</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，其再次被石泉县城郊派出所警方以当地“首起恶势力犯罪团伙”为由抓捕、刑拘；<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2019</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">2</span>月，被石泉县检察院以涉嫌“寻衅滋事罪”予以批捕；<span lang="EN-US">2019</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月，被安康市石泉县法院一审以“寻衅滋事罪”判处有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>个月，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2021</span>年<span lang="EN-US">3</span>月<span lang="EN-US">16</span>日；其不服上诉；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">7</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日。经陕西省安康市中级法院二审裁决，认定该案件事实不清、证据不足，且在案件审理中违反法律规定的诉讼程序，故发回重审。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">目前被羁押于安康市石泉县看守所。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p><p class="MsoNormal"></p><div class="separator" style="clear: both;
