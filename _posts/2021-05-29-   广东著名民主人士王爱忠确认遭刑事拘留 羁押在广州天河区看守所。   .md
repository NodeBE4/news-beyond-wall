<span><a name='more'></a></span><p><br /></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1976</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2014</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">29</span>日，六四<span lang="EN-US">25</span>周年前夕，王爱忠被广州警方于家中采取强制措施，随后以「涉嫌寻衅滋事罪」刑事拘留，关押在广州天河看守所，<span lang="EN-US">2014</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">25</span>日取保候审释放。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span lang="EN-US" style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">2021</span><span style="font-family: 宋体; font-size: 12.0pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">5</span>月<span lang="EN-US">28</span>日中午有人打电话给王爱忠，称其车被刮，诱其下楼，将人带走。随后，九名警察到王爱忠家搜查，向王妻出示了搜查证，搜走<span lang="EN-US">29</span>本书，两台电脑，一部手机。随后，还将王妻传唤到派出所，询问王爱忠的交往及言论情况，并警告勿发声。王爱忠随后以涉嫌寻衅滋事罪遭刑事拘留，关押在广州天河区看守所。王爱忠是近年来一直坚持发声的民主人士，其在<span lang="EN-US">twitter</span>上的言论一直被中共当局密切监控。此次被抓捕，很可能是因言获罪。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family: 宋体;
