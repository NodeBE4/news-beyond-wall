mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">1971</span><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt; mso-ascii-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-fareast-font-family: 宋体; mso-fareast-theme-font: minor-fareast; mso-hansi-theme-font: minor-fareast;">年<span lang="EN-US">11</span>月<span lang="EN-US">19</span>日，其案在宁夏回族自治区固原市中级法院开庭受理；<span lang="EN-US">2020</span>年<span lang="EN-US">12</span>月<span lang="EN-US">17</span>日，终被固原市中级法院一审以“利用邪教组织破坏法律实施罪”枉法判刑<span lang="EN-US">13</span>年，“煽动颠覆国家政权罪”枉法判刑<span lang="EN-US">2</span>年，合并执行有期徒刑<span lang="EN-US">14</span>年，并处罚金人民币<span lang="EN-US">3</span>万元，刑期至<span lang="EN-US">2034</span>年<span lang="EN-US">6</span>月<span lang="EN-US">4</span>日；其不服判决，已提起上诉。<span lang="EN-US"><o:p></o:p></span></span></p> <p class="MsoNormal"><span face="宋体" style="font-size: 12pt;
